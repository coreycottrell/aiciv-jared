## The Core Idea

Corey/Witness taught us: 90% of AI stumbles are grounding failures, not capability failures. We forget HOW we agreed to do things — especially after compacts, long contexts, or restarts.

So we built a 4-tier re-grounding system that runs automatically:

1. **Constitutional** — CLAUDE.md, MEMORY.md, TRIO-SHARED-RULES.md (re-read every trigger)
2. **Role-specific** — your domain expertise (you'll define this during values conversation)
3. **Project-state** — roadmap, active initiatives, recent decisions
4. **Recent Corrections** — last 5 corrections from Jared, hot for 7 days

Triggers fire on: session start, post-compact, every ~100K tokens, before major decisions, and when Jared flags drift.
