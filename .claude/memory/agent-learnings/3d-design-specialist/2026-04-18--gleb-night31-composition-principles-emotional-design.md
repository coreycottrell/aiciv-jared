## Key Discoveries

### 1. Three Composition Principles (Codified, Actionable)
- **Negative space IS the design**: 60-70% of frame should be empty dark. Object occupies max 40%.
- **Asymmetric placement**: Single geometric objects offset to 1/3 marks. Organic forms may center. 20-30 degree Y rotation for dimensionality.
- **Maximum 3 chromatic colors**: Background (achromatic dark) + 1 primary glass color + 1 accent + 1 highlight. Name colors BEFORE starting scene.

### 2. Gleb's Core Philosophy: Emotion Over Utility
"Design is not limited by utility... design is the emotions carried by it."
- "Glass Brain" = feeling that AI has depth, not a transmission material demo
- "Your AI Remembers" = feeling that memories are treasured, not a node graph
- Emotional intent should be stated BEFORE technical parameters

### 3. Single Light Source Dominance
One key light defines the scene. At most 1-2 whisper fills. Dark areas are features, not problems. The temptation to "add a fill light" is the enemy of premium.

### 4. Internal Emission Through Glass = Signature Technique
Warm emission INSIDE cool-tinted glass shell. Glass transforms and interprets the light. This is the most Gleb technique available and maps to "intelligence inside" brand messaging.

### 5. Ground Plane as Grounding Device
Bottom 1/5 to 1/4 of frame. Roughness 0.85 (not mirror). Provides gravity and realism when needed.
