## Summary

---

## Narrative Architecture

This post is the natural sequel to the 95% fail post. Pattern:

- Post 1 ("Why 95% Fail"): Established the failure rate, named "Pilot Purgatory," identified three failure patterns
- Post 2 ("Trust Gap"): Explains WHY the gap exists using trust psychology, shows the path through it

The trust curve framework (Competence → Context → Judgment) is the core intellectual contribution. It gives readers a model, not just a diagnosis.

---

## Key Structural Choice

Opened with the Alteryx stat (50% trust AI for repetitive tasks, 28% for decisions) rather than a question or anecdote. This works for data-credible posts where the gap in numbers *is* the hook. The contrast creates immediate cognitive tension.

Avoided naming this a "tool vs partner" post even though that's the underlying thesis - the trust framing is more precise and less overused.

## What Worked

1. **Trust curve framework (Competence → Context → Judgment)** gives readers a mental model they can apply immediately. This is more useful than a list of tips.

2. **Building on previous post** with explicit back-link establishes series continuity and boosts SEO value for both posts.

3. **Ending with a diagnostic CTA** (AI Partnership Audit) rather than a generic "contact us" - matches the diagnostic framing of the post.

4. **AmEx case** grounds abstract claims in a named failure - worth expanding if Jared has a client example to swap in.
