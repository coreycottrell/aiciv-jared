## Patterns To Reuse

**Self-referential proof pattern**: When the post topic IS the process used to make it (overnight AI produced an overnight AI post), the lede becomes the most compelling proof of the argument. Use this whenever possible — it collapses the gap between claim and evidence.

**Honest FAQ pattern**: The "is this putting people out of work?" question should be answered directly, not deflected. Readers respect the direct answer. Deflection reads as evasion and undermines trust.

**Sleep Test mental model**: "What happens when you close your laptop tonight?" is a high-engagement framing — it asks the reader to audit their own behavior. Works well as a section header or standalone post.
