## Patterns Applied

**CEO-vs-employee lens as structural frame**: Not just mentioned but built into a dedicated section with concrete examples (credit card statement vs manual export friction). Stronger than weaving it in.

**Honest scoping**: Post explicitly says large platforms are NOT going away. This builds credibility and avoids overclaiming, which previous posts have shown is valued by ICP.

**The "if you built from scratch" test**: Strong closer question. Invites the CEO to reframe their stack as choices rather than givens. High internal shareability.

**Graveyard metaphor**: "Integrations built by people who are gone, maintained by people who didn't build them" — this lands because it's specific and recognizable, not hyperbolic.
