## What Was Done
Completed full marketing analysis of Toast, Inc. (NYSE: TOST) covering:
- Market position (~127,000+ US restaurant locations, ~$1.3B revenue)
- 4-tier pricing structure ($0 Starter to Custom Enterprise)
- Marketing channel strategy (content-heavy inbound + large direct sales team)
- Competitive landscape (Square, Clover, Lightspeed, SpotOn, TouchBistro)
- Toast pain points and restaurant client pain points
- Three distinct PMG opportunity paths (Client Agency, Integration Product, Corporate Vendor)

## Key Findings

### Toast's Biggest Marketing Vulnerabilities
1. Content educates but doesn't commercially convert on specific features
2. Payroll/HR and Marketing Suite modules are dramatically undermarketed
3. Multi-location/enterprise marketing content is thin for their stated upmarket ambition
4. Guest data captured through POS is underutilized for restaurant client marketing

### Best PMG Entry Point
Option A (Toast Restaurant Client Agency) is the most immediately actionable:
- Serve Toast's 127,000+ restaurant operators directly
- Lead with email marketing using their existing Toast guest data
- Pair with social media management + local SEO
- Target pricing: $500-$2,500/month per restaurant client

### Integration Opportunity
Toast API is publicly documented and allows third-party access to order history, loyalty data, and guest spend. An automated marketing layer that connects Toast data to campaign execution would be highly differentiated.
