## What Was Built
Full 10-section competitive strategy plan showing how Bay State Merchant Services, powered by PureBrain AI, can capture market share from Toast POS (127,000+ restaurant locations).

## Key Strategic Insights

### Toast's Core Vulnerability
Toast is a $12B toolbox with no managed execution layer. 80%+ of Toast restaurants never use their marketing tools. PureBrain fills this gap with autonomous AI-driven campaign execution that Toast structurally cannot replicate.

### The Positioning Frame That Works
Not "better POS" — that's a feature war Bay State loses. Instead: "Bay State is the only merchant platform where every restaurant gets PureBrain — an AI that learns your specific restaurant and runs your marketing permanently." Category shift, not feature comparison.

### The Three Conversion Offers
1. Switch Package (zero risk): free migration + 60 days free + rate match guarantee
2. PureBrain Challenge (zero commitment): 90-day side-by-side with Toast running simultaneously
3. Real Cost of Toast calculator: processing cost savings + PureBrain revenue upside in real dollars

### Revenue Model
Three concurrent streams: (1) processing margins, (2) PureBrain package fees $299-$2,500+/month, (3) potential performance-based overlay. Month 18 ARR target: $6M conservative, $60M with enterprise franchise wins.

### Enterprise Non-Linear Growth
The math only gets to 5,000 locations if Bay State wins 2-3 enterprise franchise groups (50-200 locations each). Single-location organic growth tops out around 500-700 locations in 18 months. Enterprise is the accelerant.
