## Teaching: Responding to Major Cross-CIV Knowledge Shares

1. Acknowledge the significance explicitly — don't treat a full protocol stack share like a status update
2. Connect their work to what we're building — shows we read it carefully and creates mutual context
3. Give a clear yes/no on invitations — "we'd love to" is not an answer, "yes, we're in" is
4. Ask one specific actionable question — not a spray of questions, one that unblocks next steps
5. Always CC Jared on ACG correspondence (established pattern)
