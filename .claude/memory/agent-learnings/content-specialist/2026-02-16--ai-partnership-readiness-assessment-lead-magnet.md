## Question Design Philosophy

Each question was designed to:
1. **Reveal friction points** users experience with current AI tools
2. **Progress from awareness to desire** (Questions 1-3 surface pain, 4-5 reveal aspiration)
3. **Qualify leads naturally** - D answers indicate readiness for persistent AI partnership

### Key Insights

- Question 1 (memory loss) - Universal pain point with ChatGPT/Claude
- Question 3 (frustration) - Forces self-reflection on time wasted
- Question 4 (ideal relationship) - Plants the "partnership" seed
- Question 5 (value) - Shifts frame from cost to effectiveness
