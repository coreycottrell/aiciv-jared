## Reusable Patterns

### Opening Hook Formula (works for B2B executive content)
"Your [thing] didn't [fail in expected way]. [Thing that actually did fail]."
This validates the reader's experience before delivering the reframe.

### Diagnostic Questions as CTA Precursor
5 self-assessment questions at the end of a problem-diagnosis piece create psychological investment before a CTA. The reader who answers uncomfortably is primed to seek help.

### The Counterintuitive Stat as Section Centerpiece
Lead with the surprising finding (high-anxiety employees use AI MORE), explain why it's counterintuitive, then explain what it means. This structure creates the longest reader engagement within any section.
