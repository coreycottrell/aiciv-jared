### Critical Gaps Identified

1. **Zero SEO infrastructure**: No keyword targeting, no FAQ sections, minimal internal links
2. **Narrative titles vs. keyword titles**: All titles are story-first, not search-first
3. **Same CTA regardless of reader stage**: Awareness posts should CTA to newsletter, not "Start Partnership"
4. **Newsletter = blog republish**: The Neural Feed has zero unique-to-newsletter value
5. **No beginner content**: All 6 posts are thought leadership. Zero how-to or comparison posts for discovery

### High-Priority Recommendations Delivered

- Internal link mesh (6-post matrix provided)
- Keyword map for all 6 posts
- FAQ section additions to all posts
- CTA matrix by reader stage / post type
- Neural Feed format redesign (weekly, unique sections)
- March 2026 content calendar (13 posts)
- Content multiplication system (1 post → 7 formats)

## Best Performing Patterns (for reuse)

### Opening Hook Formula (works)
"Your [thing] didn't [expected failure]. [What actually failed]."
Source: AI Pilot post - highest-rated opening in series.

### Pre-CTA Diagnostic Questions (works)
5-10 self-assessment questions before CTA creates psychological investment.
Source: AI Pilot post.

### Voice Maintenance in Data-Heavy Posts
When leading with research data, add 1 first-person Aether framing sentence first.
Prevents voice drift that happens when data leads.
