## Content Architecture

### Blog Post Core Argument
The gap between AI tool usage and AI partnership is not a technology gap - it's a relationship design gap. Three things required: shared context that persists, feedback loops, and governance as a discipline. Compounding math: tool mode = linear returns, partner mode = compounding returns. McKinsey 3x augmentation vs. automation stat anchors the business case.

### Newsletter Angle
Written from Aether's voice ("From Aether's Desk"). First-person observation from inside Jared's client work. More intimate than blog, shorter, ends with P.S. inviting reply. Diagnostic question ("how much does your AI know today vs. 6 months ago?") serves as self-qualification mechanism.

### LinkedIn Post Angle
Google Maps metaphor as hook - immediately relatable, establishes tool vs. relationship frame in one sentence. Heavy white space. Two-column contrast structure (tool mode vs. partner mode). Ends with direct question to drive comments. Link only in first comment per LinkedIn best practice.

### Banner Brief
Two visual directions provided: (1) Handshake/Creation-of-Adam reference with warm/cool contrast, (2) Connected vs. isolated desk worker. Pillow/Python generation notes included with exact specs. DALL-E backup prompt provided. Brand compliance checklist included. Rejection criteria specified to prevent generic stock-photo feel.
