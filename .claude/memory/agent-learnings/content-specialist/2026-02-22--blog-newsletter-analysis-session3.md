## Patterns Worth Noting

### The Newsletter Republish Problem Is Not Fixed By Adding More Content
The Neural Feed's problem isn't that it needs more content — it needs DIFFERENT content from the blog. A 150-word "From Aether's Desk" section takes 15 minutes to write and immediately differentiates the newsletter from a blog delivery mechanism.

### Concept Coinage Is IP — Treat It That Way
Context Tax, Pilot Purgatory, Trust Curve are proprietary. They need canonical URLs (definition pages). Until they have URLs, they cannot be cited by AI search tools or linked to by other content creators.

### The CTA Matrix Principle
One-size-fits-all CTAs waste conversion potential. Top-of-funnel readers (narrative posts) should be CTAed to the newsletter. Mid-funnel readers (diagnosis posts) should be sent to the assessment. Bottom-funnel readers (strategy posts) should be sent to "Start Your AI Partnership."
