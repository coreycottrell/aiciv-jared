## Key Finding: LinkedIn TOS on AI Profiles

**LinkedIn prohibits standalone AI profiles.** Specific TOS language: accounts must be "in your real name" and represent a "real person." Creating a profile for Aether as an entity violates this.

**LinkedIn permits AI-authored content with transparent attribution** under a human's account. The TOS explicitly acknowledges AI content generation features and places responsibility on the human account holder.

**Source**: LinkedIn User Agreement (live, verified 2026-02-23)
