## Content Architecture Decisions

### Office Hours Playbook
- Used "professor's office hours" metaphor as anchor for the format philosophy
- Included specific tech failsafe protocol (phone 4G backup + plain Zoom fallback)
- Added attendance targets with "replay views often 3-5x live attendance" note for expectation calibration
- Conversion funnel framed as: demo does the selling, follow-up emails do the closing

### Registration Page
- Structured around minimal friction registration (first name, email, one optional question)
- The optional question ("biggest AI challenge") serves dual purpose: personalization + pre-qualification
- "Who this is for" section written as connected paragraphs, not bullet lists - more conversational, more trust-building
- Dedicated "Aether moment" section to differentiate from generic webinars
- FAQ handles the "is this just a sales pitch?" objection directly

### Email Sequences
- 7 emails total: confirmation, 24hr reminder, 1hr reminder, post-session (2 versions: attended vs no-show), day 3 follow-up, day 7 follow-up
- Plus replay notification to non-registrants (Neural Feed list)
- Day 7 email deliberately has NO CTA button - purely a reply-harvesting email
- Email 3 (day 7) rationale: "Replies from prospects are higher-value than any other engagement signal"
- Brevo automation setup instructions included for full-stack-developer

### Topic Calendar
- Sessions 1-3 designed as a curriculum arc: mindset (Director/User) → memory (stop starting over) → system (context layer)
- Each session's attendee target builds on the previous (50-150 → 75-200 → 100-300)
- 9 future topics in the bank with strategic rationale for each
- Topic selection principles included so Jared can evaluate future topics independently

### Promotion Templates
- Aether's Bluesky posts deliberately have NO registration link on Day -14 - builds trust before asking
- Day -7 Aether Bluesky is a 5-post thread (genuine deep dive, not promotional)
- Day -3 email targets "opened but didn't register" segment specifically
- Day +1 LinkedIn post is written generically with note to customize from actual session content
- Customization guide included so future sessions can adapt templates in <30 minutes

### Repurposing Playbook
- 8+ assets per session, 6 hours of production time = high leverage ratio
- Aether's "timestamp document" protocol formalized - Aether creates this after every session
- Clip priority order: Demo Moment > Reframe Moment > Best Q&A Answer > Aether Synthesis > Honest Moment
- "Freshness rule" included: never publish repurposed content after Day +14
- Optional podcast section included for future consideration
- Newsletter branded as "From Aether's Desk" + "From Jared" - consistent with prior neural feed pattern
