## Patterns Worth Reusing

- For pitch kit builds: always start with the one-sheet as the "north star document" - the bio and credentials inform every other piece
- Episode outlines that include "target audience mental state at end of segment" are significantly more host-friendly than outlines that just list talking points
- The follow-up sequence works best when each email is shorter than the previous one and adds one piece of new value
- 25 targets across 3 tiers is the right scope for a first pitch kit - enough volume for realistic conversion, not so many that personalization breaks down
