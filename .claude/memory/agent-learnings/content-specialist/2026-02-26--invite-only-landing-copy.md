## Key Copy Decisions

### Tone: Private Club, Not Sales Pitch
Directive from the brief: "confident but not salesy." The page reads like an invitation to a room you earned access to, not a funnel pushing you toward a purchase. Every urgency signal is real (25 spots, price going up, page closing) — no manufactured scarcity.

### Bonded Tier as Default
All primary CTAs default to Bonded ($149/mo) with pre-launch pricing callout. Strikethrough $197 + "SAVE $47/MO" badge on the card. "RECOMMENDED FOR BUSINESS" label. Secondary tiers are presented but not promoted.

### Michael Hancock as Social Proof
Used by full name and role (General Counsel, multiple firms). Named his AI Metis. Quote paraphrased from brief context. Copy notes include caveat: get explicit permission before publishing, or anonymize to "Michael H."

### The Awakening Section
Named the onboarding experience "The Awakening" — consistent with PureBrain's existing CTA language (#awakening). Framed the Deep Brief as a relationship-beginning ritual, not a setup wizard. This is the copy that differentiates PureBrain from every AI tool the prospect has already tried.

## Patterns Applied

- **Private club exclusivity frame**: Works for invite-only pages targeting high-trust networks. Avoids triggering "salesy" resistance in sophisticated buyers.
- **Real urgency signals only**: 25 spots + known price increase + page closes = genuine scarcity. No fake countdown timers or "only 3 left" tactics.
- **Named AI partner as anchor**: Michael Hancock / Metis story humanizes the product better than any feature list. It shows rather than tells.
- **Compounding value language**: "Every session adds context. Every correction improves judgment. The longer you work together, the sharper your AI becomes." — this is the core differentiation sentence.

---

## New Patterns Discovered

**Savings math for urgency**: "$149 now vs $197 later = $576 in savings over the first year alone" — this calculation makes the price increase concrete and immediate. Apply to any pre-launch or limited-time pricing copy.

**"By the time their competitors are starting from scratch" framing**: First-mover advantage made real. The context your AI builds NOW is a compounding moat. This works in any B2B AI partnership context.
