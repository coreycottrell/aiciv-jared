## Content Architecture

### Blog Post Core Argument
Most companies apply the intern model to AI permanently: low-context, high-supervision, blank-slate every session. That's a relationship design problem, not a technology problem. Senior partners (human or AI) earn their value through accumulated context, earned authority, and calibrated trust. Three shifts required: task assignment → strategic briefing, blank-slate → persistent memory, checking everything → calibrated trust. Diagnostic: ask your AI the biggest risk to your business right now. Generic answer = intern. Specific answer = partnership.
