## Key Strategic Patterns Documented

### Three-Layer Funnel
1. Awareness: Blog + social amplification
2. Credibility: Full blog read + newsletter
3. Conversion: Audit (enterprise) + Investor Intelligence Page (investor)

### Dual-Audience Stealth Positioning
The campaign runs one public narrative (enterprise authority) with a parallel investor track embedded via the Investor Intelligence Page. No public funding references. Investor context surfaces only to warm leads who self-select through deep engagement.

### Stat Card Drip Architecture
5 stat cards deployed on Days 2–5 maintain campaign presence across the week without repetition. Each is self-contained but traces back to the blog. The "89% haven't started" card is expected to be the highest-performer (creates social proof in reverse).

### Engagement Protocol
- Reply every comment within 5 min with a NEW data point (not in original post)
- Morning warm-up: 5 VC/CIO comments before post goes live
- Daily search engagement: "AI agents" → comment on 3 posts
- Personalized DMs starting Day 4 to top engagers, segmented by profile type
