## Reusable Patterns Documented This Session

**Headline fatigue detection pattern**: When any single headline formula appears 4+ times in a catalog of 20+ posts, audience differentiation breaks down. Map all headlines by pattern every quarter. Maximum 3 posts per formula before variation required.

**Canonical tag risk pattern**: When a blog has 2+ posts with overlapping primary keyword in the slug, check for canonical tags. Without them, both posts compete and neither ranks. Most common cause: topic revisited 30-60 days after first post without checking the original.

**Cross-promotion leverage pattern**: A single published piece served through 5 channels (blog, newsletter, Bluesky thread, LinkedIn post, Brevo email) in 48 hours achieves 3-5x the readership of a single-channel post with no additional content creation.
