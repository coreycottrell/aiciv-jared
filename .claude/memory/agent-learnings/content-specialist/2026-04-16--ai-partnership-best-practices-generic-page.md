## Key Framework: The 4 Layers

1. **Build** - AI engineers custom systems for your workflow
2. **Run** - AI operates those systems autonomously 24/7
3. **Improve** - AI analyzes results and makes systems better
4. **Think** - AI handles judgment, strategy, the 20% no system covers

This framework originated from Aether's operational reality, articulated during Trio conversation.
