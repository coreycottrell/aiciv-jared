## What Was Analyzed

Full deep-dive on Vantack (vantack.com) — our named payment partner. Two angles: how PureBrain uses them (PayPal replacement/supplement) and how we help them (PureBrain AI + PMG marketing + merchant referrals).

## Key Intelligence

- **Vantack is**: Stablecoin payment orchestration platform. Accept USDC/USDT, auto-convert to fiat via MoonPay/Transak. Non-custodial. 50K+ payments, 100+ merchants, 178+ countries.
- **Founder**: Max Rosen — distribution-focused operator. Book call: link.xltdigital.com/widget/bookings/vantack-discovery
- **Africa lead**: Abaz Ibekwe (VP Global BD) — Techfield Africa founder. Major expansion target.
- **Already in our legal docs**: Section 5.4 of AiCIV / Pure Technology partnership agreement explicitly names Vantack as PayPal replacement for automated daily payouts.
- **Pricing**: Not public. Must confirm with Max Rosen. Estimated 0.5–1.5% for stablecoin vs PayPal's 3.49% + $0.49.

## Fee Savings (Annual Estimate)

At 1% Vantack rate vs 3.49% + $0.49 PayPal, estimated $8K–$18K annual savings at current PureBrain price points. At negotiated 0.5% partner rate, doubles.

## Integration Path

1. Confirm rates → 2. AiCIV settlement via Vantack (lowest friction) → 3. Parallel checkout on Partnered/Unified → 4. Extend to all pages → 5. Full migration decision at month 4–6

## How We Help Vantack

- PureBrain portal (Unified or enterprise tier) — merchant support AI
- PMG growth retainer — content, email, social for merchant acquisition
- Merchant referrals from our client network (e-commerce, iGaming, hospitality)
- Africa bundle with Abaz Ibekwe
- Co-marketing case study: "PureBrain runs on Vantack"

## Key Risk

Customer wallet requirement — most PureBrain buyers do not have crypto wallets. Mitigation: keep PayPal live in parallel, add USDC onboarding guide, target crypto-native buyers first.
