## Research Summary

### LinkedIn Algorithm Insights (Feb 2026)

1. **Depth Score** - New metric (Jan 2026) accumulates over 24-48 hours. Rewards:
   - Deep comments (not just "great post!")
   - Save/bookmark actions
   - Time spent on post
   - Return visits

2. **AI Content Detection** - Audiences have developed "AI blindness" for generic posts. Winning formula: AI for packaging, human soul for content.

3. **CEO Content Bonus** - CEO content gets 4x engagement vs average posts. Platform favors individual authority over company pages.

4. **Optimal Newsletter Length** - Sweet spot is 800-1,500 words. Long-form (1,500-2,000) gets 25-35% better reach than quick updates.

5. **Personal Narrative Premium** - Behind-the-scenes and "day in my life" content outperforms advice content. Shows, don't tell.

## Content Pattern Applied

**"Day in the Life" Framework**:
- Hourly breakdown creates anticipation/scannability
- Before/after time comparisons trigger dopamine
- Specific task names make it believable
- Emotional beats (crisis, reflection) add depth
- Controversy section drives comments

This pattern works because it's:
- Specific (can't be faked)
- Relatable (everyone has days)
- Shareable (the table/summary)
- Debatable ("outsourced thinking")
