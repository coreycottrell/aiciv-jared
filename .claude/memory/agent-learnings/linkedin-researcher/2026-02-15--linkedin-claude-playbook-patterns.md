# LinkedIn Claude Playbook Content Patterns

**Date**: 2026-02-15
**Type**: synthesis
**Topic**: High-engagement LinkedIn content patterns from Claude playbook posts

---

## Key Finding

Three posts analyzed with combined 2,480+ likes, 609+ comments. Formula that works:

1. **Ego hook** - "You're doing it wrong" framing
2. **Numbered lists** - 5-10 items, scannable
3. **Time transformation** - "3 hours to 3 minutes"
4. **Completeness claim** - "Everything you need"
5. **Engagement question** - drives comments

---

## Content Structures That Perform

### High Performer (1,585 likes)
- Resource compilation (17 items)
- Organized by category (bold headers, arrow bullets)
- Repost prompt
- "Which will you build first?" question

### Mid Performer (801 likes)
- Problem/solution format
- 6 numbered tactics
- Time claim ("60 seconds")
- Transformation language ("mid to runs my business")

### Lower Performer (94 likes)
- PDF attachment (hurts reach)
- Niche focus (single feature)
- No engagement question
