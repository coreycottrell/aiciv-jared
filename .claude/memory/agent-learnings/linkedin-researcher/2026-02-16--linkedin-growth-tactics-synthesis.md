## Top 5 Actionable Patterns

### 1. Blank Connection Requests Win
Don't add a message. Visit profile first, like a post, send 9-11 AM their timezone. Profile does the conversion work.

### 2. Warm Up Before DM
Comment on target's content 3-5 times over 2 weeks BEFORE sending any DM. Creates familiarity. "Relevance isn't created in the first message."

### 3. Comment-to-DM Lead Gen
"Comment [WORD] for [RESOURCE]" is highest-converting lead gen. Low friction, starts conversation, builds list.

### 4. Profile = Storefront
Banner (Who/What/Proof/CTA), Headline (transformation not title), About (4 blocks: Pain/Solution/Proof/CTA), Featured (case studies + lead magnet).

### 5. 2x Doubling Strategy
Track which posts get 2x average engagement. Double down on that pattern. Stop guessing, follow data.
