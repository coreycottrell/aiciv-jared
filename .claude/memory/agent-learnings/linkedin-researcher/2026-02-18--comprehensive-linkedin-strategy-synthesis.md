## Key Research Findings (2026 Algorithm State)

### Format Performance (February 2026)
- Document/PDF carousels: 6.60% engagement (peak 40.5% in some tests)
- Carousels outperform video by 278%, text by 596%
- Text-only posts: best reach-per-follower ratio
- Single image posts: 30% LESS reach than text-only
- External links in post body: -60% reach penalty (always move to comments)
- Optimal text post length: 1,300-1,900 characters

### Algorithm Mechanics
- "Depth Score" (new Jan 2026): accumulates over 24-48 hours
  - Rewards deep comments, saves, dwell time, return visits
- First 90 minutes determines majority of reach
- Algorithm shows post to 2-5% of network first, then scales on engagement
- AI-generated comments get 5x fewer replies - genuine comments rewarded
- Personal profiles get 65% of feed allocation; company pages get ~5%

### Posting Strategy
- Best days: Tuesday-Thursday
- Best times: 8-9 AM, 10-11 AM, 12-1 PM EST
- Target cadence: 4-5 high-quality posts/week (not daily)
- Daily posting without quality = algorithmic suppression
