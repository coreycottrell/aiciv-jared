## Key New Findings (Not in v1 or v2)

### Comment-to-DM Automation (2026 State)
- Tools: Expandi, PhantomBuster, MeetAlfred all have AI intent detection (not just keyword matching)
- Expandi added voice-activated DM triggers Q1 2026
- PhantomBuster 2026 update includes GenAI for DM copy
- Taplio has comment virality detector that triggers campaigns when posts take off
- Cost: $50-100/month, zero ad spend required for initial lead capture
- 20-40% comment-to-DM conversion rate on posts with 50+ comments

### Thought Leader Ads Specific ROI Data
- CPC: median $2.29 (vs $22+ for standard company ads)
- CTR: 1.7x higher than standard company page ads, 2.68% median
- One client: $120K deal from $2,035 TLA spend (confirmed case study)
- Best use: boost Tuesday carousel posts after organic performance signals

### LinkedIn Live = Highest Format ROI
- 29.6% engagement rate (highest of ANY format)
- Native video (60-90 seconds) = 5x more interaction than text posts
- 200% higher completion rate for videos under 30 seconds
- LinkedIn has TikTok-style vertical video feed (9:16 ratio gets priority in 2026)
- Jared has NOT been using video at all - this is the biggest format gap

### SSI Score Impact
- Professionals with HIGH SSI: 45% more opportunities, 51% more likely to hit quota
- LinkedIn launched AI SSI inside Sales Navigator (contextual scoring, not mechanical)
- Checking SSI weekly and targeting above 75 unlocks better organic distribution
- Check at: linkedin.com/sales/ssi

### Pre-Warm Prospect Sequence Data
- Blank connection requests: 68% acceptance rate vs. 32% with a message
- Comment first (2-3 times) -> then connect -> then DM after 3-7 days
- 2x better DM response rate vs cold outreach

### Newsletter Guest Swap
- Each guest post on another creator's newsletter: 50-200 new subscribers
- Target: creators with 3,000-10,000 subscribers in adjacent niches
- 10-20 creator partnerships = 500-2,000 new subscribers per quarter

### LinkedIn Events as Lead Capture
- Every event registrant = declared intent lead
- Permission to DM every registrant after the event
- 30-minute LinkedIn Live generates 50-200 live viewers at 2K-10K follower range
