## Key Insights Captured

### Algorithm Facts (2026)

- Document/PDF carousels: 6.60% engagement rate — highest of all formats
- Comments carry 15x more weight than likes
- External links in post body = 60% reach penalty — put in first comment
- AI-generated generic content is penalized by algorithm (detected via phrasing patterns)
- First 60-90 minutes after posting determines 70% of total reach
- Engagement pods are dead — LinkedIn AI detects reciprocal patterns and shadowbans

### Optimal Posting

- 3-4 posts per week is the sweet spot for most creators
- Best days: Tuesday (10-11 AM), Wednesday (10 AM, 12 PM), Thursday (10 AM, 1 PM)
- Minimum 12 hours between posts
- Never post on weekends (50% lower engagement)

### What Works for CEO/Founder Personal Brand

- Personal stories > polished corporate content (3x more engagement)
- "Build in public" + honest failures > success stories
- Companies with active CEO social media report 40% better customer acquisition
- Jared's unique angle: runs company WITH AI collective, not just ABOUT AI

### Company Page vs Personal Page

- Personal brands outperform corporate accounts 10-to-1
- Employee-shared content = 8x amplification
- Company page is support vehicle, not primary channel
- Thought Leader Ads (amplifying personal posts via company ad account) = best LinkedIn ad format 2026

### Hook Formulas That Work

1. Counterintuitive opener: "Everyone believes X. Here's why they're wrong."
2. Specific number + surprise: "[Precise stat] nobody is talking about."
3. Personal story lead: Specific moment → what happened → unexpected lesson
4. Honest admission: "I used to think X. Then Y changed my mind."
5. Direct challenge: Provocative claim + evidence + self-reflection question

### Content Mix Formula

- 60% educational carousels/PDFs
- 30% thought leadership text posts
- 10% engagement content (polls, questions)
