## Key New Findings (Not in v1-v8)

### Saves Are Now THE Dominant Signal
- AuthoredUp analysis of 3M+ posts: saves drive 5x more reach than likes, 2x more than comments
- Posts saved 24-72 hours after publishing get 4-6x algorithm boost (360Brew sees late saves as lasting value)
- Post longevity now 2-3 weeks average (up from days in 2025)
- Source: getdewey.co/blog/linkedin-2026-algorithm-saves/

### Depth Score Replaced Velocity Metrics
- Composite metric: dwell time + "see more" clicks + comment reading depth + saves + DM shares
- 3-8 hour evaluation window replaces 60-minute "golden hour"
- 45 seconds reading > instant like (algorithm penalizes quick-like-and-scroll pattern)
- Source: blog.linkboost.co/linkedin-algorithm-changes-2026/

### Polls Are Dead
- Polls dropped to 0.07% engagement (was 4.4% — a catastrophic decline)
- Algorithm now classifies most polls as engagement bait
- Source: blog.linkboost.co/linkedin-algorithm-changes-2026/

### Single Images Underperform Text by 30%
- This is a REVERSAL from 2024-2025 when images outperformed text
- Low-effort image posts penalized — images must be information-dense
- Source: connectsafely.ai/articles/linkedin-post-examples-high-engagement-2026

### Carousel Dominance Confirmed with New Data
- 24.42% average engagement rate (CarouselMaker data)
- 3.7x higher than text posts
- 15-20 seconds dwell time vs 8-10 for simple formats
- Source: carouselmaker.co and meet-lea.com

### AMPS Commenting Framework Proposed
- Upgraded from Pattern + Missing Layer + Smart Question
- A (Acknowledge) + M (Missing Layer) + P (Personal Experience) + S (Smart Question)
- 30-80 word sweet spot identified for comment length
- 15-20 comments/day = 50-100 profile visits
- Source: blog.linkmate.io/linkedin-comment-strategy-2026/

### Newsletter ROI Data
- 30-40% open rate vs 15-25% email newsletters
- Triple notification bypasses algorithm entirely
- $500 ad spend = 300-500 new subscribers
- 47% YoY subscriber growth across platform
- Source: moburst.com LinkedIn newsletter strategies

### Profile-Content Match Now Algorithmic
- 360Brew checks profile expertise against post topics
- Loose profile = algorithm can't categorize you = reduced distribution
- Headlines carry 45% of search discoverability weight
- Source: growleads.io profile optimization guide
