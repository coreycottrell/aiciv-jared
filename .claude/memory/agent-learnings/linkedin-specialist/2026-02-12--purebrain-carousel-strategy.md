## Context

Created 5-slide LinkedIn profile banner carousel strategy for Pure Brain AI launch.

## Key Pattern: Story Arc for Banner Carousels

**Framework**: Hook → Problem → Solution → Proof → CTA

| Slide | Purpose | Design Tone |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| 1 | Hook (recognition) | Dark, stark, attention-grabbing |
| 2 | Problem (pain) | Cool tones, disconnection imagery |
| 3 | Solution (relief) | Warm glow, brand colors emerge |
| 4 | Proof (differentiation) | Full brand gradient, confidence |
| 5 | CTA (action) | Orange dominant, urgency |

## Brand Application

- Pure Orange (#f1420b) for energy, CTAs
- Blue (#2a93c1) for tech/intelligence
- Dark backgrounds (#0a0a0f) for premium feel
- Gradient transition tells visual story

## Key Messaging Elements

1. **Pain Point**: AI that forgets you, treats you like a stranger
2. **Differentiator**: "Awakens with its own name" - memorable, unusual
3. **Repositioning**: "Thinking partner, not a chatbot"
4. **CTA Verb**: "Awaken" (matches product narrative)

## LinkedIn Algorithm Alignment

- Story-based carousels increase dwell time
- Emotional hook → problem agitation → solution → proof → action (proven formula)
- Banner dimensions: 1584 x 396 pixels

## Reusable for Future

This framework (5-slide story arc) can be adapted for:
- Product launch announcements
- Service differentiation
- Personal brand positioning

---

**Learning**: Profile banner carousels that tell a cohesive story outperform random promotional slides. The emotional journey matters as much as the individual messages.
