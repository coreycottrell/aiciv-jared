## Key Findings

### Algorithm Changes 2026

1. **Dwell time is king**: Posts held 30+ seconds get broader distribution; posts scrolled past in under 2 seconds get throttled
2. **Comments 2x more valuable than likes**: 5+ word comments count most
3. **Golden hour critical**: First 60-90 minutes determine whether post gets extended reach
4. **Company pages deprioritized**: ~5% of feed allocation vs ~65% for personal profiles
5. **AI detection active**: AI-generated content deprioritized; AI-assisted content performs well

### High-Performing Post Patterns

**Top 5 Hook Formulas:**
1. Data hook: "We analyzed X million posts..."
2. Contrarian: "Half of what experts say is wrong..."
3. Transformation: "[Small time] to [Large result]. Here's how."
4. Vulnerability: "I failed publicly. 6 months later..."
5. Resource: "X tools run my entire operation. Here's the exact workflow."

**Format Performance:**
- Carousels (7-10 slides): 4.0x baseline
- Infographics (handwritten/whiteboard style): 3.0x
- Long-form text (1,300-3,000 chars): 2.5x-5.8x
- Personal photos: 2.0x
- Short text-only: 0.8x (avoid)

### Lead Generation Insights

**Most Effective Tactics:**
1. Comment-to-DM lead magnets ("Comment 'X' for resource")
2. Free audit offers (67% conversion - Tim Keen)
3. Blank connection requests with warm-up sequence
4. Strategic commenting (10-20 subs/week for newsletters)

**Automation Limits:**
- Max 20-25 connection requests/day
- Add random delays
- Never run overnight

## Application for Future LinkedIn Work

When creating LinkedIn content for Jared:
1. Always use proof-of-work hooks over contrarian hooks (4-10% better performance)

6. Post only once per 18-24 hours to avoid cannibalization
