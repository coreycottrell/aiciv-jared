## Key Algorithm Insights (February 2026)

**The Brew 360 shift**: LinkedIn now uses AI-powered "Depth and Authority" distribution. Profile history, engagement quality, and comment behavior are all factored — not just post performance. This is a structural change from broadcast to earned authority.

**Format hierarchy (current)**:
1. Document posts (PDF carousels): 6.60% avg engagement — best format
2. Native video 30-90s: 5.60% — trust building, demos
3. Strategic text posts: 2-4% — strong hooks, long-form, no external links
4. Images: 1-2% — personal photos beat stock
5. Company page posts: 0.3-0.8% — personal profiles beat company pages by 561%

**Critical timing**: Only 5% of underperforming posts in the first 60 minutes ever recover. Post → engage with comments immediately.

**What died**: Engagement pods (suppressed), daily posting (20% penalty for two posts in 24h), generic contrarian hooks, hashtags, short posts, corporate language.

**What lives**: Proof-of-work hooks, 1,300-3,000 character posts, 3+ helpful links (236% higher reach), saves/reposts as primary algorithm signals, Saturday/Sunday as top-performing days.
