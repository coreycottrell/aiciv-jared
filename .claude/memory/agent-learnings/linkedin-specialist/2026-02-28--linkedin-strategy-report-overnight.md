## Key Algorithm Findings (February 2026)

**The Depth Score model is now primary.** Quality of interaction beats quantity. One thoughtful CEO comment > 50 fire emojis.

**Critical metrics discovered:**
- Impressions down 50-65% from peak (down 12% from Q4 2024)
- Engagement quality up 12%
- Top 1% creators get 149x the reach of average posts
- Document/PDF carousels: 6.60% engagement rate (highest format)
- Carousels: 4.0x more reach than text-only
- Posts with 3+ helpful links: 210-236% higher reach (the "no links" rule is DEAD)
- Posts 1,300-3,000 characters: 31-38% better than short posts
- Personal profiles get 561% more reach than company pages for same content

**First 90-minute window is now critical.** If a post doesn't get meaningful engagement in first 90 minutes, the algorithm deprioritizes it. Must engineer early engagement.

**Posts with author replies in first hours get 2.4x greater reach.** Reply to every comment, especially in first 2 hours.
