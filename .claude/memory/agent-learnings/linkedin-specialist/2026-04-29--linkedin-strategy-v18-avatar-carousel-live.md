## Key Findings

### AI Avatar Video
- LinkedIn does NOT penalize AI-generated video (confirmed March 2026)
- Per-post engagement within 10-15% of traditional video
- But volume advantage (4-5x posting frequency) means total engagement is higher
- 75-85% production time reduction enables daily video posting
- Ruben Hassid: 754K followers, 60M+ views using AI avatar approach
- Disclosure increases curiosity, not penalty

### Carousels (2026 Data)
- 24.42% average engagement rate (3.7x vs text-only)
- 278% more engagement than video, 303% more than images
- 15-20 seconds dwell time vs 8-10 for other formats
- Portrait format (1080x1350) optimal for mobile
- Max 30 words per slide, one point per slide
- Save rate and CTR are the two key metrics

### Commenting (2026 Algorithm)
- 18+ word comments drive 30% higher engagement
- NLP classifiers actively penalize generic comments
- Conversation threading is biggest visibility multiplier
- First 10-15 minutes is golden window
- Created DEPTH framework (Disagree, Extend, Provoke, Tie, Humanize)

### LinkedIn Live
- 24x more comments than regular posts
- Free DM access to all attendees (growth hack)
- 4-week invite ladder: 4,000 invites -> 400-650 RSVPs -> 60-80 attendees
- Narrow topic targeting works better than broad
- Monthly "cash cow" live beats weekly diluted events
