## Key Patterns Confirmed for 2026

1. **PDF Carousels = highest engagement format** (6.60% vs 2% text, 278% > video). Storytelling/contrast > listicles.
2. **External links in body = ~60% reach reduction**. Always drop link in first comment instead.
3. **Comment-gated lead magnets** ("Comment GUIDE for the framework") are the dominant 2026 lead-gen mechanic. GPT-as-magnet beats PDFs.
4. **Inbound conversion 14.6% vs 1.7% cold DM-first**. Cold acceptance has dropped to 20–30%, cold reply 5–15%. Content IS the pipeline.
5. **15-min comment reply window = +90% algo boost**. First-hour engagement decides reach ceiling.
6. **Founder posts beat brand pages 3–4x** for identical content. Personal account = vehicle, company page = supporting.
7. **LinkedIn Events convert 3–5x vs landing pages**. LinkedIn Live = 24x more comments than native video.
8. **Authority-figure comments weighted higher** than 100 generic ones. Engage Donnelly, van der Blom, Welsh, Bloom, Lenny.
