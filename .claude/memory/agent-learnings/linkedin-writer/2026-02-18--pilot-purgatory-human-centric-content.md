## Voice Patterns That Worked

### Post Voice (Jared, Founder)
- Short declarative sentences with intentional pauses
- "Nobody wants to say this out loud" framing = authentic, not corporate
- Specific engagement question targeting the actual pain point of CTOs/CIOs
- Contrarian structure: acknowledge the success story, then flip it

### Newsletter Voice (Jared, Trusted Advisor)
- Opens with "I need to tell you something" = confession hook, builds intimacy
- "What I'm Seeing" section = insider perspective framing
- Personal observation followed by data validation (not data-first)
- Previews next week's topic = subscription incentive, creates continuity
