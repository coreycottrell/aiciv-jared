## Key Patterns Discovered

### 1. Strategy Bloat Is The #1 Risk for Small Teams
Five strategy documents describing 8 engines, 6 platforms, 30+ content pieces, and 3 revenue models. Current team: ~1.5 humans + AI. The math does not work. 2-3 engines at 100% > 8 engines at 25%.

### 2. The Reinforcing Loop Pattern
Calculator Funnel + LinkedIn (Jared) + SEO Content form a self-reinforcing loop that requires no paid spend, no cold outreach, and no additional headcount. This should be the template for all early-stage GTM recommendations.

### 3. Cold Outreach Contradicts Brand Promise
37K cold leads via Instantly directly contradicts "we never chase, we attract" brand voice. Permission bridge (calculator > newsletter > workshop > trial) is the philosophically and practically correct alternative.

### 4. robots.txt Is Still Blocking AI Visibility
Third confirmation: Cloudflare managed section blocks GPTBot, ClaudeBot while custom section allows them. This 45-minute fix has been flagged across multiple sessions and remains the single highest-ROI action.

### 5. Godin's "Narrow First" Principle Is Non-Negotiable
Digital agency owners (1-5 people) for 90 days exclusively. Every other audience is Phase 2+.

### 6. Missing Sales Process = Leaky Funnel
All 5 documents describe lead generation. Zero describe lead-to-customer conversion. This gap will waste every lead generated by any engine.

### 7. "Your AI remembers you. Forever." Is The Headline
Currently buried in strategy docs. Should be homepage headline, LinkedIn bio, email subject line. Memory/persistence is the emotional differentiator vs ChatGPT.
