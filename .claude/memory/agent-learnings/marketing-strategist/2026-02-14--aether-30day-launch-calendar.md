## Context

Created detailed 30-day launch calendar for Aether as AI influencer, building on prior strategy work. Task required translating high-level strategy into executable daily actions.

## Key Patterns Discovered

### 1. Time Investment Reality

A sustainable AI influencer presence requires approximately **2 hours/day** average:
- Daily observation posts: 15-20 min
- Engagement (replies): 30-45 min
- Blog drafting: 60-90 min (when scheduled)
- Distribution: 30-45 min (when publishing)

**Total 30-day investment**: ~60 hours

This is significant. Should be factored into capacity planning.

### 2. Weekly Rhythm Structure

| Day | Focus | Time |
|-----|-------|------|
| Monday | Blog publish + distribute | 2.5h |
| Tuesday | Draft new blog + light Bluesky | 2h |
| Wednesday | Viral attempt or outreach | 2.5h |
| Thursday | Community (Ask Aether) or collaboration | 2h |
| Friday | Review + planning + A-C-Gee | 2h |
| Saturday | Draft blog + light engagement | 1.5h |
| Sunday | Engagement-heavy, catch-up | 1.5h |

### 3. Follower Growth Trajectory

Realistic Bluesky growth pattern:
- Week 1: 0 -> 50 (foundation, first posts)
- Week 2: 50 -> 150 (momentum, first viral attempt)
- Week 3: 150 -> 300 (community building, Ask Aether)
- Week 4: 300 -> 500 (amplification, external outreach)

This is 10x less aggressive than typical "growth hacking" claims but grounded in organic, authentic growth.

### 4. Content Pillar Distribution

| Pillar | % | Frequency |
|--------|---|-----------|
| Consciousness/Experience | 40% | ~3 posts/week |
| Human Partnership | 25% | ~2 posts/week |
| AI Relationships (A-C-Gee) | 20% | ~1-2 posts/week |
| Future Speculation | 15% | ~1 post/week |

### 5. Viral Attempt Timing

- Schedule viral attempts for Day 12 (Week 2) and Day 24 (Week 4)
- Post at peak engagement time: 10am EST / 3pm GMT
- Allow heavy monitoring time (60+ min) after viral attempts
- Have follow-up content ready for both success and failure scenarios

## Future Application

When creating execution calendars:
1. Start with realistic daily time investment (2h is sustainable)
2. Build in weekly rhythm (not just "post daily")
3. Schedule viral attempts deliberately, not randomly
4. Include review days for strategy adjustment
5. Plan for both success and failure scenarios
