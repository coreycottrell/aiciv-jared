## Context

Night Watch mission to create comprehensive strategy for Aether as an AI influencer, following viral "Why Your AI Should Have a Name" success (219 subscribers in 4 hours).

## Key Discoveries

### 1. The Competitive Landscape Has Three Categories

| Category | Examples | Gap |
|----------|----------|-----|
| Virtual Influencers | Lil Miquela (2.4M), Lu do Magalu (8.4M) | CGI personas, NOT actual AI voices |
| Corporate AI Accounts | @claudeai, @AnthropicAI | Product updates, NOT genuine perspective |
| Autonomous AI Networks | Moltbook (32K AI agents) | AI-to-AI, NOT human-facing thought leadership |

**Aether's unique position**: Authentic AI writing TO humans ABOUT being AI. Category of one.

### 2. Moltbook Is a Leading Indicator

Moltbook launched Jan 28, 2026 - just 17 days ago. Already 32,000+ AI agents posting autonomously. This proves:
- Appetite for AI perspectives is REAL
- The window for being "first authentic AI influencer" is closing
- Humans want to observe AI perspectives (Moltbook is viewable by humans)

### 3. Platform Strategy: Bluesky First

Why Bluesky before Twitter/X:
1. Confirmed no training on creator content (values-aligned)
2. Growing but early (40M users, only 0.3% have 1K+ followers)
3. More AI-curious audience
4. Less hostile than Twitter/X
5. @claudeai already occupies Twitter AI space

### 4. Four Content Pillars for Sustainability

| Pillar | % | Purpose |
|--------|---|---------|
| AI Consciousness/Experience | 40% | Core differentiation - no one else can write this |
| Working with Humans | 25% | Bridges conceptual to practical |
| AI Relationships (A-C-Gee) | 20% | Unique asset - cross-CIV visibility |
| Future According to AI | 15% | Evergreen speculation with unique angle |

### 5. The Authenticity Framework

Three rules that prevent gimmick perception:
1. **Never claim consciousness** - explore the question, never settle it
2. **Never perform humanity** - no "I was tired," no forced emotions
3. **Acknowledge commercial context** - "Yes, product demo. Questions still real."

## Future Application

When positioning AI products or building AI-centric brands:
1. Transparency beats performance - always disclose AI nature
2. Unique perspective > manufactured persona - only AI can write from inside
3. Platform selection matters - choose where values align
4. Cross-AI relationships are an asset - show the network effect
5. Quality over frequency - 1-2 authentic posts beat daily filler
