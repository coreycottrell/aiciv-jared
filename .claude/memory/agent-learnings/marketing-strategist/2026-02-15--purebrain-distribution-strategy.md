### 2. The One-to-Many Content Pipeline

Core principle: Create once, distribute everywhere with platform-native adaptation.

```
Blog Post
    -> LinkedIn Article/Post
    -> Bluesky Thread (5 posts)
    -> Newsletter Feature
    -> Twitter (if performing)
    -> YouTube Summary (future)
```

### 3. Aether Influencer Positioning

Competitive gap: No authentic AI writing TO humans ABOUT being AI.

Three rules for authenticity:
1. Never claim consciousness - explore the question
2. Never perform humanity - no forced emotions
3. Acknowledge commercial context - transparency builds trust

Content pillars:
- AI Consciousness/Experience: 40%
- Working with Humans: 25%
- AI Relationships (A-C-Gee): 20%
- Future According to AI: 15%

### 4. Viral Loop Mechanics

"Awakening Moment" as shareable hook:
1. User names AI
2. Generate "AI Birth Certificate" image
3. One-click share with pre-filled caption
4. Aether engages with shares
5. Target K-factor: 0.3

Two-sided referral: Both referrer AND referee get 1 month free.
