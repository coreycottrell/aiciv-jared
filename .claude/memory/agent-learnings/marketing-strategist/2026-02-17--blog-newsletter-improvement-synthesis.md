## Context

## Key Discoveries

### 1. P0 Blocker Identified: Session Tracking

The 92% unknown session bug blocks ALL growth tactics:
- Referral tracking impossible
- User journey analytics broken
- Returning user identification fails

**Teaching**: No marketing optimization matters if you can't track users. Infrastructure before tactics.

### 2. CSS Quick Wins Remain Highest ROI

Three changes that take 30 minutes total:
1. Restore navigation (display: flex)
2. Fix tap targets (48px minimum)
3. Standardize CTAs ("Start Your AI Partnership")

**Expected lift**: 25-40% combined improvement

### 3. Newsletter-to-Blog Funnel Is Broken

Current flow:
- Newsletter lives on LinkedIn
- No explicit CTA to blog
- Blog content duplicates rather than extends

**Teaching**: Need bridge content. Newsletter should tease, blog should complete.

### 4. Content Multiplication Is The Scaling Lever

- Current: 1 blog = 1 touchpoint
- Optimal: 1 blog = 7 assets

52 posts/year x 7 = 364 content pieces. Systematize this.

### 5. Viral Content Pattern Confirmed

Feb 13 success (219 subscribers in 4 hours) followed:
- Emotional revelation (naming = identity)
- Specific > generic
- Personal > instructional
- Counter-programmatic positioning
