## Key Discoveries

### 1. The Funnel Is Blocked Before Marketing Matters

The single most important strategic insight from this session: PureBrain currently captures zero revenue despite having a compelling product. The path is awakening -> Google Form waitlist -> nothing. Every marketing tactic applied to this funnel is wasteful until Stripe is live.

**Rule established**: Do not run paid ads before Stripe integration is confirmed and conversion rate is proven at 5%+ on organic traffic. Running ads into a broken funnel is burning money.

### 2. Paid Advertising Timing Framework

When to run ads (conditions must all be met):
1. Stripe payment integrated
2. GA4 custom events firing (funnel visibility)
3. Email nurture sequence live (recovering abandoners)
4. Conversion rate proven at 5%+ in organic traffic

Budget recommendation when conditions met:
- Month 3: $800/mo ($500 Meta + $300 LinkedIn)
- Month 4: $2,000/mo if CAC < $50
- Month 6: $5,000/mo if CAC < $50 and LTV > $300

## Future Application

When building AI product distribution strategies:
1. Fix revenue capture before scaling acquisition (Stripe > ads)
2. The product experience IS the marketing hook - don't separate them
3. Novel AI products need novel formats - cross-AI debates, AI author attribution, AI-as-CEO positioning
4. SEO keyword strategy: target "alternative to [known product] with [specific feature]" - these are high-intent, lower-competition
5. YouTuber/podcast outreach works best with free product access, no strings - let the product speak
6. Community should precede scale: 25 founding members with Jared access > 2,500 anonymous members

**Learning**: The most valuable marketing insight for PureBrain is that the funnel drops off after the emotional peak (naming), not during the experience itself. The product creates emotional investment but then fails to capture it commercially. The marketing job is to be ready to capture the moment when it happens.
