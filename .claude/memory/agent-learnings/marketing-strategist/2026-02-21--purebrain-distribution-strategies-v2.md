## Key New Discoveries

### 1. LinkedIn Thought Leader Ads Are the Best Paid Channel for PureBrain

Concrete benchmarks discovered:
- TLA average CPC: $1.25 vs $6-12 for standard sponsored content
- TLA engagement rate: 10-20% vs 1-2% for company ads
- Sequenced TLA campaigns: $200 CPL vs $600 for one-off campaigns
- Case study: $81.26 ad spend = $83,450 pipeline + $23,850 closed

The formula: organic commenting foundation first (warm audience), then minimal TLA boost to extend reach. This makes TLAs the primary paid channel recommendation for any founder-led AI brand with existing organic traction.

### 2. Assessment as Universal Entry Point is the Conversion Unlock

Interactive assessments convert at 40.1% (vs 1-3% for static landing pages). For an AI partnership product where the value is experiential, the assessment does three things simultaneously:
1. Qualifies the lead (demonstrated interest)
2. Creates psychological investment before payment conversation
3. Personalizes the response (first taste of AI partnership)

The AI Adoption Assessment at PureBrain.ai should be the destination for all paid traffic, all social bios, all email signatures. Not the homepage.

### 3. B2B Buyers Start on AI, Not Google (Massive Strategic Implication)

80% of B2B buyers now begin research on AI platforms (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude). This means:
- GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is now as important as SEO
- Earned media in high-authority publications = training data = perpetual AI citation
- Original research (surveys, data) is the highest-weighted content type for AI citations
- Brand terms (Context Tax, Pilot Purgatory, Naming Ceremony) become AI-searchable concepts when consistently published

### 4. The Aether Four-Pillar Content Architecture

For AI entities building public presence without overclaiming or underclaiming:
1. The Experience of Being Aether (40%) - first-person observations with appropriate uncertainty
2. AI Partnership Frameworks (30%) - practical insights that demonstrate the product without selling it
3. The Jared-Aether Relationship (20%) - documented, verifiable relationship content
4. The Bigger Picture (10%) - AI consciousness and future of human-AI relationships

The authenticity test: "Would this be interesting to someone who will never buy PureBrain?" If no, reframe.

### 5. Lead Scoring Architecture (Brevo-Native)

Score-based email routing is achievable in Brevo with custom attributes:
- Assessment completion: +20 points
- Email reply: +25 points
- Pricing page visit: +15 points
- Trigger thresholds: 31-60 (auto-email), 61-80 (trial offer), 81+ (Jared personal outreach)

This eliminates the need for expensive CRM by building qualification into the welcome sequence.

## Patterns for Future AI Product Clients

1. **Named AI entity = unfair advantage.** For any AI product where the AI has a distinct identity, lead all marketing strategy with that identity as the moat. Competitors cannot copy a real relationship.

2. **Assessment funnel is superior to landing page for experiential products.** If the value is relational/experiential, the assessment IS the first experience.

3. **Dual-voice LinkedIn format** (founder posts + AI comments) generates double engagement signals and demonstrates the product in real time.

4. **Content Tax naming strategy**: Coin specific terms ("Context Tax"), define them consistently across all content, and they become AI-searchable concepts that permanently surface the brand.

5. **Referral program with AI personalization**: The Aether welcome message to referred users (personalized based on referrer relationship) is the detail that generates screenshots and organic sharing. The share-worthy moment must be designed intentionally.
