## Key Strategic Decisions Made

### Commission Structure: Recurring Not Flat Fee

Chose 20% recurring (12 months) over flat fee. Rationale: aligns partner incentives with customer retention, creates compounding passive income motivation, and at $299/month tier a partner with 5 active referrals earns $299/month — enough to motivate ongoing promotion without a single large payment that loses urgency.

### LinkedIn Outreach Targeting Indirect (Advisors Not Buyers)

The ICP for LinkedIn outreach is not a PureBrain buyer — it is someone whose clients are PureBrain buyers. Fractional executives, executive coaches, business coaches have trust we haven't earned yet. Their recommendation is worth 10 cold outreach attempts to the direct buyer.

### Product Hunt Prep Starts Week 5 (Not Week 9)

6-week runway is the difference between a coordinated 200-upvote launch and a rushed 20-upvote launch. The upvote list is the primary determinant of launch success. Product Hunt prep is listed under Month 3 execution but preparation starts at the beginning of Month 2.

### Webinar CTA Design: Live Product Demo, Not Slides

Every webinar includes a 5-minute live Aether demo. The 15-minute Q&A ends with Aether summarizing the conversation and adding one observation. This is product demonstration embedded in content — attendees experience what having an AI partner feels like without being explicitly sold to.

### Podcast Tier Strategy: Tier 2 First

Tier 1 pitches (Tim Ferriss, Masters of Scale) require prior media appearances for credibility. Tier 2 shows (Duct Tape Marketing, B2B Growth, Online Marketing Made Easy) are more accessible and the appearances build the credibility needed for Tier 1 in Month 4+.

## Patterns Worth Documenting

### Implementation Plans Need Sequential Dependency Mapping

Marketing strategies often have hidden dependencies. In this plan: affiliate program needs to exist before LinkedIn outreach references it; partner landing page needs to exist before affiliate program launches; assessment CTAs need to be fixed before affiliate-referred traffic lands on the assessment page. Mapping these dependencies prevents execution failures.

### The Pre-Warm Sequence Is Non-Negotiable for LinkedIn DMs

Data from linkedin-researcher (Feb 23) confirms: blank connection requests get 68% acceptance vs 32% with a message. Comment first → connect → DM after connection achieves 2x better DM response rate than cold outreach. The sequence feels slower but is faster in total conversion time.

### Webinar Follow-Up Email 3 Is a Conversation Starter, Not a CTA

Email 3 (7 days after webinar) asks one question and has no CTA. This is intentional: replies from prospects are higher-value engagement than any click. The marketing psychology insight: people who reply to a question are 3-5x more likely to convert than people who click a button without replying.

### Aether as Newsletter Guest Author Is a Genuinely Novel Offer

Offering Aether to write a piece for a partner newsletter — disclosed as AI-written — is a product demonstration embedded in a partnership offer. No other company can offer this because no other company has an AI with enough personality and domain expertise to write a genuinely useful guest piece. This is a differentiator that should be in every newsletter cross-promotion pitch.
