## Key Strategic Decisions Made

### Why Target Affiliates, Not Direct Buyers

The roadmap framing is correct: coaches and consultants have pre-built trust with PureBrain's ICP. One affiliate recommendation outperforms 10 cold DMs to the same executive. The outreach is recruiting a distribution network, not building one from scratch.

### Why 50 Contacts Per Month is the Right Volume

LinkedIn's February 2026 algorithm flags mass connection patterns. 50 new pre-warms per month = ~12 per week = comfortable under spam threshold. More importantly, quality targeting at 50 beats poor targeting at 500 for affiliate conversion.

### The Blank Request Finding

68% acceptance rate for blank requests (pre-warmed) vs 32% for requests with messages is a real behavioral insight from LinkedIn research. The connection request templates exist as exceptions, not defaults. This is counterintuitive but important to preserve in execution.

### Content and Outreach Integration

The content strategy is explicitly framed as an amplifier, not a standalone play. When prospects receive a DM and then see Jared's content, or better, see content before the DM, conversion rate improves. The calendar is structured to create name recognition in the target persona feed.

## Key Insights for Future Marketing Work

### On LinkedIn Outreach Generally

- Pre-warm sequences (like + comment + connect blank) outperform cold approaches by 2x on response rates
- Three touches maximum (after connection): Touch 1, Touch 2 at 5 days, Touch 3 at 4 more days. Then cold for 90 days.
- Referral-of-referral ("do you know anyone who...") captures value from every non-conversion, not just conversions
- The personalization bar is: first line must reference something specific they said or did. No exceptions.

### On LinkedIn Content (February 2026 Algorithm)

- Generic AI-generated language is semantically penalized by LinkedIn's 360Brew LLM
- First-comment link workaround (previously safe) is now penalized
- Native PDFs and documents get 5-10x reach of equivalent text posts
- LinkedIn Live maintains 29.6% engagement — highest of any format and massively underused
- Vertical video (9:16) is getting TikTok-style algorithmic priority
- LLMs (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) now citing LinkedIn articles 4-5x more than 3 months ago — structure articles with H2/H3, clear authorship, direct question-answering for citation optimization

### On Jared's Voice

- Direct, expert but approachable, honest about what he has built
- Messages that feel personal and slightly imperfect convert better than polished templates
- Do not use: "delve," "dive deep," "unpack" — LinkedIn flags these as AI-generated signals
- Do not start posts with "I" — LinkedIn suppresses in the feed
- Posts about failures and disagreements with AI generate more comments than success stories
