## Key Strategic Decisions Made

### 1. Tagline Strategy: Lead with Memory, Not Features

Primary recommended tagline: "The AI that actually remembers how you work" (44 chars)

Rationale: Product Hunt's audience is AI power users who have tried many tools. Their primary frustration is starting every conversation from scratch. "Actually" addresses the skeptic who has heard similar claims before. This positions PureBrain against the category failure, not against specific competitors.

### 2. 6-Week Preparation Is Non-Negotiable

The difference between 200+ upvotes and 20 upvotes is almost entirely determined by the size and quality of the committed upvote list — and that list takes 6 weeks to build. Preparation starts Week 5 (March 23), even though the launch is in Week 11 (May 6).

The PH algorithm specifically weights upvotes from accounts with established history and engagement. New accounts created just to upvote have near-zero algorithmic weight. This means you cannot manufacture your way to a strong launch at the last minute.

### 3. Maker Comment Is the Most Valuable Real Estate

The first comment posted by the maker (within 5 minutes of launch going live) is pinned at the top and is the most-read content on any PH listing. The template created in `product-page-copy.md` is personal, invites specific feedback, and asks genuine questions. This is not the place for product marketing copy.

### 4. Ethical Upvote Building Only

Product Hunt's algorithm detects coordinated voting patterns, and account bans are permanent. The activation strategy in `upvote-community-strategy.md` is built entirely around genuine supporters who already have PH accounts and have already interacted with PureBrain. No purchasing, no "create an account just to vote," no bulk coordination.

### 5. Hunter as Multiplier, Not Requirement

A top hunter can drive 300-800 extra upvotes from their network. But the backup plan (maker-only launch) is legitimate — many strong PH launches have done this. The hunter decision deadline is April 12 (T-24). If no confirmed hunter by then, proceed as maker-only and compensate with heavier email list activation.

### 6. Post-Launch Conversion Window Is 48 Hours

PH traffic spikes on launch day and drops sharply by Day 3. The `post-launch-followup.md` front-loads all conversion actions into the first 48 hours. After that, the value shifts to long-term: the Product Hunt badge, the permanent listing, the retrospective content, and the press pitch using launch data as a hook.

## Product Hunt Mechanics Documented

For future launches and reference:

- **Submission time**: 12:01 AM PST (PH resets at midnight PST — earliest submission gets most exposure hours)
- **Algorithm factors**: Upvote velocity (early upvotes weighted more), comment response rate, hunter follower count, account age of upvoters
- **Gallery specs**: 5 images at 1270x760px, logo at 240x240px PNG transparent, video 2-3 min MP4 with captions
- **Short description**: 260 char limit
- **Tagline**: 60 char limit
- **Hunter finding**: producthunt.com/leaderboard — filter by recent activity in AI/productivity

## PureBrain Positioning Angle for PH

The product hunt audience responds to: problems stated clearly, founder authenticity, "why now," and immediate try-ability.

PureBrain's strongest angle for this audience:
- "Most businesses are using AI wrong. Here's how we know, and here's how you find out where you stand."
- The free assessment as the zero-friction entry point (try it without paying)
- The specificity of scoring (not "Level 2 of 5" — actual percentage scores per category)

What the PH audience does NOT respond to: corporate language, "revolutionary," "cutting-edge," or anything that sounds like a press release.
