## Key Research Findings Applied

- **Identity > Cash for B2B referrals**: Research (Cello, Expando AI) shows identity-based programs generate 30-50% higher referral rates in knowledge services
- **AI influencer cringe root cause**: Content that announces expertise rather than demonstrating it. Fix: observation-based content only
- **Physical mail open rates**: 80%+ vs email 20-25% — dramatic returns on low-volume physical touches
- **Proactive corrections**: Customers who experience vendor proactively correcting themselves have 3x higher satisfaction scores
- **72-hour psychology**: Three objections in B2B decision delay: "need more info" → "don't understand product" → "I'll do it later". Each email removes exactly one.
- **LinkedIn Feb 2026 algorithm**: Rewards reply-generating content; penalizes engagement bait and generic AI language
