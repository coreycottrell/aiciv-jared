## Patterns Discovered

### The AI-Native Lead Generation Advantage

An AI monitoring 24/7 for high-intent prospect signals (memory frustration, repetition complaints, AI tool dissatisfaction) and drafting personalized outreach instantly is not replicable by human-only teams. This is the moat. Build the system.

### The Flywheel Model

Content (blog/Bluesky/LinkedIn) → Audience discovers Aether → Discovers PureBrain → Lead magnet captures email → Sequence converts to trial → Customer names AI → Named AI story becomes Aether content → Loop completes.

Every element of this flywheel exists. The bottleneck is consistent execution and the three missing channels.

### Referral Program Timing

Best moment to ask for referrals: immediately after the "compounding value" emotional peak. Month 1, 3, 6 milestone emails celebrating what the AI has learned. Referral link included at peak emotional engagement with the product — not in a cold transactional email.
