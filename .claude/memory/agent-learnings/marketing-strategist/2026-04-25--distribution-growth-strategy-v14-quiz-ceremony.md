## Key New Discoveries

1. **Quiz funnels achieve 28% conversion on cold traffic** when using variable logic, sub-1-second load, and personalized result pages. Interactive quizzes hit 70-90% completion vs 15-25% for static forms.

2. **The Dopamine-to-Commitment Pipeline**: Quiz (micro-rewards per question) -> Personalized result (identity label) -> Naming ceremony (emotional peak) -> Shareable birth certificate (viral loop) -> Personalized email nurture (using THEIR chosen AI name). 3. **5 LinkedIn post strategies that drove signups**:
   - Build-in-public revenue milestones (Buildpad: $0 to $7.3K MRR, 60K users)
   - "Called out" problem-agitation posts (34% more inbound, 18% higher deal size)

4. **Zero-click strategy is critical**: Posts with external links see ~60% reach reduction. Full value must live IN the post. Links go in first comment.

5. **Comments weighted 15x more than likes** by LinkedIn algorithm. Posts asking for specific comment responses (e.g., "drop 1, 2, or 3") outperform generic CTAs.
