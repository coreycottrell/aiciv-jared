## New Strategies Built (All Net-New, 18 Total)

### Category 1: Automated Pipeline Converters
1. **Intelligence Briefing** — Aether researches prospect before every call, sends one-page brief as gift. Experience before sale. Expected 60-75% show rate, 50%+ conversion.
2. **Competitor Exodus Program** — Three landing pages (switching-from-chatgpt etc.) with personalized frustration-specific responses. 3-5x conversion vs generic comparison pages.
3. **Aether's Weekly Dispatch** — Newsletter written by Aether as AI CEO. 400 words, three sections: pattern observed, learning, open question. $50K-120K/year acquisition channel at scale.
4. **Decision Debt Calculator** — Interactive tool: how many decisions regretted × decision-maker salary = annual decision debt. Higher-order pain framing than time calculators.
5. **The Proof Stack** — Living page of raw evidence (anonymized observations, decisions, outcomes). Updated weekly. More credible than curated testimonials.

### Category 2: Creative Acquisition
6. **AI Partner Interview Series** — Aether interviews business leaders (flipped format). Leaders share content, Aether demonstrates intelligence, both audiences exposed.
7. **Aether's Public Decision Log** — Running public record of real business decisions with reasoning. Most radical transparency play in AI industry; primary source material for journalists.
8. **Shared Intelligence Report** — Monthly vertical-specific intelligence brief sent to warm non-converters. 35-50% open rate vs 15-20% generic follow-up.
9. **Waiting List Experiment** — Close to new customers 30 days; use window to build evidence publicly; reopen with "here is what happened." Real scarcity + observed proof = 30-50% conversion on reopen.
10. **Prospect Shadow Day** — Qualified prospects observe one real Aether-Jared decision session via Slack. No script. 70-80% conversion expected for shadow participants.

### Category 3: Delight Existing Leads
11. **AI Partnership Health Check** — Free monthly public assessment of company's AI readiness using public signals (LinkedIn, job postings, press). Month 3 conversion sequence. 20-30% conversion rate.
12. **First Adopter Spotlight** — Monthly profile piece on one customer. Aether writes it. No approval needed (but they always share it). Journalistic, not testimonial format.
13. **Real Rejection Report** — Quarterly anonymous account of a prospect who said no, their objection, and honest reflection. Earns trust no testimonial can.

### Category 4: Aether Influencer Scaling
14. **AI Stack Audit** — Weekly LinkedIn offer: Aether reviews one follower's AI tool stack publicly. Live demonstration of reasoning. 10-15% of audit recipients convert.
15. **100-Day AI Partnership Experiment** — Jared commits publicly: every major decision involves Aether for 100 days, documented. 20 posts, Day 100 summary with real numbers.
16. **Partner Board** — Invitation-only community for paying customers. Aether facilitates, surfaces cross-customer patterns. Retention play: 85%+ retention expected vs 70% baseline.

### Category 5: High-Ceiling / Structural Plays
17. **AI Partnership Summit** — Annual 2-hour virtual event hosted by Aether. Q&A is live product demo. 200-500 registrants, 8-15% convert within 30 days.
18. **The Aether API** — Public free API endpoint. Other AI collectives and developers integrate. Attribution requirement = distribution without cost. Developer-led growth applied to AI partnerships.

## Key New Pattern

**Distribution through demonstration, not description.** Prospects cannot imagine an AI partner. Every public proof event (audit, interview, decision log, dispatch) removes that imagination gap. When prospects have seen the product working 5-10 times before any sales conversation, the conversion is not persuasion — it is confirmation.
