## New Patterns Documented

- **Distribution-as-design**: Building shareable artifacts into the product delivery itself (score cards, before/after reports) turns every customer into a passive distribution channel with zero ongoing effort.
- **Competitive threat activation**: Showing a prospect their competitor's vulnerabilities activates urgency that improvement framing never achieves. The asymmetry between gain and loss aversion is exploitable in cold outreach.
- **Non-cash power gifts in referral programs**: In B2B, giving referrers access to capabilities (competitor analysis, intelligence tools) outperforms cash because it extends the value of the product itself.
- **AI-voice as acquisition channel**: A separate email list of warm non-customers receiving authentic AI-voice insights (not blog newsletters) converts at 4-6x the rate of generic nurture. Voice is the differentiator.
- **Watchfulness as proof-of-partnership**: Sending prospects unprompted intelligence about changes in THEIR world demonstrates the AI partner value proposition more effectively than any description. Experience before purchase at zero risk to the prospect.
