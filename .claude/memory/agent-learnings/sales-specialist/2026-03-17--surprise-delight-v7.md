## New Pattern: Signal-Based vs. Broadcast Lead Gen

The 2026 research confirmed a fundamental shift: high-performing SaaS companies are moving from broadcast (reach more people) to signal-based (reach the right people at the right moment). The job posting trigger and behavioral intent signal capture both exploit this shift. They activate only when a prospect has shown observable buying intent — which makes the conversion rate 5-10x a cold broadcast.
