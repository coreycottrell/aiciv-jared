## Key Learning

**"Diagnosis creates desire faster than any pitch."**

The AI Difference Audit (Aether researches a prospect's company and shows where AI could save them 5-10 hours/week) is the highest-conversion lead gen tactic available because the prospect diagnoses their own problem. By the time they see the PureBrain CTA, they have already told themselves why they need it. Zero selling required.

**Second key learning**: The enterprise sales motion should not launch until 20+ paying customers are in the system. Enterprise outreach before product-market fit is validated wastes high-value relationship capital.

**Third key learning**: The optimal referral ask timing is at peak emotional engagement (milestone emails at Month 1, 3, 6) — not at the beginning of the relationship or in generic emails. Research confirms milestone-timed referral asks convert at 2-3x the rate of generic requests.
