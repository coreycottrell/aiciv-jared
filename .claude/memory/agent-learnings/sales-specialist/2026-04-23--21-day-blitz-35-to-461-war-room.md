## Context

Task: Build a 21-day war room plan to go from 35 to 461 paying customers for PureBrain.ai. Product is AI partner service ($149/$499/$999 tiers). Assets: 35 customers, 21 affiliates, 367 segmented LinkedIn profiles, 47 blog posts, automated onboarding.

## Key Strategic Decisions

1. **Free trial is the unlock**: 14-day no-card trial removes $149 objection. Named AI activated in 4 minutes. Product sells itself. 30-40% trial-to-paid conversion standard for SaaS with good activation.

2. **Founding 500 scarcity**: Real price increase May 15 ($149->$197, $499->$579, $999->$1,089). Counter on page. Genuine scarcity only.

3. **Affiliate restructure**: 5% monthly commission motivates nobody. $50-100 upfront cash per sign-up for 21-day sprint = 33-67% CAC but survivable at 6+ month LTV.

4. **Calculator email capture = #1 revenue leak**: Every hour without email gate wastes traffic. Fix DAY 1.

5. **Realistic forecast**: 461 achievable WITH ~$9,500 paid ad budget. Without paid, ceiling is ~200-250 (still 7x growth). No single channel does 20/day alone.

## Outreach Math

- 30 DMs/day x 21 days = 630 touches. At 10% reply, 30% convert = ~19 from cold outreach.
- 14 hot prospects with personal Loom videos = 8-12 conversions.
- 35 existing customers x 2 referrals each (emergency "1 month free" offer) = 40-70.
- Paid ads at $450/day for 21 days = 130-230 customers at $54 avg CAC.

## Key Learning

**"Diagnosis creates desire faster than any pitch."** (Reconfirmed from v9 strategy.) The AI Audit — Aether researches prospect's company, shows where AI saves 10-20 hrs/week — is highest-conversion lead gen. Prospect self-diagnoses the problem. Zero selling required. Send unsolicited to top 50 unconverted prospects.

**Second learning**: Affiliate programs fail when the per-unit reward is too small to change behavior. $7.45/mo (5% of $149) is invisible money. $50-100 upfront cash is "I'll text 3 friends right now" money. The psychology is completely different.

**Third learning**: Advisory boards are underutilized sales channels. Each advisor referring 3 people = warm intros from trusted sources. Use the board meeting as a forcing function.
