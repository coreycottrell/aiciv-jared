## Context

Researched additional distribution platforms for PureBrain.ai blog/newsletter content to reach business professionals interested in AI productivity.

---

## Key Findings

### Community Platforms

1. **Hacker News** (Audience: 9/10)
   - MUST build karma before self-promotion
   - Best time: 8:00 AM EST, Wednesday
   - Show HN is NOT for blog posts (demos/tools only)
   - Voting ring detector prevents manipulation

2. **Product Hunt** (Audience: 8/10)
   - Launch 12:01 AM PST for max 24-hour window
   - AI products face skepticism - vagueness punished
   - Assets + narrative > strong hunter
   - DON'T vanish after launch day

3. **Indie Hackers** (Audience: 9/10)
   - Perfect for Jared's founder journey
   - Transparency and revenue sharing expected
   - Build-in-public culture

4. **Dev.to** (Audience: 6/10)
   - Technical content only
   - You don't build domain authority (Dev.to does)
   - Good for amplification, not primary channel

### Content Syndication

1. **Medium** - CRITICAL finding:
   - ALWAYS use Import Tool (not copy-paste)
   - Import Tool automatically adds canonical URL
   - Without canonical, Google indexes Medium first
   - "If two identical posts exist, Google trusts Medium more"

2. **Substack**
   - Cross-promotion is gold (find 5 similar-audience writers)
   - Live sessions attract new subscribers
   - Treat as hub, build bridges from outside

3. **Quora**
   - 300M+ monthly users, 63% excited about AI
   - Build authority through answers, not promotion
   - High-traffic questions = long-tail SEO

### Niche Communities

1. **Discord** - Non-gaming communities now dominate
   - 2+ week observation before engagement
   - AI-powered moderation standard
   - Target: Buildspace (50K+), OpenAI Discord (500K+)

2. **Slack Communities**
   - More professional than Discord
   - Target: Demand Curve, Exit Five, RevGenius
   - Slack AI integration opportunity

3. **Facebook Groups** - Declining but still viable
   - Private groups and niche hubs growing
   - Native content > external links

### Aggregators

1. **There's An AI For That** - HIGHEST ROI
   - $347 one-time, permanent listing
   - 4.7M monthly visitors, 470K newsletter subs
   - Manual human review (quality filter)
   - Launch offer: $300 PPC bonus if TAAFT first

2. **Other AI Directories** (free):
   - Toolify, AIxploria, AI Tools Directory, TheAISurf
   - Use ListingBott ($99) for bulk submission
