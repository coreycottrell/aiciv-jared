# AI Directory Submission Research: PureBrain.ai

**Date**: 2026-02-23
**Type**: synthesis
**Topic**: Best AI directories for submitting a consultancy/partnership AI platform

## Context

Researched top AI tool directories and B2B service directories for PureBrain.ai, which is an AI partnership platform (consultancy model, NOT SaaS). Two-track strategy required: AI tool directories + B2B service directories.

## Key Findings

### Top B2B Service Directories (consult model)
- **Clutch.co**: DA 89, 2.4M/mo visitors, FREE basic profile, requires client reviews to rank. BEST for consulting firms.
- **GoodFirms**: 110K+ companies, FREE profile, AI consulting category exists, profiles cited by Perplexity/GPT.
- **DesignRush**: Has "AI Consultancy" category, FREE basic profile, $200/mo for premium placement.

### Top AI Tool Directories (tech buyers)
- **There's An AI For That**: 2-4M/mo visitors, DR 76+, $347 paid OR free monthly X thread
- **aitools.fyi**: 850K/mo visitors, 40% CTR, FREE or $20 fast-track - best ROI
- **FutureTools.io**: Free, selectively curated by Matt Wolfe, novel tools preferred
- **Futurepedia**: 484K/mo visitors, FREE or $497 verified listing with 1K+ avg clicks per tool

### Positioning Key for Tool Directories
For SaaS-focused directories, frame as "AI business partner platform" not "consulting service" - emphasize the dedicated AI partner (Aether) that learns company context as the product differentiator.

## Priority Order
1. Clutch.co (free, highest DA, business buyers)
2. GoodFirms (free, AI consulting category)
3. TAAFT ($347 or free monthly)
4. aitools.fyi ($20 fast-track)
5. FutureTools (free, selective)
6. DesignRush (free basic)
7. Futurepedia (free or $497 verified)

- Long description (150-250 words)
- Logo PNG (512x512+)
- 3-5 screenshots
- Demo video (optional but increases approval)
- Client testimonials (critical for Clutch/GoodFirms ranking)
