# PureBrain Blog Banner Style Guide

**Created**: 2026-02-17
**Source**: Jared's approved design
**Reference Image**: docs/from-telegram/PureBrain.ai blog post - Newsletter size.png

---

## Banner Layout (Top to Bottom)

### 1. Header Row
- **Top Left**: PUREBRAIN.ai logo text
  - "PUREBR" in PT Blue (#2a93c1)
  - "AI" in PT Orange (#f1420b)
  - "N" in PT Blue
  - ".ai" in white
- **Top Right**: Pure Brain hexagon logo (orange swirl icon)

### 2. Background
- **NOT plain gradients** - use actual imagery
- Neural brain visualization (glowing blue/cyan)
- Data visualization elements (charts, graphs, dots)
- Deep blue base with glowing neural connections
- Translucent overlay so text is readable

### 3. Title Area (Centered)
- **Main Title**: ALL CAPS, white, large bold font
- **Subtitle**: Sentence case, white, smaller italic font
- Example:
  - "WHY AI MEMORY CHANGES EVERYTHING"
  - "The foundation of AI partnership"

### 4. Attribution Bar
- Black background bar
- "AETHER - AI PARTNER AT PURE TECHNOLOGY"
- "AETHER" in PT Blue (#2a93c1)
- "AI" in PT Orange (#f1420b)
- Rest in white

### 5. CTA Bar (Bottom)
- Black rounded rectangle
- "Awaken Your Partner AI at PUREBRAIN.ai"
- "PUREBRAIN" with proper color coding (PUREBR blue, AI orange, N blue, .ai white)

| Color | Hex | Usage |

| PT Blue | #2a93c1 | "PUREBR", "N", "AETHER" |
| PT Orange | #f1420b | "AI" in branding |
| White | #ffffff | Main text, ".ai" |
| Black | #000000 | Attribution/CTA bar backgrounds |

---

## Dimensions

- **Blog/Newsletter**: 1456 x 816 (or similar 16:9 ratio)
- **LinkedIn**: May need 1200 x 627 crop
- **Bluesky**: 1200 x 630 works

---

## Key Principles

1. **Use real imagery** - neural brains, data viz, NOT plain gradients
2. **Readable text** - ensure contrast with background
3. **Consistent branding** - PUREBRAIN.ai color coding on every image
4. **Professional polish** - looks like enterprise marketing, not AI-generated
5. **CTA always present** - "Awaken Your Partner AI at PUREBRAIN.ai"

---

## For Future Generations

When creating blog banners:
1. Find/generate a relevant background image (neural, tech, business)
2. Apply dark overlay for text readability
3. Add PUREBRAIN.ai top left
4. Add hexagon logo top right
5. Center the title (ALL CAPS) and subtitle
6. Add AETHER attribution bar
7. Add CTA bar at bottom
