# PureBrain 5-Year Financial Model — Key Assumptions & Findings
**Date**: 2026-03-08
**Type**: financial-model
**Topic**: PureBrain.ai 5-year projection baseline

## Confirmed Pricing (from purebrain.ai WP REST API)
- Partnered: $499/month
- Unified: $999/month
- Awakened: $999/month (waitlist/infrastructure tier — UNDERPRICED)

## Key Financial Assumptions
- Starting capital: $50K
- Philippines team cost: $1,000–$2,500/person/month
- Anthropic API cost: $20/user/month (blended, Year 1)
- Gross margin: 79% (Year 1) → 88% (Year 5)
- Monthly churn: Partnered 3%, Unified 1.5%, Awakened 0.8%

## Model Outputs (Base Case)
- Year 1 ARR: $882K subscription + $95K services = ~$1M
- Year 3 ARR: ~$4.76M
- Year 5 ARR: ~$10.15M (subscription + services + expansion)
- Break-even: Month 14 (cumulative)
- First EBITDA-positive month: Month 3
- Year 5 headcount: 22 FTE
- Year 5 EBITDA margin: 44%

## Biggest Financial Insight
PMG warm network LTV:CAC ratios are 110:1 to 416:1. Industry benchmark is 3:1.
This is the single most important financial asset in the model.
First 90 days of PMG outreach determines whether Year 1 is $1M or $2.4M ARR.

## Critical Recommendation
Reprice Awakened to $2,499/month in Year 2. Currently at $999 = enterprise infrastructure at SMB price.
This single change adds $420K ARR in Year 3 without acquiring a single new customer.
