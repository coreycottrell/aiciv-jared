## Key Insights for Future Work

- Gross margin 53% in Y1 is correct and normal for AI SaaS. Path to 65%+ is operational (caching, volume discounts, AI-deflected support).
- Payment processing (2.9% + $0.30) is frequently omitted from SaaS COGS. At $24.50 total COGS, Stripe fees are $5.50 — the 3rd largest cost component.
- One Fortune 500 white-label deal adds $84M Year 5 ARR. Jared's P&G/Coke network is the most valuable off-balance-sheet asset.
- Annual contracts eliminate monthly churn risk and provide upfront cash. Priority from Day 1.
- The single most impactful action: Get one enterprise client to sign annual contract before or at launch.
