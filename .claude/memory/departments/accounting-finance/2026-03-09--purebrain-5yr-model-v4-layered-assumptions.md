## Key Modeling Decisions

- v3 churn corrections kept: 5% launch phase → 2.5% by Month 9 → 1% at 12mo+
- v3 COGS kept: $22.50/customer/month Y1, declining to $10.20 by Y5
- v3 phased hiring kept: 17 FTE at launch → 29 end of Y1 → 86 at Y5
- v3 enterprise sales cycles kept: 3–6 months to close
- PMG modeled as existing revenue: ~$2.4M Y1, growing to $14.2M Y5
- Enterprise builds modeled separately from subscriptions: $50K–$15M contracts
- Licensing as near-zero marginal cost at scale: highest margin stream
- The $16B+ figure lives in Year 7–9 at Vision pace, or Year 5 if 3–5 Fortune 500 WL deals close

## The Licensing Lever

Single most important upside: one Fortune 500 white-label deal.
P&G (100K employees) at $85/seat/month royalty = $102M ARR from one client.
Three such deals = $306M ARR from licensing alone.
Jared's warm pipeline (P&G, Coke, Pepsi, Mars, Kroger, Target, MGM, Carnival) makes this non-speculative.
