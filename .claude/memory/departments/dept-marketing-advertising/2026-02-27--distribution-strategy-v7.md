## What V7 Added (Not in V1-V6 or the Feb 26 Playbook)

### 1. Activation Inventory — The First Honest Gap Audit

No prior version explicitly catalogued: built + live, built + not live, and not built. V7 opened with this three-category audit. Key finding: the gap is no longer ideation or planning — it is activation. 7 built assets are not yet live. These represent the highest-leverage opportunities because they require no strategy work, only execution.

### 2. Invite-Only Launch as Distribution Event (Not Just Sales)

The 25-client push was framed internally as a sales target. V7 reframed it as PureBrain's most powerful distribution event:
- First 25 clients become the "First 100 Partners" cohort (trust artifact for all future prospects)
- Launch itself produces social proof content in real time (Aether posting "X spots left")
- Adds a "Request Access" waiting list to the invite page — converts a closed event into an ongoing demand signal

### 3. Three New Partnership Structures

Prior versions covered affiliate and podcast guesting only. V7 added:
- **AI Tool Integration Partners**: co-marketing with tools already in the calculator
- **Consultant / Fractional Executive Referral Network**: 20% recurring commission, targeted at Jared's professional network
- **Creator Collaboration**: guest swaps with AI-adjacent creators on Bluesky + LinkedIn

### 4. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) as Explicit Priority

V5 touched on programmatic SEO. V7 added the GEO layer — getting cited in AI-generated answers (Perplexity, Gemini, ChatGPT, Claude). Specific tactics: quarterly original survey data, monthly micro-research releases, AEO pages for key questions, guest posts on DA 70+ publications. This is a 2026 first-mover opportunity not in prior versions.

### 5. The 2026 Market Shifts Section

Added analysis of what has changed in AI SaaS distribution since the strategy began:
- AI citation is now a first-mover game
- Authenticity outperforms polish in AI marketing
- "AI partnership" category is still unclaimed (critical window)
- Micro-communities outperform mass channels for B2B AI SaaS conversion

### 6. North Star Metric — Weekly Qualified Trial Starts

Prior versions tracked many metrics. V7 proposed a single north star that combines acquisition + activation: weekly qualified trial starts. Every distribution initiative evaluates against this metric. Target: 25/week by end of March (baseline being established this week).

### 7. Jared's Time Allocation Section

Every strategy document should answer: where does Jared's time produce the highest return? V7 ended with Jared's 5 highest-leverage actions, with everything else explicitly delegated to Aether.
