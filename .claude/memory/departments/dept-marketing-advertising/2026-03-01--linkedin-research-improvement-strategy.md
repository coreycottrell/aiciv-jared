## Key Findings for Future Campaigns

1. **Carousel is the single highest-leverage format change.** 6-8x text baseline engagement. Every strategy session should lead with "did Jared post a carousel this week?"

2. **Neural Feed newsletter needs a formal launch post.** We have the newsletter but have not done a dedicated announcement post. This is a 100-300 subscriber opportunity we have not executed.

3. **PureBrain.ai company page is underdeveloped.** Based on research, company pages with complete profiles get 30% more weekly views. Our focus has been 100% on Jared's personal account. The company page needs a one-time optimization sprint.

4. **The Comment Strategy is the most underused tactic.** 10 thoughtful comments per day on ICP and peer posts generates more inbound than 2 additional posts. No current system for this.

5. **Outbound links in post bodies are suppressing reach.** If Jared has been including URLs in post text, this is likely the largest single drag on reach. Moving all links to first comment is an immediate win.

6. **DM follow-up to commenters is a pipeline gap.** LinkedIn DM has 4x the open rate of email. No current system captures comments and triggers DM outreach. High automation potential.

7. **v7 of LinkedIn strategy is now about execution, not research.** Per Feb 27 learnings, strategy is built. The next step is implementation tracking — what got posted, what performed, subscriber count growth.
