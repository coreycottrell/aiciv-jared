## Key Findings

### Algorithm (March 2026)
- Organic reach down 65% but engagement quality up 12%
- Comments are 8-15x more valuable than likes
- First 90 minutes after posting determines 70% of ultimate reach
- Carousels: 24.42% engagement rate, 11.2x impressions vs text-only
- Video reach collapsed (down 200%) — skip unless highly produced
- External links in post body suppressed — always put in first comment
- AI-detected generic content is penalized

### Platform Stats
- 80% of B2B social media leads come from LinkedIn
- 277% more effective for lead gen than Facebook + Twitter combined
- 89% of B2B marketers use it; 62% report it produces leads
- Lead Gen Forms convert at 5x vs external landing pages

### Content Strategy for Jared
- 4 pillars: AI Director Framework, Real Story, Industry Spotlights, Neural Feed
- Minimum 3 posts/week; optimal 5 posts/week
- 2 carousels per week minimum
- Best hook types: contrarian, specific number + result, direct call-out, open loop, bold opinion
- Optimal post length: 900-1,500 characters

### Lead Gen Tactics
- Signal-based outreach (funding news, executive changes) = highest ROI
- Comment engagement mining after each post
- "Comment to receive" CTA drives engagement + warm leads simultaneously
- DM welcome sequence: Day 1 / Day 3 / Day 7

### Neural Feed Growth
- Teaser post day before each edition = passive subscriber growth
- Pin in Featured section = every profile visitor sees it
- Add link to every post's first comment
- Weekly cadence, never miss — 3+ consecutive editions = 4x subscriber retention
