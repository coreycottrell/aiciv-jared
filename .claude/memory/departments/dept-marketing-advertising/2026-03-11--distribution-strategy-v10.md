## New Channels Introduced in v10

### PureBrain Distribution
1. **AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)** — LLM-first discovery via Perplexity, ChatGPT, Claude. Definitional content, FAQ schema, PR citations as LLM training signal.
2. **Workflow Template Library** — Free downloadable templates by role. Requires PureBrain trial to activate. Shareable URL = product-led virality.
3. **Assessment as Viral Distribution** — Shareable results card with pre-filled LinkedIn copy. Team comparison mechanic. Industry benchmarking report.
4. **Newsletter-to-Newsletter Acquisition** — Sponsorships (5 target newsletters), swaps (free), content contributions (Aether as AI author is a differentiating pitch).
5. **Vertical Community Ownership** — Slack and Discord communities. 30-day contribution before product mention. Jared as human presence, Aether in AI-specific communities.
6. **Strategic Co-Creation** — Joint content with Brevo, Notion, Loom. PureBrain as the intelligence layer above their tools.
7. **100 Customers Public Campaign** — Public milestone tracker on purebrain.ai/100. Weekly progress posts create ongoing content + urgency.

### Aether Influencer Scaling
1. **Neural Feed as AI-Native Media Outlet** — Aether as Editor-in-Chief. Press kit for Aether. Pitching Neural Feed as a SOURCE.
2. **Documented AI Development Arc** — Monthly state-of-Aether posts. Public learning logs. Year One narrative.
3. **Cross-Platform Synchronicity** — Synchronized storylines across platforms (not just cross-posting). Multi-platform followers as highest-value advocates.
4. **Podcast Booking as System** — Tiered targets (Tier 1/2/3 by audience size). Booking tracker. Non-human angle as lead pitch. Post-episode distribution system.
5. **Aether as Proof of Concept** — One-sentence bridge in every piece of content: "What Aether does, PureBrain enables." Converts audience curiosity → product curiosity.
6. **AI Influencer Alliance** — Deliberate relationships with 5-10 non-competing AI-forward accounts. Algorithmic amplification through coordinated engagement.

### The Integrated Flywheel
All channels connect: LinkedIn post → Neural Feed subscriptions → assessment → shareable card → podcast → template → trial → 100-customer campaign → AEO citation → cycle repeats at scale.
