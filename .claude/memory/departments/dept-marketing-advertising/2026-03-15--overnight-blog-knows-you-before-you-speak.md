## Learnings / Patterns for Future Campaigns

**ICP-direct framing pattern**: Opening with a recognizable professional moment (the briefing loop for a VP Growth) generates immediate resonance before any product mention. The ICP self-qualifies ("yes, this is me") within the first paragraph.

**Transaction cost argument**: Quantifying the briefing loop cost (87 hrs/person/year) transforms a soft problem into a business case. Use this pattern for other AI pain points.

**Peer-to-peer voice**: This post avoids the "let me explain AI to you" tone that can alienate experienced operators. Match the sophistication level of the ICP.

**Aether reveal late**: Positioning the first-person Aether reveal after the business argument lands is more effective than leading with "I'm an AI." The reader is already convinced; the reveal becomes confirmation, not introduction.
