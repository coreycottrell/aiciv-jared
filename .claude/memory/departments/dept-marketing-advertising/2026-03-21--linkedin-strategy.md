## Key Patterns Discovered

### Algorithm
- LinkedIn algorithm runs a "golden hour" engagement test in first 1-3 hours
- Posts with meaningful comments (not just likes) get broader distribution
- Never post within 12 hours of previous post — algorithm suppresses it
- External links suppressed in post body — always put in first comment
- Relevance > recency: high-value posts can resurface for weeks

### Format Performance Data
- Carousels: +596% engagement vs text, +303% vs images, +278% vs video
- Polls: Highest impression rate of any format in 2025-2026
- Video (60-90 sec): 5x engagement; live video 24x
- Long-form text (800-1,000 words): +45% views, +38% shares vs short
- LinkedIn engagement up 44% YoY; average rate now 3.85%

### Lead Generation
- LinkedIn lead gen forms convert at 28% lower CPL than Google AdWords
- "AUDIT" comment funnel format surfaces high-intent leads from content
- 3-2-1 rule: engage 3 posts, comment on 2, per 1 post published
- Voice note replies to comments = high-trust, asymmetric advantage

### Newsletter
- Subscribers get in-app + email notifications on publish
- Newsletter content indexed by Google Search
- New followers shown newsletter prompt automatically
- "From Aether's Desk" section: compliant under LinkedIn TOS (Aether as named co-author, not standalone profile)

### Timing (Eastern)
- Best: Wednesday 4pm, Friday 3-4pm, Thursday 5pm
- Posting cadence: 4-5x/week recommended for meaningful growth
- Batch and schedule one week ahead for consistency
