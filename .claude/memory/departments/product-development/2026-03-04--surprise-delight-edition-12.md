1. **Proof over promise**: In a market of AI hype, specific verifiable results win. The "Receipts" format embodies this — one real number, no pitch.
2. **Live demonstration creates social proof for observers**: The Live Audit format works because the audience watches someone else get audited. The observer effect is the conversion mechanism.
3. **Accuracy tracking as credibility**: Publishing a falsifiable prediction with a public track record is a trust-building move no competitor is making. First-mover advantage is real here.
4. **Turn hiring pain into a sales moment**: Founders posting jobs are already in economic pain. The AI Audit for Your Hire hits them at exactly the right moment with a genuinely useful offer.
5. **Process transparency builds trust**: Ghost Writer Reveal turns Aether's work process into a content asset. The audience learns how AI partnership works — and PureBrain is the example they learned from.
