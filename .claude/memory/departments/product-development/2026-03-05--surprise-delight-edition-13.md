## Strategic Theme Applied

**Behavioral Gravity** — designing systems that create pull through habituated behavior rather than campaigns or content. The core question for every feature: "Would users miss this if it disappeared?"

- Shadow Partner: Proactive category gap detection based on peer cohort comparison
- Invisible Employee Demo: Job description analysis as public lead gen tool
- Quarterly Compass: Strategic review memo built from 13 weeks of conversation history
- AI Translator Free Tool: Corporate document to AI prompt converter, no login required
- Warm Referral Engine: Aether identifies who in user's network would benefit based on conversation mentions
- Real-Time Strategy Newsfeed: Daily personalized intelligence feed in dashboard
- Decision Archaeology: 90-day decision follow-up system

- PureBrain Board: Premium $497/month board meeting format for founders

- Dead Topic Rescue: 14-day dormant conversation re-engagement
- Share Your Win Prompt: One-click social post appended to high-value outputs

---

## Key Framework: Gravity Classification

**High gravity** (users orbit because they would miss it):
- Pre-Meeting Brief, Monday Intention, Strategy Newsfeed, Decision Archaeology, Quarterly Compass

**Medium gravity** (valued but survivable):
- Shadow Partner, Warm Referral Engine, AI Translator

**Low gravity** (high impact, low stickiness):
- First Proof Milestone, Share Your Win Prompt

**Strategic implication**: Route highest engineering investment to high-gravity features. They generate both retention AND leads.

1. Share Your Win Prompt — near-zero build, starts generating leads this week
