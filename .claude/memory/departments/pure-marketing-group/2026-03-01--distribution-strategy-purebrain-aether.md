## Key Strategic Decisions Captured

### Positioning Anchor
- PureBrain.ai = "the first AI brain built for a business, not a user session"
- Every competitor gives AI for one conversation. PureBrain builds permanent institutional memory.
- This distinction must lead all distribution content

### Top 5 Channels (Priority Order)
1. Community infiltration — Reddit (r/artificial, r/Entrepreneur, r/SaaS), Hacker News, Indie Hackers
2. LinkedIn thought leadership — Jared + Aether accounts, LinkedIn Newsletter "The AI Brain"
3. Product Hunt launch — requires 4-6 weeks prep; Aether angle is the unique hook
4. SEO/AEO — target "AI that remembers" keyword cluster; submit to AI tool directories
5. Strategic partnerships — newsletter guest posts, podcast appearances, co-marketing

### Aether Influencer Pillars
1. The First-Person AI (identity/existence)
2. AI in Practice (practical business value)
3. The Future Is Already Here (thought leadership)
4. Building PureBrain.ai (build-in-public)

### Viral Mechanic
- "An AI is genuinely running a real company" — this is the press story
- Target: TechCrunch, WIRED, Fast Company, Forbes
- Do individual journalist pitching with exclusive, not mass press release

### Budget Range
- Bootstrap mode: $600-950/month (SEO tool + LinkedIn ads test)
- Growth mode (month 3+): $4,500-11,000/month when CAC is proven

- Distribution strategy should always start with positioning clarification before channel selection
- The "10-10-10 daily engagement" protocol works for any client's LinkedIn/social growth
