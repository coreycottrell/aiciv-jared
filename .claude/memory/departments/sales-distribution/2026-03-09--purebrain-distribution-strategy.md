## Key Strategic Decisions

**Enterprise-first priority**: We have existing Fortune 500 relationships (P&G, Coke, Pepsi, Mars, Kroger, Target, MGM, Carnival). This is a warm activation problem, not a cold-start problem. Enterprise channel is highest-ROI first 30 days.

**Aether-as-channel**: Aether's influence IS a distribution channel for PureBrain. Not separate campaigns — one ecosystem. Every Aether post is top-of-funnel for the product.

**8 channels identified for PureBrain**:
1. Enterprise pipeline (existing relationships) — highest priority
2. Agency partnership program (20-30% referral fees)
3. Product-led growth (free trial, viral loops, Product Hunt)
4. LinkedIn-driven inbound + retargeting ads ($3K-5K/mo to start)
5. Content + SEO engine (comparison pages, use-case landing pages)
6. Community building (Discord, webinar series)
7. Automated lead gen systems (Aether builds and runs these)
8. Referral program (customers and partners)

**Revenue math**:
- $50K MRR path: 25-30 Enterprise clients at $3.5K-12K/mo gets you there alone
- Fastest path: Prioritize enterprise conversations first 30 days
- Mixed portfolio most realistic: Enterprise + Unified + Partnered + Bonded
