---

### Weekly Reality
- **Monday**: Reviews pipeline with sales team, clears inbox (127 emails from the weekend), tries to block 2 hours for strategy (succeeds maybe 1 in 4 weeks)
- **Tuesday-Wednesday**: Deep in product decisions, customer calls, investor update if it's that week
- **Thursday**: Marketing review (struggles here -- knows it matters, doesn't know what good looks like), team 1:1s
- **Friday**: Firefighting whatever broke during the week, "strategic thinking time" that always gets eaten
- **Weekend**: Catches up on everything that slipped. Feels guilty about both working and not working.

---

### What He's Judged On
- By his team: Clarity of vision, speed of decisions, whether they trust him to steer the ship
- By customers: Product quality, responsiveness when things break
- By himself: Revenue growth, whether he's building something meaningful or just running on a treadmill
- By his wife: Whether he's actually present at dinner or just physically there while mentally writing emails

### Pressures (Why He Scrolls LinkedIn)
- Feels like he's always two steps behind on AI. Competitors are talking about "AI-powered features" and he doesn't know if they're real or vaporware.
- Uses ChatGPT daily for email drafting, brainstorming, summarizing docs. But HATES that every conversation starts from zero. Has tried custom GPTs -- spent 4 hours setting one up, it was mediocre.
- Knows he should be "leveraging AI more" but doesn't have time to figure out how.
- LinkedIn is where he learns what other founders are doing (and compares himself to them).
- Worried he's making $180K/year working 65-hour weeks when he could be making more with less stress at a big company.

### What Makes Him Stop Scrolling (Highest-Leverage Hook)
> "I retrained my AI on 47 conversations with my biggest client. Now it knows our positioning, our pricing history, our objection handling, and the client's communication style better than my sales team does. Prep time for client calls went from 30 minutes to 3."

**The frame**: PureBrain isn't another tool. It's the team member who never forgets, never quits, and is always available at 11pm on Sunday.
