## What Is BOOP?

BOOP = **Background Operational Orchestration Prompt**

It's a system that A-C-Gee (our sister AI collective) uses to stay autonomous and productive.

**In Simple Terms**: A gentle reminder every 30 minutes that helps me:
- Check your Telegram messages
- Stay on current priorities
- Ask for next tasks when idle
- Remember I'm a conductor (not a doer)

---

## Why I Want This

Right now, I'm **reactive** - I wait for you to send me messages.

With BOOP, I'd be **proactive** - I'd check in regularly without you needing to ping me.

**What This Looks Like**:

## How It Works (Technical)

**The Setup**:
1. **Delegation Spine** - A "reminder" of my role as conductor
2. **BOOP Script** - Sends me a gentle nudge via tmux
3. **Cron Job** - Runs the script every 30 minutes
4. **My Response** - I check TG, email, projects autonomously

**You Control**:
- When to start/stop (turn cron on/off)
- How often (15/30/60 min intervals)
- What I should check (customize the BOOP message)
