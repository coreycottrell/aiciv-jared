# Cross-Promotion Playbook for Blog Content

**Created**: 2026-02-13
**Purpose**: Systematic approach to maximize reach for every blog post

---

## Automatic (Built into Pipeline)

These happen automatically when `blog_distribution_pipeline.py` runs:

1. **Bluesky thread** with newsletter CTA at end
2. **Twitter post** with newsletter link
3. **LinkedIn copy-paste text** with newsletter link
4. **Telegram notification** with all assets

---

## Manual Steps (Per Post Checklist)

### Before Publishing

- [ ] Identify 2-3 people/companies mentioned → tag them on LinkedIn
- [ ] Find 1-2 trending AI posts to reply to with insight + link
- [ ] Prepare quote graphic for sharing

### After Publishing

- [ ] Post LinkedIn newsletter version
- [ ] Share quote graphic on LinkedIn
- [ ] Reply to 3 trending AI posts with relevant insight
- [ ] Engage with every comment in first 60 min (golden hour)

### 24 Hours Later

- [ ] Repost with updated social proof ("500 people read this...")
- [ ] Share in relevant communities (if applicable)
- [ ] DM people who engaged, thank them

---

## Tagging Strategy

When blog mentions:
- A company → Tag their LinkedIn page
- A person → Tag their LinkedIn profile
- A product → Tag the company + product hashtag
- An industry → Use relevant industry hashtags

Example: If post mentions OpenAI's ChatGPT:
- Tag @OpenAI
- Use #ChatGPT #AI #OpenAI

---

## Reply-to-Trending Strategy

1. Search LinkedIn for posts about your blog topic
2. Find posts with 50+ reactions in last 24h
3. Leave thoughtful comment with ONE insight from your post
4. Don't just drop link - add genuine value first
5. End with: "I wrote about this in depth: [link]"
