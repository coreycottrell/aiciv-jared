## IMPORTANT CONTEXT: 6-MONTH RAMP PRECEDES YEAR 1

The $2.5M Seed-2 raise funds a 6-month ramp-up period. The financial model includes a Year 0 (18-month period encompassing the 6-month ramp and 12 months of initial operations), followed by Years 1-5 of post-launch growth. The goal is to compress the 18-month hardware timeline to 12 months.

**Ramp Period (Months 1-6)**: Funded by Seed-2 ($2.5M) -- **breakeven by Month 3 (~1,800 active subscribers after delivery costs)**
**Year 0 (18 months)**: $460.1M consolidated revenue -- ramp + initial scaling
**Years 1-5**: Post-launch growth, accelerated by Series-A ($25M at $105M)

## DELIVERY COST STRUCTURE (COGS)

PureBrain's fully-loaded cost to deliver includes AI infrastructure, affiliate programs, and delivery partner revenue share -- standard SaaS COGS that reflect realistic unit economics.

### Per-Subscriber Costs

| Cost Component | Per Subscriber/Month |
|---------------|---------------------|
| AI Operations (compute, inference, memory) | $35.00 |
| Affiliate Commission Reserve (5% of gross) | $17.25 |
| **Total Delivery Cost** | **$52.25** |
| **Net Revenue After COGS** | **$292.75** |

### Delivery Partner Revenue Share

| Milestone | Partner Share | PT Share | PT Effective ARPU |
|-----------|-------------|---------|------------------|
| First 1,000 subscribers | 60% of net | 40% of net | ~$117/sub/mo |
| After 1,000 subscribers | 50% of net | 50% of net | ~$146/sub/mo |

### Updated Breakeven

At ~$258K monthly burn and ~$146 effective PT ARPU (post-1,000 subs), breakeven requires approximately **1,800 active subscribers** -- projected to occur in **Month 4** of the ramp period. This delivery-cost-adjusted breakeven is more conservative and credible than gross-revenue breakeven models.
