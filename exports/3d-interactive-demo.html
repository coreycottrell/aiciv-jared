Glass Material Pipeline

Physically Based
Glass Rendering

MeshPhysicalMaterial with full transmission simulation. Light passes through, bends, and scatters — producing the deep optical quality of real crystal.

  • Transmission + thickness with Beer's Law light absorption
  • IOR 1.5 with chromatic dispersion per channel
  • Environment reflections via HDRI lighting probe
  • 60fps target with adaptive quality scaling
◊
MeshPhysicalMaterial
Three.js physically based rendering. Transmission=1.0, ior=1.5, roughness=0.02, thickness=0.8. HDRI environment map sampled for both reflections and refractions.
◆
Post-Processing Stack
Bloom with luminance threshold for specular highlights. Mouse parallax tilt via smooth exponential lerp at 4% per frame. Procedural glass fallback if GLB load fails.
Postprocessing + Interaction

Bloom, Depth
& Mouse Tilt

Selective bloom on high-luminance regions gives specular highlights their glass-lens quality. Move your mouse to tilt the model. Scroll this page to see transform-on-scroll.

  • Bloom threshold 0.85 — only true highlights glow
  • Mouse follow with 4% lerp — smooth, weighted response
  • Scroll progress drives Y rotation and camera Z
  • Ambient occlusion via SSAO approximation
