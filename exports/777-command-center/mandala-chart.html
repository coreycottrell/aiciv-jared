How To Use
1. Center cell — Type your ONE big goal.
2. 8 surrounding cells — The 8 qualities or areas needed to achieve it. They auto-mirror to the center of each outer 3x3.
3. 64 outer cells — Specific habits, tasks, or actions for each quality. Check them off as you complete them.
All data saves automatically to your browser. Create multiple charts for different goals.
