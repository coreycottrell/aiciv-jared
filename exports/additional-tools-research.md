## PRICING METHODOLOGY NOTES

1. All prices are in USD, monthly billing (not annual). Annual billing typically saves 15-30%.
2. Prices reflect individual or smallest business tier. Team and enterprise pricing is higher.
3. Sources verified February 2026. AI tool pricing changes frequently - verify before publishing.
4. Enterprise-only tools (Harvey AI, HireVue, Gong) noted but excluded from totals due to "Contact Sales" pricing.
5. Some tools offer generous free tiers (GitHub Copilot Individual, Amazon CodeWhisperer) but paid tiers listed for comparison.

---

## KEY MESSAGING OPPORTUNITIES FOR PUREBRAIN

1. **The stack tax**: Average small business using 5-7 AI tools pays $200-400/mo minimum before enterprise tools
2. **The switching cost**: Learning 10 different interfaces, login fatigue, integration headaches
3. **The overlap problem**: ChatGPT, Jasper, AND Writesonic - paying 3x for the same capability
4. **The expertise gap**: Most businesses pay for tools they only use at 20% capacity
5. **The integration nightmare**: Making Zapier connect HubSpot to Mailchimp to Notion to SEMrush

**PureBrain's angle**: One partnership. One interface. One relationship. Zero tool sprawl.
