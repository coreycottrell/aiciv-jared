# AI Directory Submission Plan for PureBrain.ai

**Compiled**: 2026-02-23
**Purpose**: Strategic directory submissions to increase visibility for PureBrain.ai as an AI partnership/consulting platform
**Important Note**: PureBrain.ai is an AI partnership platform (consultancy model), NOT a SaaS tool. Directory selection reflects this distinction.

---

## Executive Summary

PureBrain.ai sits at an interesting intersection: it has AI technology at its core, but delivers value through a consultancy/partnership model rather than a self-serve SaaS product. The best directory strategy uses a two-track approach:

1. **AI Tool Directories** (for discovery by tech-savvy business buyers actively searching AI solutions)
2. **B2B Service Directories** (for business decision-makers evaluating consulting/agency partners)

The directories below were selected for real traffic, free or low-cost listing options, and relevance to business buyers rather than just developers.

---

## Priority Tier 1: High-Impact, Must-Submit

### 1. There's An AI For That (TAAFT)

**URL**: https://theresanaiforthat.com/submit/

**Listing Cost**:
- Free: Monthly X (Twitter) thread giveaway - one free listing picked per month
- Paid: $347 one-time fee for priority review (processed within 1 business day)

**Traffic / Authority**:
- Global ranking improved from 13,114 to 10,254 (31.59% traffic increase as of Dec 2025)
- Estimated 2-4M monthly visitors (one of the largest AI directories)
- Domain Rating: 76+ (Ahrefs)
- Strong audience: 75% male, heaviest in 18-34 age group (tech-forward early adopters)

**Submission Process**:
- Manual review by staff
- Standard processing: 1-2 days
- Listings are permanent (no expiration)
- Accepts: AI tools, GPTs, AI influencers/services

**Special Requirements**:
- Tool/service URL required
- Description of what problem it solves
- Category selection
- Screenshot or logo

**Fit for PureBrain.ai**: HIGH. TAAFT accepts AI services broadly, not just SaaS. The "AI partnership" framing works well in their AI services/consulting category. Recommend paying the $347 for guaranteed listing given the traffic upside.

### 2. Clutch.co

**URL**: https://clutch.co/get-listed (specific AI consulting: https://clutch.co/consulting/ai)

**Listing Cost**:
- Free: Basic profile (no cost)
- Paid: Premium features (Clutch+, Sponsored placements) at varying rates

**Traffic / Authority**:
- 2.4 million monthly visitors
- Domain Authority: 89 (extremely high - one of the top B2B directories online)
- Primary audience: Business buyers evaluating service providers
- 50%+ year-over-year traffic growth

**Submission Process**:
- 3-step process, takes ~10 minutes
- Visit clutch.co/get-listed
- Create company profile with services, team size, rates, portfolio
- Verification happens through client reviews (recommend collecting 3-5 reviews minimum)
- Reviews are the primary ranking factor - start collecting immediately

**Special Requirements**:
- Legitimate registered business with official website
- 3-10 verified client reviews recommended for strong profile ranking
- Hourly rate range, team size, service focus areas
- Portfolio/case studies strongly recommended
- Client references for verified reviews

**Fit for PureBrain.ai**: VERY HIGH. Clutch is THE directory for B2B service buyers. An AI consulting firm listing here reaches decision-makers with budget. This is where Fortune 500 procurement teams search. The free basic profile is a no-brainer - start here first.

## Assets Needed Before Submitting

To submit to ALL directories above, prepare these assets once:

1. **Short description** (50-75 words): What PureBrain.ai does and who it's for
2. **Long description** (150-250 words): Full value proposition with differentiators
3. **Logo file**: High-res PNG with transparent background (512x512 minimum)
4. **Screenshots**: 3-5 screenshots showing the platform/service in action
5. **Demo video or GIF**: Optional but increases approval rate on tool directories
6. **Category tags**: AI Consulting, AI Partnership, Business AI, Enterprise AI, Custom AI Systems
7. **Key differentiator statement**: "Not another AI tool - a dedicated AI business partner that learns your company context over time"
8. **Client testimonials**: Even 1-2 quotes dramatically improve B2B directory profiles
9. **Pricing range**: General indication (helps Clutch/GoodFirms categorize correctly)
10. **Team size**: Required for most B2B service directories

---

## Positioning Language for Directories That Prefer Tools

For tool-centric directories (TAAFT, FutureTools, Futurepedia), frame PureBrain.ai this way:

> "PureBrain.ai is an AI business partner platform that builds and deploys custom AI systems for companies. Unlike AI tools you configure yourself, PureBrain.ai assigns a dedicated AI partner (Aether) that learns your business context, integrates with your workflows, and operates autonomously - giving you an AI team member rather than another software subscription."

This framing acknowledges the "platform" nature while communicating the consultancy/partnership model as a feature, not a limitation.
