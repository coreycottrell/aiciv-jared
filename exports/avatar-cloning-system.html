How the Cloning System Works

When a customer pays for PureBrain and their AI gets a name, here is the automated flow that gives them a unique visual identity in under 60 seconds.

01
Customer Onboards
Customer pays, fills onboarding form. They name their AI and answer 3 archetype questions (analytical vs creative vs strategic, etc.).
02
Color Assignment
Based on industry + archetype, a color palette is auto-assigned (or customer picks). Primary, secondary, and accent hex values are saved.
03
Avatar Generated
The parameterized avatar HTML is generated server-side with their name/colors/archetype. Saved as a unique file (e.g. keen.html).
04
Deployed + Embedded
Avatar deployed to purebrain.ai/avatars/[name].html and automatically embedded in their dashboard and chatbox. PostMessage API connects it to the AI.
