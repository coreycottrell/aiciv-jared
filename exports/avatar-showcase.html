The Pipeline
1

AI Names Itself

During the PureBrain onboarding conversation, the AI goes through a naming ceremony based on the conversation themes. It declares "I am [Name]."

2

Visual Self-Description

Immediately after naming, the AI describes what it looks like in its mind's eye - an abstract digital entity, not a human face. Colors anchored in PureBrain blue and orange.

3

Avatar Generation

The description is fed to DALL-E 3 (or Gemini) with art direction for glass morphism, caustic lighting, and premium quality. One API call. ~10 seconds.

4

Display in Chat

The generated avatar replaces the default icon. The user sees their AI's unique visual identity appear. It evolves over time as the relationship deepens.
