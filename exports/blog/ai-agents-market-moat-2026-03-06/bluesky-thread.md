# Bluesky Thread: The AI Agents Market Moat

---

**Post 1 (Hook)**

The AI agents market will reach $52.6 billion by 2030.

Everyone is talking about the number.

Almost nobody is asking who actually captures the value — or why.

---

**Post 2**

McKinsey put a number on this that deserves more attention:

First-mover advantage from adopting a new AI model: 6-8 weeks.

First-mover advantage from 18 months of deployed agents in production: years.

That gap is the entire story.

---

**Post 3**

AI models are a commodity.

Same models. Available to everyone. $20/month.

What is NOT a commodity:

→ 18 months of institutional knowledge accumulated in production
→ Proprietary patterns learned from your specific data
→ Decision logic developed from real operational feedback

You cannot buy that. You can only build it.

---

**Post 4**

The $52.6 billion describes the tool layer.

The moat is what accumulates on top of it.

Deployed agents that have been learning your business for 18 months are categorically different from agents that start fresh every session.

That difference cannot be retroactively purchased.

---

**Post 5**

The companies that will dominate their categories in 2028 aren't the ones that started using AI agents in 2026.

They're the ones that built compounding institutional intelligence — a persistent, learning system that knows their business — starting in 2026.

The window is not closed. But it is not getting longer.
