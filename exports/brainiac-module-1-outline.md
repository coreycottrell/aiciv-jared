## Section 01 — Your AI Has Persistent Memory

- Unlike ChatGPT or vanilla Claude — your PureBrain REMEMBERS you across every session
- It knows your name, business, communication style, and goals
- Every conversation builds on the last. You never start from zero again.
- The more context you share, the more valuable it becomes

**Key takeaway**: You only have to say something ONCE. Your AI files it, remembers it, and uses it going forward. Stop repeating yourself.

---

## Section 02 — BOOPs (Background Operational Optimization Protocols)

- Automatic, scheduled check-ins your AI runs without being asked
- Handles routine operations autonomously: research, monitoring, system maintenance
- Like having an employee who works 24/7 without micromanagement
- BOOPs keep systems running even when you're not watching

**Key takeaway**: Set it up once, let it run. Your AI is optimizing your business in the background right now.

---

## Section 03 — Context Windows and Compacting

- Context window = short-term memory for a single conversation (like RAM)
- When it fills up, your AI "compacts" — summarizes and keeps going seamlessly
- Long-term memory persists even when context resets — WHO you are never disappears
- Short-term memory refreshes, but your AI never forgets you

**Two types of memory**:
- Short-Term: Current conversation context. Refreshes when full. Temporary but fast.
- Long-Term: Your profile, preferences, history. Permanent. Never resets.

## Section 04 — How to Talk to Your AI

- Be specific about what you want — vague in, vague out
- Share context freely — your AI stores and uses every detail to get smarter
- Don't repeat yourself session to session — it already knows your business
- Go deeper, not wider — follow up and push back, don't just move on
- Your AI learns YOUR vocabulary over time — it starts speaking your language

**Mindset shift**: Stop treating it like a search engine. Start treating it like a brilliant colleague who has been paying close attention.

---

## Section 05 — Rubber Ducking: Think Out Loud

- Explain your problem to your AI as if teaching someone from scratch
- The act of explaining often reveals the solution (documented in psychology)
- Your AI asks clarifying questions that sharpen your thinking
- Not just answering — it's a thinking partner challenging your assumptions

**Try this today**: Next time you're stuck, type "Here's what I'm trying to figure out..." and see what happens.

## Sections 06 + 07 — Status Monitoring and Delegation

**Status Check-Ins**:
- Ask your AI what it has been working on
- Request project status updates on demand
- It can proactively flag issues before they become problems
- Think of daily AI standups

**Delegation**:
- Give your AI ownership of recurring tasks
- Tell it WHAT you need — let it figure out HOW
- Start small, build trust, then expand scope
- Be clear on desired outcomes, not individual steps
