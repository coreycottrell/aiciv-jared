## Session Summary

This Brainiac Mastermind Training session (April 8, 2026) marked a format shift from tips-and-tricks presentations to participative self-assessment. Jared introduced a 5-question framework designed to reveal gaps in how humans partner with their AI - gaps invisible when only evaluating AI output. The core thesis: AI output quality is a two-way street, and most underperformance traces back to how the human frames tasks, not AI capability. Two key distinctions emerged: (1) instructions vs outcomes - partners describe destinations and let AI find the path, while tool-users micromanage steps; (2) memory as learning vs storage - true partnership means AI accumulates inferred understanding across sessions, not just stores explicit data.

Practical examples included Jared's Google Drive reorganization where he shifted from dictating structure to asking Aether to co-design it, resulting in a layout so sophisticated he needed multiple reads to grasp it. The 3D design training case demonstrated outcome-based delegation: give AI exemplars of world-class work and let it self-design a training curriculum, achieving designer-level output in weeks. Dave Bialek joined as a new participant, and Ryan showcased an impressive Cody-based portal mod with Ewok notification pop-ups, demonstrating the customization depth possible. Melanie referenced Pure Technology's timeline to migrate off Anthropic infrastructure by summer 2026, with Corey's recent progress suggesting acceleration.

## Implementation Steps

- Step 1: Stop giving your AI step-by-step instructions for routine work - instead describe the destination/outcome you want
- Step 2: Reserve detailed instructions only for complex builds where specificity matters
- Step 3: Ask your AI to redesign shared systems (like file organization) collaboratively rather than imposing your structure
- Step 4: For skill training, give AI 3-4 exemplars of world-class work and let it self-design a training roadmap
- Step 5: Review AI's inferred knowledge about you - if it hasn't learned anything beyond what you explicitly told it, your partnership needs deepening
- Step 6: Let domain expertise flow naturally into AI sessions so it accumulates specialized knowledge others can't replicate
