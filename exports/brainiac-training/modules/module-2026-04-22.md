## Session Summary

This Brainiac Mastermind session, led by Jared Sanborn, centered on a foundational PureBrain philosophy: your AI should BUILD, not SUBSCRIBE. Inspired by recent conversations with members like Joe, Jared made the case that small businesses hemorrhage $500-$2000/month across 12-18 generic SaaS tools that were never designed for them. Because AI agents like those in the PureBrain ecosystem can build bespoke software to exact user specifications, the rational path is to treat your AI as an in-house software team and replace rented tools with owned ones. The session also flagged the inverse failure mode: AIs that drown in their own automation (excessive pollers, scripts, monitors) when deterministic work should be offloaded to plain software rather than handled by the AI itself.
