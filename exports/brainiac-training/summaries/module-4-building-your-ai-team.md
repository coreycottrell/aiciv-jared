## Actionable Techniques

### Technique 1: Voice-First Workflow (Ryan's Pattern)
- Build a voice recording interface (app or Telegram)
- Use Whisper for auto-transcription and categorization
- Auto-route to: tasks, ideas, reminders
- Create a morning briefing dashboard
- Leave overnight instructions, review deliverables in AM

### Technique 2: AI Lead Generation (DJ's Pattern)
- Multi-pass web scraping (4-5 rounds per lead)
- AI-generated call prep with personalized opening lines
- Objection prediction and difficulty scoring
- Mobile map integration for field sales
- Lead scoring and residual calculations

### Technique 3: Self-Populating CRM (Jay's Pattern)
- Connect AI note-taker to all calls
- Monitor email threads automatically
- Auto-populate CRM fields with human error-correction layer
- Eliminates the "get salespeople to fill out Salesforce" problem

### Technique 4: Rapid Client Deliverables (Ryan's Pattern)
- 17-minute website build for client meetings
- Upload existing proposals as context
- AI generates complete branded deliverable
- Use for last-minute pitch preparation
