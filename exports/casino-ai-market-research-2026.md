### Market Context

- Source: [Business Wire Online Poker 2025](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20250602812357/en/Online-Poker-Global-Strategic-Business-Report-2025-Market-to-Reach-$11.4-Billion-by-2030)

### The Bot and RTA Problem

**Real-Time Assistance (RTA)**: Software that feeds optimal decisions to players during live hands using solver outputs — effectively makes users unbeatable. Banned by all major platforms, but detection is the arms race.

**Scope of Problem**:
- Bots process thousands of potential outcomes in milliseconds
- RTA tools using solver outputs can make players statistically unbeatable against human competition
- Detection systems must differentiate human decision rhythms (inconsistent, emotional) from algorithmic precision (fast, optimal)
- Online gambling faces 3-5x higher chargeback rates than other sectors

**Detection Methods in Use**:
- Behavioral pattern analysis: decision speed, reading accuracy of betting patterns
- Long-term win rate analysis: rates exceeding human capability thresholds
- Click sequence and timing biometrics: human timing is irregular; bots are not
- Cross-referencing hand histories against solver outputs ("Fair Play Check" — PokerStars + GTO Wizard)

### Major Platform Investment

**PokerStars**:
- 50-person Game Integrity Team running alongside automated detection systems
- Machine learning systems monitoring gameplay continuously, adapting to new cheating methods
- **95% proactive detection rate** before other players report suspicious activity
- Source: [PokerStars Anti-Cheating 2025](https://www.deucescracked.com/pokerstars-introduces-revolutionary-anti-cheating-measures-for-2025/)

**GGPoker**:
- Banned 40+ accounts in a single enforcement action in 2020
- Confiscated $1.2 million in detected cheating proceeds
- "Official war" on RTA and solver abuse
- AI detection and behavior tracking deployed continuously
- Source: [AI in Poker 2025](https://cc-poker.com/en/blog/ai-in-poker)

### Commercial Implication
Poker bot and RTA detection is an arms race with direct revenue stakes. Platforms that cannot maintain game integrity lose player trust and player volume. The B2B market for third-party integrity tools is emerging as a discrete revenue category for technology providers.
