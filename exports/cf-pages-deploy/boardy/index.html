The Fundamental Difference

Boardy is a transaction machine. It calls you, qualifies you, introduces you, and moves on. Each interaction is complete in itself. The AI does not know you any better after 10 introductions than it did after the first call. The product does not deepen.

PureBrain is a relationship. Every conversation adds context. Every decision you work through together teaches the AI more about how you think, what you care about, and what you’re building. The product compounds. Session 100 is meaningfully more useful than session 1 — because the AI actually knows you.

This distinction is not a feature difference. It is a fundamental product philosophy difference. Boardy is built around the premise that professionals need better introductions. PureBrain is built around the premise that professionals need an AI that works for them as a thinking partner — one that grows with them over time and never forgets.

Real Scenarios

Which One Do You Actually Need?

The right choice depends entirely on what problem you’re trying to solve.

Use Boardy when…

You need a warm intro to a VC

You’re a founder actively fundraising and you want to get in front of investors you haven’t met yet. Boardy is the right tool. It can connect you with investors in its ecosystem through a process that both parties opt into.

Use PureBrain when…

You need to prep for that VC meeting

Before the meeting Boardy arranged, you need to think through your pitch, sharpen your financials story, and prepare answers to hard questions. PureBrain is the right tool. It knows your business inside and out.

Use Boardy when…

You want to expand your startup network

You’ve just joined an accelerator or VC ecosystem and you want to quickly meet other founders, scouts, and potential customers. Boardy’s community model solves this.

Use PureBrain when…

You want an AI that actually knows your business

You’re making daily decisions — pricing, hiring, strategy, partnerships — and you want an AI that remembers what you decided last week and why. PureBrain is built for this. Boardy doesn’t remember you at all.

The honest answer…

Some founders use both

Boardy connects you with people. PureBrain helps you become the kind of professional those people want to know. They serve different needs in the same person’s professional life — and they don’t compete with each other.

The critical point…

Most professionals don’t need Boardy

If you’re not in the startup-VC-accelerator ecosystem, Boardy has no value for you. If you’re a professional outside that world — an agency owner, consultant, service business — PureBrain serves you. Boardy does not.
