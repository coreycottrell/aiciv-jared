5 Power Prompts

Leave with prompts that actually work

You will leave the workshop with these fully customized for your business — not generic templates, but prompts tuned to your context, your team, your voice.

Power Prompt 01
The Process Extractor
Pulls structured SOPs out of your head using guided conversation. Turns tacit knowledge into repeatable agent instructions.
"I'm going to describe how I [process]. Ask me clarifying questions until you can write a complete step-by-step SOP I could give to a new employee or an AI agent..."
Power Prompt 02
The Junior Expert Brief
Gives your AI the right context to act as a knowledgeable specialist in your domain — not a generic assistant, but someone who knows your industry.
"You are a senior [role] at [company type]. You know [domain] deeply. You default to [communication style]. Your job is to help me with [task]..."
Power Prompt 03
The Ping Pong Loop
Structures iterative collaboration so each round gets better. Replaces the frustrating "this isn't what I wanted" cycle with a clear improvement path.
"Give me your best attempt, then tell me the 3 assumptions you made. I'll correct any that are wrong, then you revise..."
Power Prompt 04
The Trust Gate
Before deploying any agent action, this prompt structure makes the AI show its work, flag uncertainty, and request confirmation on high-stakes steps.
"Before taking any action, state: what you're about to do, why, what could go wrong, and whether you need my confirmation..."
Power Prompt 05
The Delegation Brief
The structure for assigning multi-step tasks to an agent team. Clear scope, success criteria, constraints, and escalation rules — in one prompt.
"Task: [what]. Success looks like: [criteria]. Constraints: [limits]. Escalate to me if: [triggers]. Deliver: [output format]..."

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to be technical to attend? +
No. This workshop is designed for business owners, operators, and leaders — not engineers. If you can use a computer and have a business process to extract, you're qualified. The API Foundations module (Module E) is optional for those who want to go deeper technically.
What AI tools do I need to have before the workshop? +
We recommend having a Claude Pro or Claude Team account ($20–$25/month). We'll use Claude throughout the workshop. You can sign up at claude.ai before attending. If you already use ChatGPT or another AI, the principles apply — but our exercises are optimized for Claude.
Is this a recorded course or a live workshop? +
It's live — with a recording provided afterward. The live format matters because Process Extraction requires real-time feedback and iteration. You can't fully extract a process alone; you need a facilitator pushing back and refining with you. The recording is included so you can revisit the material.
What's the difference between this and the Brainiac Mastermind Training? +
Brainiac Mastermind Training is the ongoing members' library — modules, case studies, and advanced techniques for active PureBrain members. This workshop is a standalone intensive — one session, focused on getting you from zero to one working deployment in a single day. The workshop is also available to non-members.
Can I apply the workshop cost toward a PureBrain membership? +
Yes. If you join PureBrain within 30 days of attending the workshop, your $200 individual fee or a proportional credit from the team fee will be applied toward your first month. Email jared@puretechnology.nyc after attending to claim this.
What if I've already been using AI for a while? +
Good — you'll get more out of it. Experienced AI users consistently tell us the process extraction and agent deployment sections unlock things they couldn't figure out on their own. The gap between "uses AI a lot" and "runs AI systems" is bigger than most people expect. This workshop closes that gap.
