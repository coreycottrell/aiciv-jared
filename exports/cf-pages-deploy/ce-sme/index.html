Frequently Asked Questions

What is TIE?
TIE stands for The Intelligence Engine. It's the AI backbone of CE SME that learns your business over time — your writing style, your rate card, your client preferences, your industry norms. Every module is powered by TIE, which means cross-module intelligence comes standard.
Is my data secure?
Yes. CE SME runs on Cloudflare infrastructure with data isolated per business. Your data is never shared between accounts, never used to train AI models, and stored in compliance with PIPEDA privacy standards. All connections are encrypted end-to-end.
Can I use this outside Canada?
Yes. CE SME works globally, but Canadian business context (GST/HST, CRA compliance, PIPEDA, provincial regulations) is the default intelligence layer. If you operate internationally, TIE adapts while keeping Canadian compliance as the foundation.
What integrations are available?
QuickBooks, Wave, and FreshBooks integrations are on the roadmap. Currently, CE SME is a standalone platform with import/export capabilities. The billing module generates standard invoice formats compatible with major accounting software.
Can my team access it?
Multi-user team access is coming in v2. Currently, each account supports a single user. Team collaboration features including role-based permissions and shared dashboards are actively in development.
How is this different from HoneyBook or Dubsado?
CE SME is AI-native, not AI-bolted-on. While tools like HoneyBook focus on one domain (proposals/CRM), CE SME covers proposals, operations, projects, billing, HR, and content in a single platform. The key difference is cross-module intelligence — TIE connects everything so a won proposal automatically creates a project, which feeds billing when complete. No other tool does this.
