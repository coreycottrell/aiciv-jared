Where We're Honest

The Gaps We Acknowledge

Honest intelligence means naming what we don't have — not just what we do. These are real gaps, not spin.

📈
Revenue Gap
Sierra at $165M ARR vs PureBrain at $3.2K MRR. This is not a product gap — it is a commercial execution gap. The pipeline is not built at scale yet. The product depth exists; the sales motion does not.
🧠
Letta's Memory Architecture
Letta's agent self-editing memory (sleep-time compute, 3-tier architecture) is technically ahead of where PureBrain's internal memory sits today. This is a real technical gap, not a positioning gap. It is on the roadmap.
🌎
Distribution Gap
Microsoft's 1B Office users and Salesforce's 18,500 enterprise relationships represent distribution advantages that take years to build. Brand recognition in enterprise sales is compounding — they have years of compounding we don't.
⚡
Where the Gap Closes Fast
Product depth is already differentiated. The AiCIV protocol, constitutional identity, and overnight ops track record are built — not roadmap. Commercial execution is the accelerator needed, and it is being built now.
