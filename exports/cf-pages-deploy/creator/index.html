Questions

Everything you want to know

How is this different from ChatGPT? ▼

ChatGPT writes generic content in a generic voice. PureBrain learns your specific vocabulary, sentence structure, CTA style, and tone from your actual past content. The output sounds like you wrote it — not like a robot trying to sound like you.

Can my AI handle customer support? ▼

Yes. Upload your FAQs, pricing pages, methodology docs, and testimonials to your Knowledge Base. Your fan chat page will answer questions using those files. If it doesn't know something, it tells fans to reach out directly — it never makes things up.

What platforms does it support? ▼

PureBrain generates content for LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter/X, and Bluesky — all in one click. Each platform gets its own optimized format (thread structure for Twitter, hashtag patterns for Instagram, etc.) while keeping your voice consistent across all of them.

How does the voice matching work? ▼

Import your past posts or paste examples of your writing. PureBrain analyzes your average sentence length, vocabulary complexity, emoji usage, CTA patterns, and signature phrases. It builds a voice fingerprint that gets more accurate every time you generate content.

Is my data secure? ▼

Your content lives in your isolated database. We never use your writing to train models for other creators. Your knowledge base, content history, and fan conversations are yours — always. You can delete everything at any time.
