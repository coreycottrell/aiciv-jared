What's Changing

Change 01

Tech Daily Gets Tighter

Reduced from 15-20 min to a strict 5-10 min standup. Ahsen and Alex co-lead to keep it focused on blockers only.

15-20 min→5-10 min
Who
Jared Sanborn, Ahsen Awan (co-lead), Alex Seant (co-lead), Corey Cottrell, Waqas Nasir, Shahbaz Ali, Muhammed Zafeer, Russell Korus • Optional: Melanie Salvador
Change 02

Deep Dive Commercial Becomes All-Hands

Friday's commercial deep dive expands into a full Weekly Tactical All-Hands. Lightning rounds from every team, metrics review, blockers.

Deep Dive Commercial→Weekly Tactical All-Hands
Who
Jared Sanborn, Melanie Salvador, Corey Cottrell, Ahsen Awan, Alex Seant, Chy, Aether, Nathan, Waqas Nasir, Shahbaz Ali, Muhammed Zafeer, Russell Korus, Metis, Rimah, Logie • Full team
Change 03

New: Product / Customer Meeting

Bi-weekly Tuesday session led by Aether. Focuses on customer feedback, product roadmap alignment, and feature prioritization.

(none)→Tue 9am, bi-weekly
Who
Aether (lead), Jared Sanborn, Ahsen Awan, Corey Cottrell, Melanie Salvador, Chy • Optional: Nathan, Alex Seant
Change 04

Commercial Daily Becomes Pipeline Review

Renamed and refocused. Melanie, Chy, and Nathan run a tight 15-min pipeline review instead of a broad commercial sync.

Commercial (broad)→Pipeline Review (15 min)
Who
Melanie Salvador (lead), Chy, Nathan • Optional: Jared Sanborn
Change 05

Governance Stays, Gets Defined

Wednesday governance meeting remains but with clear ownership: Chy, Metis, Rimah, and Logie. 45-minute time box.

Who
Chy (lead), Metis, Rimah, Logie • Optional: Jared Sanborn, Corey Cottrell
Change 06

Leadership Meeting Formalized

Monday 11am locked at 60 minutes. Jared plus full leadership team. Strategic decisions and cross-team alignment.

Who
Jared Sanborn (lead), Melanie Salvador, Corey Cottrell, Ahsen Awan, Chy, Aether, Russell Korus • Optional: Alex Seant, Nathan
Change 07

Triangle Sync Goes Async

Jared + Chy + Aether triangle coordination becomes async-first via portal and Telegram. No standing meeting — sync only when needed for strategic decisions.

Who
Jared Sanborn, Chy, Aether • Async via portal/Telegram
Change 08

Monthly Strategic & Quarterly Off-Site

Monthly strategic deep dives (2-4 hrs, one topic) and quarterly off-sites continue unchanged. Clear ownership and pre-read requirements added.

Who (Monthly)
Jared Sanborn, Melanie Salvador, Corey Cottrell, Ahsen Awan, Chy, Aether, Russell Korus, Alex Seant

Net Impact Summary

  • Tech dailies cut by ~50% in duration (10 min saved per day)
  • New Product/Customer bi-weekly adds strategic customer focus
  • Friday becomes the anchor all-hands (single weekly sync point)
  • Pipeline review replaces broad commercial sync (sharper focus)
  • Every meeting has a named owner and time box
  • Net weekly meeting time decreases despite adding one new meeting
  • Governance and Leadership meetings get explicit attendee definitions
  • Jared's calendar protected: fewer, shorter, more impactful meetings
