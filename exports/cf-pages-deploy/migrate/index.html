🟢 How to Export from ChatGPT

  1. Open ChatGPT and click your profile icon
  2. Go to Settings > Data Controls
  3. Click "Export data" -- ChatGPT will email you a link
  4. Download the .zip file from the email
  5. You'll upload it in the next step
ℹ️

If your current AI won't let you export or refuses to give you your data, you can skip this step and proceed to migrate without it. Your data is yours -- but not every platform makes it easy.

🟠 How to Export from Claude

🔵 How to Export from Gemini

  1. Go to takeout.google.com
  2. Click "Deselect all", then find and select "Gemini Apps"
  3. Click "Next step" > "Create export"
  4. Download the .zip file when ready (Google emails you)
  5. You'll upload it in the next step

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is my data safe? ▼

    All processing happens in your browser. Your files never leave your device. We only store the extracted summary (topics, preferences, communication style) -- never your raw conversations.

    How long does it take? ▼

    About 60 seconds. Most of that is the initial upload. The analysis itself is nearly instant since it all runs locally in your browser.

    What if I use multiple AIs? ▼

    Import from each one. PureBrain combines all your context into a single unified profile. The more history you import, the better your AI partner knows you from day one.

    Can I undo this? ▼

    Your imported context can be modified or deleted anytime in your PureBrain settings. You have full control over what your AI partner remembers.

    What formats are supported? ▼

    ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Copilot ZIP exports are auto-detected. For Perplexity, Meta AI, and other platforms, you can paste conversations directly or use the Quick Memory Transfer method.

    Do I need to be a PureBrain customer? ▼

    You can start with a free trial. The migration tool works for everyone -- import your data, see your AI profile, and decide if PureBrain is right for you.
