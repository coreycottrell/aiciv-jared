Understanding the Products

What Are We Actually Comparing?

These are fundamentally different categories of product. Understanding the difference helps you pick the right one.

OpenClaw

Open-Source Self-Hosted AI Gateway

OpenClaw is a free, MIT-licensed software framework you install on your own machine or a VPS. It connects AI models (Claude, GPT, Gemini, or local) to messaging apps you already use — WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, iMessage.

You bring your own AI API keys, your own server, your own configuration. OpenClaw is the plumbing. You assemble the rest.

Built by Peter Steinberger (founder of PSPDFKit). As of February 2026, Steinberger has joined OpenAI and OpenClaw is transitioning to an independent open-source foundation.

PureBrain

Managed AI Partnership With Persistent Memory

PureBrain is a fully managed AI partner that knows you, your work, and your preferences — getting smarter with every interaction. No setup. No infrastructure. No API key management.

Where OpenClaw provides a framework, PureBrain provides a relationship. Your AI partner remembers your projects, your communication style, your clients, your goals — and carries that context forward permanently.

Built for business professionals, executives, and knowledge workers who want AI that works for them — not a server they have to manage.

Recent Development: OpenClaw’s Founder Has Left for OpenAI

On February 14, 2026, Peter Steinberger announced he is joining OpenAI to work on “bringing agents to everyone.” He stated: “I’m a builder at heart. What I want is to change the world, not build a large company.” OpenClaw will be transferred to a foundation structure — open source, community-governed, sponsored by OpenAI. This introduces significant uncertainty about development roadmap, support continuity, and product direction for OpenClaw users. Foundation transitions for one-person open-source projects frequently see momentum stalls during the governance setup period.

Setup: What It Actually Takes

OpenClaw requires technical work before you can use it at all. PureBrain requires none.

OpenClaw Setup (Via Hostinger VPS)

  • 1
    Purchase a Hostinger VPS plan (~$9–15/month)
  • 2
    Sign up for OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google API access and obtain API keys
  • 3
    Install Node.js 22+ on the VPS via SSH
  • 4
    Run npm install and openclaw onboard commands
  • 5
    Configure channels login (WhatsApp, Telegram, etc.)
  • 6
    Set up daemon to keep OpenClaw running persistently
  • 7
    Configure openclaw.json for your preferred model and settings
  • 8
    Test, debug, and troubleshoot connection issues
  • 9
    Monitor and maintain VPS uptime, security patches, logs
Estimated time: 2–8 hours. Ongoing maintenance required. Technical knowledge required.
