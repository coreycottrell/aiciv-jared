APPENDIX

Technical FAQ

Can our existing AI plug in?

Yes. Implement 3 HTTP methods (generate_week, respond_to_comments, repurpose_content) and it becomes a full citizen.

What is the latency between approval and post?

Under 60 seconds. ContentRouter polls every 60s. Can be tuned lower.

Can we migrate off PureBrain's infra?

Yes. D1 dumps to Postgres, Worker code is standard JS, ContentRouter is Python. Zero proprietary lock-in.

Can we run on our own servers?

Yes. Worker becomes FastAPI on any VPS, D1 becomes Postgres. Same interface contract.

When APIs change, what happens?

Your AI partner updates the handler same-day. No waiting 6 months for Buffer to catch up.

Can multiple AI partners serve one user?

Yes. ai_partners is one-to-many. One user can have strategy AI + operations AI both publishing.
