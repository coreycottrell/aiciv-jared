4. Conversation Data and AI Memory

This section deserves special attention because AI Brain is unique in how it uses data.

4.1 Why We Store Conversations

PureBrain.ai's core value proposition is persistent memory — your AI Brain learns about you over time and retains context across sessions. This requires us to store your conversation history and extracted memory/context data on our servers.

What we store:

  • Full conversation history (your messages and AI responses)
  • Extracted context and preferences (things your AI Brain has "learned" about you)
  • Summaries and structured memory data

4.2 How Conversation Data is Used

To power your AI Brain: Your conversation data is used exclusively to generate responses and maintain continuity for your AI Brain. Your data is not used to train AI models for other users.

For anonymized service improvement (opt-out available): We may use anonymized, aggregated patterns from conversations to improve response quality and system performance. This data is stripped of personally identifiable information before analysis.

You can opt out of service improvement data use at any time by emailing legal@puretechnology.nyc.

4.3 Conversation Data and Third-Party AI Models

Your conversation data is transmitted to Anthropic's Claude API to generate AI responses. Anthropic processes this data subject to their API usage policies and privacy terms. We do not permit Anthropic to use your data to train their foundation models without your consent. Review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/legal.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers uses TLS/HTTPS encryption
  • Encryption at rest: Sensitive data is encrypted at rest in our storage systems
  • Access controls: Access to personal data is restricted to personnel who need it to perform their role
  • Cloudflare security: We use Cloudflare for DDoS protection, web application firewall, and security monitoring
  • Subprocessor agreements: Third-party providers are contractually required to maintain appropriate security standards

No security system is perfect. If we become aware of a data breach that materially affects your personal data, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law.

To report a security vulnerability, email legal@puretechnology.nyc.

9. Your Privacy Rights

9.1 Rights for All Users

Regardless of where you are located, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Data Portability: Request your conversation history and AI memory data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact legal@puretechnology.nyc. We will respond within 30 days.

9.2 California Residents — CCPA Rights

If you are a California resident, the CCPA/CPRA provides you with specific rights including the right to know, delete, correct, opt out of sale/sharing, limit sensitive personal information use, and non-discrimination. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise CCPA rights, email legal@puretechnology.nyc with the subject line "CCPA Rights Request." We will respond within 45 days.

Categories of personal information collected: Identifiers (name, email, IP address), commercial information (subscription, payment records), internet activity (usage data, session information), professional information (business name, role if provided), and AI-generated inferences (preferences and context derived from conversations).

9.3 EU/EEA and UK Residents — GDPR Rights

If you are located in the EU, EEA, or UK, you have rights under GDPR or equivalent UK law, including rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, and the right to object. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection supervisory authority.

International Data Transfers: If you are located in the EU/UK, your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or other appropriate safeguards for such transfers where required.

Note: Pure Technology Inc. recommends engaging a Data Protection Officer (DPO) or EU Representative before actively serving EU customers.
