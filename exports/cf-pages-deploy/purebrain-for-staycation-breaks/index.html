Section 01

Executive Summary

Staycation Breaks operates five premium lodge parks across England — Woodlakes Retreat (Norfolk), Sherwood Retreat (Nottingham), Black Bull Retreat (Barmston), Applegrove (Scarborough), and Hideaway Retreat (Lincoln). The opportunity is clear: AI agents that work around the clock to find the right customers, capture them into a proprietary database, book them directly, and keep them coming back — all without adding headcount. This is the blueprint to make that happen.

🎯

The Core Problem

Holiday parks currently rely on third-party booking platforms (Booking.com, Airbnb, holiday aggregators) that take 15–25% commission, own the customer relationship, and block direct re-marketing. Every booking through an intermediary is a customer you do not own.

⚡

The PureBrain Solution

AI agents that find your ideal holiday-maker, bring them into your own booking platform, store them in your proprietary CRM, process payment directly, and then automate the entire lifecycle — offers, newsletters, upsells, and loyalty — so every customer you acquire compounds in value over time.

📈

The Commercial Outcome

Zero commission on direct bookings. A growing, owned list of holiday-makers who book again and again. A marketing engine that runs 24/7 without a marketing team. Commercialisation of every park through AI — not through hiring.

Agent Architecture

12 AI agents working in concert — each with a defined role, running 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

LAYER 1 — CUSTOMER ACQUISITION AGENTS

AGENT 01 🔍

Prospect Hunter

Continuously scans social media, travel forums, Reddit communities, and holiday groups for people actively planning UK staycations.

Identifies high-intent staycation searchers
Monitors keywords: "UK lodge break", "hot tub holiday", "staycation Norfolk", etc.
Feeds qualified leads into the CRM pipeline
AGENT 02 🎣

Lead Magnet Agent

Creates and deploys targeted content offers — "UK's Best Lodge Breaks" guides, seasonal deal lists, hidden gem retreats — to capture email addresses.

Generates park-specific lead magnets monthly
A/B tests offer formats and landing pages
Grows the email list autonomously
AGENT 03 📢

Social Presence Agent

Maintains active, engaging social media presence across Facebook and Instagram for all five parks — posting content, responding to comments, and building community.

Posts 5–7x per week per park social account
Responds to enquiries within minutes
Runs engagement campaigns during peak booking windows
AGENT 04 🤝

Partnership Outreach Agent

Identifies and initiates contact with complementary businesses — travel bloggers, staycation influencers, family activity brands — for referral partnerships.

Builds a pipeline of referral partners
Drafts and sends partnership proposals
Tracks referral bookings per partner

LAYER 2 — DATABASE, BOOKING & PAYMENT AGENTS

AGENT 05 🗄️

CRM Intelligence Agent

Builds, enriches, and maintains the proprietary holiday-maker database — tracking every customer's park preferences, booking history, family composition, and lifetime value.

Scores every lead (hot/warm/cold) automatically
Enriches profiles with behavioural data
Flags high-value customers for VIP treatment
AGENT 06 📅

Booking Concierge Agent

Handles inbound booking enquiries via chat, email, and web form — qualifying, answering questions, and guiding customers through the direct booking flow 24/7.

Responds to enquiries in under 60 seconds
Recommends the right park based on customer needs
Reduces enquiry-to-booking friction to near zero
AGENT 07 💳

Revenue Optimisation Agent

Monitors occupancy across all five parks in real time and dynamically manages pricing, availability windows, and last-minute deal creation to maximise revenue per available lodge.

Dynamic pricing by park, season, and demand
Auto-creates last-minute offers for gaps in calendar
Forecasts revenue 90 days forward
AGENT 08 🔔

Post-Booking Agent

Takes over immediately after a booking is confirmed — sending pre-arrival information, upselling add-ons (welcome packages, activity bookings), and ensuring every guest arrives excited.

Automated pre-arrival email sequence
Upsell relevant add-ons per park and guest type
Collects post-stay reviews automatically

LAYER 3 — RETENTION & COMMERCIAL INTELLIGENCE AGENTS

AGENT 09 📧

Newsletter Architect

Plans, writes, and sends the Staycation Breaks newsletter — monthly for the general list, weekly for hot leads, and seasonal for returning customers. Every send is personalised by interest.

Generates newsletter content from park activity
Segments sends by customer type and park preference
A/B tests subject lines and send times
AGENT 10 🔁

Rebooking Agent

Systematically identifies past guests who are overdue for their next trip and sends perfectly-timed re-engagement campaigns based on their original booking patterns.

Identifies re-booking windows per customer

Converts past guests before they book with a competitor
AGENT 11 ⭐

Loyalty & VIP Agent

Manages a formal loyalty programme for frequent guests — tracking stays, awarding benefits, and making top customers feel genuinely valued with personalised touches.

Tracks stays across all five parks per guest

Drives word-of-mouth through referral rewards

Commercial Intelligence Agent

The brain of the operation — aggregating data from all 11 agents, all five parks, and all booking channels into a single executive dashboard showing exactly where the business stands and where to focus next.

Weekly commercial performance reports to Graham

Section 04

Own Your Booking Platform

A fully custom, direct-to-customer booking and payment system — owned by Staycation Breaks, branded by Staycation Breaks, profitable for Staycation Breaks.

🏗️

Direct Booking Engine

A branded booking portal at staycation-breaks.com where customers can search by date, location, party size, and preferences — and complete a full booking with payment in under 3 minutes.

  • Real-time availability calendar across all five parks
  • Multi-lodge comparison tool ("compare Woodlakes vs Sherwood")
  • Park-specific landing pages with rich photography and virtual tours
  • Mobile-first design — most holiday bookings happen on mobile
  • Instant booking confirmation with automated email receipts
💳

Integrated Payment Processing

A fully integrated payment system that keeps 100% of the transaction value inside Staycation Breaks — with deposits, instalments, and full-payment options to reduce booking friction.

  • Stripe or similar: card, Apple Pay, Google Pay support
  • Deposit + balance payment split (e.g. 25% now, balance 8 weeks before)
  • Automated payment reminders and failed-payment recovery
  • Refund and cancellation policy automation
  • Full revenue reporting per park, per period
🗄️

Proprietary Customer Database

Every person who enquires, books, or even just browses with intent enters the Staycation Breaks database — tagged, scored, and ready to be re-marketed to directly, forever.

  • Complete customer profile: name, contact, party size, past stays, preferences
  • Booking history across all five parks per customer
  • Lead scoring (likelihood to rebook, lifetime value)
  • GDPR-compliant consent management built in
  • Exportable, portable — you own the data entirely
📊

Operations Dashboard

A single command centre giving Graham and his team complete visibility across all five parks — occupancy, revenue, upcoming arrivals, cancellations, and commercial pipeline.

  • Live occupancy view: all 5 parks on one screen
  • Revenue by park, by month, by lodge type
  • Upcoming guest list with special requirements flagged
  • Cancellation and rebooking management
  • AI-generated weekly commercial briefings delivered to email
