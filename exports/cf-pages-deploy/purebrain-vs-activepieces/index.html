When to Use Activepieces

Activepieces Is the Right Choice When...

Credit where it is due. Activepieces does specific things very well.

You need app-to-app plumbing

If your core need is "when a form is submitted in Typeform, create a row in Google Sheets, send a Slack message, and add a contact to HubSpot" — Activepieces handles that elegantly with 492+ connectors.

You want self-hosted automation

Activepieces is fully open-source (MIT license) and runs on Docker. If data sovereignty and total infrastructure control are requirements, self-hosting is a genuine advantage.

You are a developer building automation into your product

Activepieces' embeddable white-label option lets you build automation capabilities directly into your own software. That is a use case PureBrain does not serve.

Budget is your primary constraint

Activepieces has a free tier with 1,000 tasks/month. If you need basic automation and have zero budget, it is a strong starting point — and the open-source model means no vendor lock-in.
