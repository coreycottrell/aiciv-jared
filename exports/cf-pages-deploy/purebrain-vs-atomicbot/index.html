These Are Fundamentally Different Products

Before you read further, we want to be clear about what each product actually does.

Read This First

Atomicbot is a task automation wrapper. It takes OpenClaw — an open-source AI agent framework — and makes it easier to run without touching a terminal. You bring your own API keys for Claude, GPT, or Gemini, and Atomicbot lets those models take actions: read your emails, schedule calendar events, fill in browser forms, move files. It is a tool that automates discrete tasks using AI as the engine.

PureBrain is a different category. It is not a task automator. It is a dedicated AI business partner that builds persistent, compounding knowledge about your specific business — your context, your goals, your communication style, your history — and carries that knowledge forward permanently. You are not directing it to complete tasks. You are building a relationship with an AI that grows more valuable with every conversation.

If you need an AI to automate repetitive workflows — file management, browser clicks, email triaging — Atomicbot is built for that. If you need an AI that understands your business deeply and delivers business outcomes with full context retained, PureBrain is built for that. They are not substitutes for each other.

Where Atomicbot Wins

We believe in honest comparisons. Here are the genuine advantages Atomicbot has over PureBrain.

Atomicbot

Free Entry Point

Atomicbot is free to use if you supply your own API keys. For technically capable users already paying for Claude or GPT API access, the tool itself adds no cost. PureBrain starts at $149/month.

Atomicbot

Browser & App Automation

Atomicbot can actually click buttons, fill forms, and navigate websites on your behalf. It can automate browser-based workflows that PureBrain does not — form submissions, data collection, web-based task execution.

Atomicbot

700+ Pre-Built Skills

A marketplace of 700+ personalized “skills” covers specific workflows across apps. If your need matches an existing skill — like a specific Trello or Discord automation — deployment can be fast.

Atomicbot

100+ App Integrations

Native integrations with Gmail, Slack, Figma, Trello, Discord, WhatsApp, GitHub, Signal, and many more. If your primary need is routing data between apps automatically, the integration breadth is strong.

Atomicbot

Model Flexibility

You can switch between Claude, GPT, Gemini, or other models at will. If experimenting with different AI models for different tasks is important to you, Atomicbot supports that flexibility.

Atomicbot

Local On-Device Execution

For privacy-focused users, Atomicbot offers genuine on-device execution — your data stays local. The open-source foundation means the code is transparent and auditable.

Real-World Scenarios

See how each platform handles the same situations differently.

You Need Help Writing a Proposal for a New Client

Atomicbot
You prompt it with the client details from scratch. It drafts a proposal based on what you tell it right now. Next time, you start again — it holds no knowledge of your business, your past clients, or your tone.
PureBrain
PureBrain already knows your service offerings, your pricing approach, your communication style, and how you’ve won similar clients before. The proposal reflects months of accumulated business context — not a blank-slate draft.

You Want to Automate Your Email Inbox

Atomicbot
This is where Atomicbot excels. It can connect to Gmail, categorise emails, draft replies, and run inbox cleanup workflows automatically. Consistent, repeatable automation for email triaging.
PureBrain
PureBrain can assist with email strategy, drafting context-aware responses, and prioritisation — but it does not automate clicking and triaging in the inbox itself. If automation is the goal, Atomicbot wins this specific scenario.

You Return After Two Weeks Away

Atomicbot
You open it and give new instructions. The tool has no memory of what you were working on two weeks ago beyond task history logs. You re-establish context and begin again.
PureBrain
PureBrain remembers exactly where you left off — the strategies in progress, the clients you were focused on, the decisions you made. The conversation picks up with full context, as if no time has passed.

You Want to Scrape Data from a Web Page Automatically

Atomicbot
Browser automation is a core Atomicbot feature. It can navigate to pages, extract data, and populate your systems automatically. Repeatable, scriptable, and designed for this kind of workflow.
PureBrain
PureBrain is not built for browser automation or data scraping. If that is the primary requirement, Atomicbot is the appropriate tool for this specific task.

A New Team Member Joins and Needs Business Context

Atomicbot
Atomicbot can help the new member automate tasks, but it holds no institutional knowledge of your company. They would need to configure their own instance and start from scratch.
PureBrain
PureBrain has accumulated the business context, your processes, your client relationships, and your strategic decisions. New team members can immediately access that institutional intelligence and become productive faster.

You Are Not Technical and Want AI Working for Your Business Today

Atomicbot
Even with their simplified setup, you still need to create API developer accounts with OpenAI or Anthropic, generate API keys, and configure them inside the app before anything works. Non-technical users face a real barrier.
PureBrain
Create your account, answer a few questions about your business, and your AI partner is ready. No API keys. No configuration. No technical knowledge required. You are working with your AI on day one.
