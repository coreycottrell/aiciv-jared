Read This First

GlobalGPT is an AI model aggregator. For one subscription, you get access to 100+ AI models — GPT-5, Claude Opus, Gemini, image generators, video tools, and research apps. If you need a cheap, all-in-one dashboard to try different AI tools, GLBGPT might genuinely be useful for you.

PureBrain is something different. It’s not a model switcher. It’s a single AI partner that builds persistent memory of your business, your goals, your context, and your history — and compounds that knowledge over time. You’re not buying access to generic models. You’re building a relationship with an AI that works for your business specifically.

So: if you want breadth and model variety at low cost, GLBGPT is built for that. If you want an AI that grows with your business and delivers outcomes — not just outputs — PureBrain is built for that. They are not the same category of product.
