The Key Distinction

"Mentimeter captures the moment. PureBrain captures the journey."

Mentimeter's entire design philosophy centers on anonymity and session-by-session freshness — participants join with a code, respond, and leave. That's a feature, not a bug. PureBrain's entire design philosophy centers on the opposite: persistent identity, remembered context, and an AI relationship that deepens over months and years. They're not competing for the same value.

Honest Assessment

Which One Is Actually Right for You?

These tools serve genuinely different needs. Some professionals need both — for different parts of their work.

Mentimeter is right for you if...

  • 🎬You present regularly and want to make those presentations interactive and engaging
  • 🎬You run training sessions, workshops, or university lectures with passive audiences
  • 🎬Anonymous participation is a feature — you want honest responses without attribution
  • 🎬You need real-time visualization of audience responses during a live event
  • 🎬Your value comes from what happens during the presentation, not after
  • 🎬You work in HR, L&D, academia, or events where audience engagement is the deliverable
  • 🎬Per-presenter pricing at ~$27/month makes sense for your presentation frequency

PureBrain is right for you if...

  • ✓You want AI intelligence that compounds across months and years, not sessions
  • ✓You want an AI that knows you personally — your goals, history, and working style
  • ✓You're a professional who needs a thinking partner, not a presentation add-on
  • ✓The value you need comes from between presentations, not during them
  • ✓Strategic decisions, ongoing learning, and knowledge retention matter more than live polling
  • ✓You use Mentimeter for presentations AND want PureBrain for everything else
  • ✓You want your AI relationship to travel with you and deepen over time
