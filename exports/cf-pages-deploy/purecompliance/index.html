07
AI Advantages vs. Drata
How Drata Does It

Generic policy templates that companies customize manually. Static, pre-built control mappings maintained by their compliance team.

How PureCompliance Does It

Feed Claude the company's industry, size, tech stack, existing tools, and regulatory requirements. Generate a complete, customized policy document in 30 seconds.

📜 AI-Generated Policies Tailored to the Company

▼

A 50-person SaaS healthcare startup gets an Information Security Policy that references their specific AWS infrastructure, Okta SSO setup, HIPAA requirements, and remote-first work model -- not a generic template. Complete in 30 seconds.

🔗 AI Control Mapping

▼

AI dynamically maps controls to framework requirements. Add ISO 27001 to existing SOC 2 and AI shows which controls already satisfy ISO requirements (typically 60-70% overlap). When frameworks update (e.g., PCI DSS v4.0), AI re-maps automatically.

🔍 AI Evidence Analysis

▼

Instead of "MFA is not enabled" (binary), AI says: "MFA is enabled for 94% of users. 3 service accounts lack MFA. These accounts have admin access to production databases. Recommendation: Enable MFA or implement compensating controls. This affects SOC 2 CC6.1, ISO A.8.5, and HIPAA 164.312(d)."

🎯 AI Audit Preparation

▼

AI simulates auditor behavior. It asks the same questions an auditor would, evaluates your answers, identifies weak spots, and coaches you. "An auditor will likely ask about your change management process for database schema changes. Your current evidence shows PR reviews for application code but no evidence for database migrations."

⚡ AI Dynamic Risk Scoring

▼

Risk scores update continuously based on real-time signals. New vulnerability disclosed? Risk score adjusts. Employee leaves without offboarding? Risk score increases. New encryption enabled? Risk score decreases. All automatic, all explained. Living risk register vs. quarterly snapshot.

💬 Conversational Compliance Assistant

▼

Chat interface where compliance managers ask: "Are we ready for our SOC 2 Type II audit?" or "What controls do we need to add for HIPAA?" or "Generate an executive summary of our compliance posture for the board." Democratizes compliance knowledge -- junior team members operate at senior level.

📨 AI Security Questionnaire Auto-Responder

▼

Upload any security questionnaire (DDQ, VSA, SIG, custom) and AI fills it out using your compliance data, policies, and evidence. Human reviews and approves. Security questionnaires take 2-8 hours manually. AI does 90% in 5 minutes. For companies receiving 10+ questionnaires/month, this alone justifies the subscription.

Honest Assessment (What We Can't Do)

Auditor Network / Partnerships

▼

Drata has established relationships with major audit firms (Coalfire, A-LIGN, KPMG). We won't get "recommended by your auditor" at launch.

Mitigation

  • Build an auditor-friendly portal any CPA firm can use
  • Offer free auditor accounts for grassroots adoption
  • Target smaller/mid-size audit firms
  • Revenue-share with referring audit firms

SOC 2 Certification Itself

▼

We help you GET ready for SOC 2 -- we don't GRANT certification. That requires a licensed CPA firm.

Mitigation

  • Build marketplace of vetted audit firms
  • "Audit-ready guarantee" -- if auditor finds gaps we missed, we fix free
  • Bundled pricing with partner audit firms

Platform Certification

▼

Drata is itself SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certified. Enterprise buyers may ask about our certifications.

Mitigation

  • Cloudflare infrastructure is SOC 2 / ISO 27001 certified (inherited)
  • Pursue our own SOC 2 within 6 months using our own platform
  • "We're our own first customer" -- compelling story

Market Trust / Brand Recognition

▼

Drata ($200M+ raised), Vanta ($203M raised) have years of market presence and thousands of customer logos.

Mitigation

  • Price advantage is real and compelling for SMBs
  • AI-native is a genuine differentiator
  • PLG -- let the product sell itself
  • Build case studies fast with early adopters
  • Content marketing around AI compliance thought leadership
