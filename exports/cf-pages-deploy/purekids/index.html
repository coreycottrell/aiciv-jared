AI-Powered Features

What Makes PureKids Different

The AI doesn't just block. It learns, adapts, and helps families grow together.

Earned Autonomy

Kids earn expanded access through responsible behavior. Complete homework? Get 30 minutes of games. Demonstrate good judgment? Unlock a new app. The AI tracks patterns and recommends graduation steps to parents.

Time-of-Day Intelligence

Apps available only at certain times. School hours = learning only. Evenings = social + entertainment with limits. Bedtime = phone becomes an alarm clock. Parents set the schedule, AI enforces it.

Contact Validation

Parents must approve every addition to the DM contact list. AI flags suspicious contacts, age-inappropriate conversations, or unknown numbers. Group chats require parent opt-in.

Context-Aware Filtering

AI understands context — school vs home vs friends' houses. At school: educational apps only. At home: parent-approved apps. At a friend's: maintains rules but adapts to social settings.

Family Intelligence Dashboard

Not surveillance — partnership. Weekly family reports show usage patterns, conversation starters ("Your child searched for X — here's how to talk about it"), and recommendations for expanding trust.

Age-Adaptive Controls

Controls evolve from ages 8 to 18. A 9-year-old's PureKids experience is completely different from a 15-year-old's. The AI adjusts automatically as kids mature and demonstrate readiness.

AI Companion (Not Chatbot)

Kids get a named AI companion that helps with homework, answers questions age-appropriately, and models healthy digital behavior. Powered by PureBrain's partnership technology.

Emergency Override

Kids can always call 911, parents, and designated emergency contacts regardless of any restrictions. Safety-first architecture that never blocks critical communication.
