Best Practices.

Simple habits to unlock your full potential with PureBrain. These are the patterns our most successful partners share.

Delegate Before Bed

Send your tasks at night. Wake up to completed work. Your AI partner works 24/7 -- use off-peak hours for complex, multi-step projects.
