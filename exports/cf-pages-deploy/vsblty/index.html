Product Suite

AI Software That Sees, Understands & Acts

Edge and cloud-enabled software solutions combining digital signage, audience analytics, facial recognition, and threat detection in a single platform.

📊
Analytics

DataCaptor™

Real-time audience measurement using camera and sensor technology with AI and machine learning. Captures anonymous demographic data, dwell time, attention metrics, and sentiment analysis at point of engagement.

📺
Content Delivery

VisionCaptor™

AI-driven targeted display content delivery system. Serves proximity-aware, demographically relevant messaging at point of purchase. Transforms static displays into intelligent, responsive media touchpoints.

🛡
Security

VSBLTY Vector™

State-of-the-art facial recognition and weapon detection software. Interfaces with local or remote databases to detect persons of interest and threat objects within a camera's field of view in real time.

📈
Measurement

VSBLTY Metrics

Computer vision measurement platform for validating media impressions at point of sale. Provides verified audience data, engagement rates, and conversion analytics for retail media networks.
