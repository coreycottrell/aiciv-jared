## PART 1: Create a Claude Account From Scratch

### Entry Point
- URL: **https://claude.ai**
- Also available via: desktop app (Mac/Windows), mobile app (iOS/Android)

### Step-by-Step

**Step 1: Go to claude.ai**
Open a browser and navigate to https://claude.ai

**Step 2: Choose your sign-in method**
You will see two options:
- **"Continue with Google"** — Recommended for speed. Uses your existing Google account.
- **"Continue with email"** — Enter your email address, receive a magic link.

*(Note: No password creation is required. Authentication is either Google OAuth or email magic link.)*

**Step 3a — If you chose "Continue with Google":**
1. A Google account picker will appear.
2. Select your Google account or enter your Google email.
3. Enter your Google password if prompted and click "Next."
4. Google authentication completes and you are redirected to Claude.

**Step 3b — If you chose "Continue with email":**
1. Enter your email address in the field.
2. Click "Continue with email."
3. Check your inbox for an email with subject line: "Secure link to log in to Claude.ai"
4. Click the link in the email.
   - If you click the link on the SAME device you started on: you will be automatically logged in.
   - If you click the link on a DIFFERENT device: a verification code will appear. Enter that code on your original device.

**Step 4: Phone number verification (mandatory)**
During first-time account creation, Claude requires phone verification:
1. Enter your mobile phone number.
2. Check the box confirming you are at least 18 years old.
3. Click "Send Verification Code."
4. Enter the OTP (one-time code) you received via SMS.
5. Click "Verify and Create Account."

*(Important: One phone number = one Claude account. This cannot be bypassed.)*

**Step 5: Enter your name**
Enter your first and last name when prompted, then click "Send" or "Continue."

**Step 6: Account created**
You now have a free Claude account. You will land on the main chat interface at claude.ai.

## PART 2: Upgrade to Claude Max

### What is Claude Max?
Claude Max is a premium subscription with significantly higher usage limits than the standard Pro plan ($20/month). It comes in two tiers:

| Tier | Price | Usage vs. Pro |
|------|-------|----------------|
| **Max 5x** | $100/month | 5x more usage than Pro |
| **Max 20x** | $200/month | 20x more usage than Pro |

Both Max tiers include:
- Higher usage limits
- Priority access to newest Claude models and features
- Access to Claude Code (terminal-based AI coding tool)
- Access to Cowork (delegate complex tasks)
- Desktop + mobile apps in one subscription
- Monthly billing only (no annual option currently)

### Upgrade Path A: Brand New User (No Account Yet)

1. Go to **https://claude.ai** and create your account (see Part 1 above).
2. During the onboarding process after account creation, select the **Max plan option** when prompted.
3. Choose your preferred usage tier: **5x ($100/mo)** or **20x ($200/mo)**.
4. Enter your payment information (credit or debit card — only payment method accepted).
5. Click "Subscribe" to confirm.
6. Your Max subscription is now active.

### Upgrade Path B: Existing Free Account

1. Log in to your existing Claude account at **https://claude.ai**
2. Click your **initials or name** in the lower-left corner of the interface.
3. Select **"Settings"** from the dropdown menu.
4. Navigate to **Settings > Billing**.
5. Click **"Upgrade plan."**
6. On the upgrade page, select the **Max plan**.
7. Choose your tier: **5x ($100/mo)** or **20x ($200/mo)**.
8. Enter your payment information (credit or debit card).
9. Click **"Subscribe."**
10. Upgrade is immediately active.

**Shortcut URL**: You can also go directly to **https://claude.ai/upgrade** to skip to the upgrade screen.

### Upgrade Path C: Existing Pro Plan Subscriber ($20/mo)

1. Log in to your Claude account at **https://claude.ai**
2. Click your **initials or name** in the lower-left corner.
3. Select **"Settings."**
4. Navigate to **Settings > Billing**.
5. Click **"Upgrade plan."**
6. Select the **Max plan** option.
7. Choose your tier: **5x ($100/mo)** or **20x ($200/mo)**.
8. Review and **confirm your upgrade.**
9. Billing is automatically adjusted on a **prorated basis** for the remainder of your current billing cycle.

*(If you had an annual Pro plan, any remaining balance may be applied as account credits.)*

## PART 3: Billing Notes (Important for Users)

- **Payment method**: Credit or debit card only. No PayPal, no crypto, no invoicing on Max.
- **Auto-renew**: Subscriptions auto-renew each billing period. Cancel anytime in Settings > Billing.
- **Max is monthly only**: No annual option currently available for Max plans.
- **Geographic restriction**: Paid plans require you to be physically located in a supported country. A phone number from a supported country is required.
- **Upgrades are prorated**: If you upgrade mid-cycle (e.g., Free to Max, or Pro to Max), you are charged the prorated amount for the remainder of the billing period.
- **Discounts**: No standard discounts. Anthropic may occasionally run limited-time promotions via official channels.
