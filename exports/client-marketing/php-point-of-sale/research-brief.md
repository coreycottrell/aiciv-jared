# PHP Point of Sale - Research Brief
**Date**: 2026-03-05
**Prepared by**: Client Marketing Director

---

## Company Overview

**PHP Point of Sale** (phppointofsale.com) is a cloud-based and on-premise POS software platform built for small-to-medium retail businesses. Founded as an open-source PHP project that evolved into a commercial SaaS product.

- **Price**: $32.50/month (billed annually), ~$34/month monthly
- **Free Trial**: 14 days, no credit card required
- **Model**: Unlimited devices, employees, inventory items per license
- **Multi-location**: Additional license per location
- **Setup**: Marketed as 30-minute setup

**Other Software Integrations**:

**Hardware Support**: EMV/chip card readers, mag stripe, receipt printers, barcode scanners, cash drawers

## Payment Processing Pain Points (Inferred from Product Profile)

1. **Multi-processor fragmentation**: They support Stripe + Braintree + their own processor — merchants managing multiple payment relationships, reconciliation complexity
2. **Transaction fee opacity**: Small retailers often don't know if they're getting good rates; interchange-plus vs flat-rate confusion
3. **PCI compliance burden**: Small retailers rarely have IT staff; compliance scope is often unclear
4. **Ecommerce payment sync**: They integrate with Shopify/WooCommerce but payment data reconciliation across channels is a known headache
5. **Hardware lock-in**: EMV reader compatibility varies; merchants risk buying hardware that doesn't work with preferred processor
6. **Chargeback management**: Small retailers often lose chargebacks by default due to lack of time/expertise
7. **International/crypto friction**: They advertise crypto support but most payment infrastructure for their market isn't built for it cleanly
8. **Limited payment analytics**: Their strength is inventory/sales reporting; payment-specific analytics (decline rates, card type mix, authorization rates) are often weak in POS platforms

## Competitive Landscape

Main competitors: Square POS, Clover, Lightspeed, Shopify POS, Vend

PHP POS differentiates on: **price** (much cheaper than Square/Clover), **unlimited users/devices**, and **open ecosystem** (not locked to one payment processor like Square or Clover).

This open ecosystem approach is their biggest advantage AND their biggest opportunity for us — they need a payment partner who matches their "open, no lock-in" philosophy.
