## Key Insights for Blueprint
1. **Pain point**: Likely relying on third-party platforms (Booking.com, Airbnb) — losing 15–25% in commission per booking
2. **Opportunity**: 5 parks = 5 distinct audiences = need for unified but differentiated approach
3. **Core ask**: Proprietary booking + payment + CRM + email marketing = full commercial independence
4. **Scale play**: Cross-park customer lifecycle (Sherwood guest → Woodlakes guest next season)
