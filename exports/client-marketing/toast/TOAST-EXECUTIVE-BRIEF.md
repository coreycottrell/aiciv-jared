# Toast POS Marketing — Executive Brief
**Date**: March 10, 2026 | **Prepared by**: Client Marketing Department

---

## The Opportunity in One Sentence

Toast serves 127,000+ restaurants whose operators paid for an all-in-one marketing platform and are using almost none of it — and we are going to be the agency that closes that gap.

---

## What We Are Building

A vertically specialized marketing agency for Toast restaurant operators. We enter as a managed services agency, build proof of concept with 2-3 restaurants in the first 90 days, then layer in a Toast API integration product that automates the marketing intelligence gap — turning raw POS transaction data into personalized campaigns that no general-purpose agency can replicate.

**Three revenue streams at maturity:**
1. Managed marketing retainers ($750-$5,000+/month per client)
2. Toast-connected SaaS marketing automation product ($149-$249/month per location)
3. Toast corporate content/marketing services relationship (18-24 month horizon)

## Why This Wins

**The gap is real.** Toast has the tools. Restaurant operators don't have the time or knowledge to use them. No major marketing agency has built specifically for the Toast ecosystem.

**The moat is durable.** Toast API integration — turning POS transaction data into automated marketing — is something no generalist agency can do manually. Once built, it becomes the product that creates compounding competitive advantage.

**The market is enormous.** At 1% penetration of Toast's 127,000+ location base with the integration product alone: $3M+ annual recurring revenue.

**The timing is right.** Toast is growing into enterprise and multi-location. Independent operators are getting squeezed by chains with real marketing budgets. The demand for affordable, restaurant-specialized marketing is at an inflection point.

## The First 90 Days — What Has to Happen

1. ICP finalized and 150+ prospects in CRM by Day 15
2. Free Toast Marketing Audit offer launched, 15-20 delivered by Day 60
3. First paying client signed by Day 45

## Biggest Risk to Address Now

Proof of concept must generate real, measurable results. If the first 2-3 clients do not produce numbers worth publishing (repeat visit rate change, email open rates, Google review velocity), the sales case for Phase 3 is weak. Price the first clients generously to ensure full engagement and cooperation in documenting outcomes.
