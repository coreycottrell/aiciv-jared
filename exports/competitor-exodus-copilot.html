The hidden cost

The 4 ways Copilot keeps you locked in

This isn't just about cost. It's about who controls your AI relationship.

01

Ecosystem dependency

Full capability requires M365. Partial use = partial value. Your AI effectiveness is tied to your Microsoft contract.

02

Microsoft's roadmap, not yours

Every Copilot improvement is Microsoft's product decision. Your most needed capability might never arrive if it doesn't fit their strategy.

03

No business customization

Copilot knows your files. It doesn't know your strategy, your values, your voice, or what makes your business unique.

04

Reactive, not proactive

Copilot assists when you ask. It never initiates. It doesn't notice patterns, surface insights, or flag things you should see.
