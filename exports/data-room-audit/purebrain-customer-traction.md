## CUSTOMER RETENTION THESIS

PureBrain is specifically designed for **inverted churn** — the longer a customer stays, the less likely they are to leave.

### The Memory Moat Effect

| Month | What PureBrain Knows About You | Switching Cost |
|-------|-------------------------------|----------------|
| Month 1 | Basic business context | Low |
| Month 3 | Deep product/service knowledge; key customers; projects | Medium |
| Month 6 | Communication style; decision history; team dynamics; competitive intelligence | High |
| Month 12 | 12 months of decisions, outcomes, experiments, lessons | Very High |
| Month 24 | Irreplaceable institutional knowledge | Extreme |

No competitor can replicate this memory. When a subscriber considers switching to ChatGPT:
- They lose all accumulated business context
- They restart the 629% compound advantage from zero
- Every past decision, conversation, and lesson is gone

This is the opposite of traditional SaaS churn dynamics. Standard SaaS sees maximum churn in months 1–3. PureBrain should see minimum churn in months 1–3 (before the memory builds) and near-zero churn after month 6.

### Retention Drivers Beyond Memory

1. **Brainiac Community**: Network effect — leaving PureBrain means leaving the community of AI-forward business owners
2. **Workflow Integration**: By Month 3, AI workflows are embedded in daily operations. Removing them is disruptive.
3. **BOOP Automation**: Background tasks running automatically create habitual reliance
4. **Training Curriculum**: Ongoing Brainiac modules provide continuous value and education
5. **Team Usage**: When the whole team uses PureBrain, individual cancellation is a department decision, not a personal one
