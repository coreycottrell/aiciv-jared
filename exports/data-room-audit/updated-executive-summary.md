## WHY PURE TECHNOLOGY WINS

### 1. First Mover with Real Moat

PureBrain has an 18+ month head start on the persistent-memory AI business partner category. The moat compounds: every month of customer AI memory makes switching harder.

### 2. Two Independent Growth Engines

If Pure Phone hardware is delayed, PureBrain generates revenue independently. If the AI market softens, the $4T+ marketing data market provides massive upside. The two engines de-risk each other.

### 3. The 100-Person Model at $72B Revenue

Under 100 people generating $72B in Year 5 revenue implies 78% EBITDA margins. This is not an aspiration — it is the architectural constraint that forces AI-first operations and keeps the business structurally defensible from competition.

### 4. Compounding Consumer Intelligence (Unprecedented)

The DiMAP platform, once at scale, will have the most comprehensive opt-in consumer profile database ever assembled. Combined with PureBrain's AI relationship layer, Pure Technology will simultaneously know:
- What consumers buy, watch, and care about (DiMAP)
- How businesses think and make decisions (PureBrain)

This is the Complete Intelligence Picture — brands knowing consumers deeply, businesses thinking clearly, and PureBrain connecting both sides.

Jared has run this company since 2017. He survived the startup years when PMG was scraping $14K profit on $77K revenue. He built PureBrain because he experienced the problem personally — he needed an AI that knew his business. He did not wait for a VC to fund this feature. He built it, launched it, and put paying customers on it.

That kind of founder conviction — building what you need, then selling it — is the most reliable predictor of long-term success.
