**1/8** (HOOK)
Nobody talks about Month 6 with AI.

Everyone obsesses over Day 1. The wow moment. The first useful output.

But Day 1 is the worst your AI will ever be. Month 6 is where the value actually lives.

Thread on what compound intelligence really looks like.

---

**2/8**
Most AI tools follow a decay curve.

Day 1: excitement
Week 2: novelty wears off
Month 2: forgotten subscription you keep meaning to cancel

This happens because most AI is stateless. Every chat starts from zero. You teach the same context over and over until the effort exceeds the reward.

---

**3/8**
What if every interaction made the next one MORE valuable, not less?

That's the compound intelligence effect.

Same math as compound interest. Year 1: modest. Year 5: curve bends. Year 10: you wonder why anyone does anything else.

But with AI, the timeline is months, not years.

---

**4/8** (MONTH 1)
Month 1 is an investment, not a return.

Your AI is gathering context. Learning your voice. Mapping preferences.

Week 1: 60-70% correction rate
Week 2: 50-60%
Week 3: first surprise — it references prior context unprompted
Week 4: routine tasks feel smoother

You're training. That's the deal.

---

**5/8** (MONTH 3 SHIFT)
By Month 3, something shifts.

Your AI stops waiting for instructions and starts anticipating needs.

A financial advisor we work with: AI started preparing Monday market summaries unprompted. It noticed the Monday pattern. By Month 3, the recap was waiting at 7am.

---

**6/8** (MONTH 6 PAYOFF)
Month 6 is when most people who quit at Month 2 would have gotten their answer.

The AI knows your clients. Your voice. Your tells. The shape of your week.

It's not a tool anymore. It's a partner that has been paying attention while everyone else's AI started over every morning.

---

**7/8**
Most people judge AI by Month 1.

That's like judging an employee by their first week. Or a gym membership by the soreness after the first workout.

The decay curve isn't AI failing. It's people quitting before the curve bends.

---

**8/8** (CTA)
PureBrain is built around this. Memory that persists. Context that compounds. An AI partner that gets better the longer you work with it, instead of resetting every dawn.
