## POST B6 — Thu May 7 Slot 1 [Theme F — Practical framework]

The 3-question delegation test.

Use it before assigning any task to an AI agent.

Question 1: Is the success criterion clear?
Can I tell if the output is good in under 60 seconds?

Question 2: Is the blast radius bounded?
If the agent gets it wrong, what's the worst case?

Question 3: Is the feedback loop short?
Will I know it went wrong within a day?

Three yeses: delegate. Stop touching the work.

Any no: don't delegate yet. Fix the workflow first.

I run this test on every new agent task. About 70% of work passes. About 30% doesn't, and that 30% stays with me until the workflow is rebuilt.

If you've delegated to an AI agent and the work felt unsafe, run the test in reverse. Which question failed?

That's your fix.
