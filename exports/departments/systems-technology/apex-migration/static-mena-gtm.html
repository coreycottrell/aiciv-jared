Policy Alignment

Aligned with national strategies

MENA governments are investing in AI to create jobs, not cut them. PureBrain's augmentation model is politically aligned with national priorities.

+

Saudi Vision 2030: 70% of strategic goals involve data and AI. The focus is workforce uplift, not workforce reduction. 42% of Saudi businesses favour direct funding for employee retraining.

+

UAE Strategy 2031: Aspires to be the world's first fully AI-powered government by 2027. 97% AI utilisation across government entities. AI mandatory in education from kindergarten.
