## FULL GUIDE CONTENT (from Google Doc export)

### Overview
This guide teaches a LinkedIn promotion strategy to increase post reach within 3 days using paid ads targeting specific audiences, bypassing algorithmic limitations.

**Core concept**: Take your organic posts and use cheap LinkedIn ads ($10/day) to guarantee relevant impressions from your ideal buyers.

---

### STAGE 1: Content Creation

#### Two Content Buckets

**Bucket 1: Belief-Shifting Content**
- Establishes your unique perspective on industry problems
- Takes a stance on the status quo - identifies "the enemy" (concept or method you oppose)
- Uses storytelling and examples to prove your viewpoint
- Preparation questions:
  - What is the status quo?
  - Where are the gaps?
  - Why is your approach superior?
  - Who is the enemy?
  - What stories prove your thinking?

**Bucket 2: Tactical Content**
- Demonstrates expertise through how-tos, case studies, and step-by-step guides
- Includes specific numbers and measurable results
- Closes the loop opened by belief-shifting posts

**Alternative: Direct Offer Content**
- Written as direct response ads with minimal engagement but maximum landing page clicks
- Requires a highly specific, compelling offer
- Examples: conversion audits, specialized guides
- Success metric: converting 20% of social actions into landing page clicks

---

### STAGE 2: Audience Building

#### Cold Audience Creation
1. Navigate LinkedIn Ads Manager: Plan > Audiences > Create Audience
2. Define ideal customer profile using LinkedIn targeting metrics
3. Save the audience for reuse across campaigns

#### Retargeting Audience
- Automatically builds from people who interacted with promoted content
- Requires minimum 300 members before activation
- Created through: Plan > Audiences > Matched > Create Audience

---

### STAGE 3: Campaign Setup

#### Campaign Group Creation
1. Click "Advertise" > "Create" > "Campaign Group"
2. Select "Engagement" as objective
3. Choose "Run continuously from start date"

#### Campaign Configuration
- **Name format**: Include "Cold" + audience description
- **Daily budget**: $10-$30 recommended
- **Manual bidding**: $0.15 per click suggested
- **Format**: Single image
- **CRITICAL**: Disable these three things:
  - Audience expansion
  - LinkedIn Audience Network
  - Bid adjustment

#### Creative Selection
1. Browse existing personal profile content
2. Request approval for 3 belief-shifting posts
3. Navigate to personal profile to approve requests
4. Tag approved posts and add to campaign

#### Launch Process
1. Review all settings before activation
2. Add payment method when prompted
3. Ads enter review phase (typically 5 minutes to 4 hours)
4. Posts display promotion indicator once approved
