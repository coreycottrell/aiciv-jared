## 9. RISK FACTORS

- Risk: Downtime as user base scales

- Risk: Established players (Google, Meta, Amazon) entering the space
- Mitigation: Blue Ocean positioning, hardware moat, opt-in model, first-party data advantage (cookieless tailwind)

## APPENDIX: KEY USE CASES (DiMAP Platform)

1. **Application Installs & Use Time Studies**: Clients target by apps installed and frequency of use. Users review apps (screen recorded + microphone + optional video). 3–15 minute studies; eye tracking optional (higher pay rate).

2. **Mystery Shopping**: Users visit physical or online stores and provide structured feedback. ~$100/user/study, 4 studies per user per year.

3. **Focus Groups**: Virtual focus groups via phone — 2x per year per user, $416/user/study. Screen + microphone recording.

4. **Consumer Experience Studies**: 5 studies per year per user, $37.50/user/study. 7.5-minute average.

5. **Surveys & Questionnaires**: 25 per year per user, $4/survey. Fastest revenue per time unit.

6. **Social Media Grassroots Influencer Posts**: Users with 20 to 1M followers post about brands. $5/post, 20 posts/year. Pure platform eliminates fake follower risk.

7. **Mobile Advertising (Retarget)**: 404 targeting events per user per year at $0.25 each = $10.10/user/year.

8. **Pure Vision (Smart Retail)**: Integration with VSBLTY's in-store digital displays. Pure Phone users detected via Bluetooth/NFC when near a VSBLTY display → personalized content served in real time based on their complete consumer profile.
