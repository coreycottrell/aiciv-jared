## DILIGENCE PROCESS OVERVIEW

**Phase 1: Initial Review (Week 1)**
- Investor reviews the one-pager and FAQ
- Investor requests access to investor portal for detailed materials
- NDA executed
- Initial call with Jared (30 min)

**Phase 2: Deep Diligence (Week 2-3)**
- Financial review: cap table, financials, unit economics
- Legal review: corporate documents, IP, contracts
- Product review: demo, architecture, roadmap

**Phase 3: Reference Calls and Final Questions (Week 3-4)**
- Customer reference calls (2-3 customers)
- Technical deep dive (optional, for technical investors)
- Final Q&A with Jared

**Phase 4: Close**
- Subscription agreement executed
- Funds wired
- Cap table updated

## PRIORITY ITEMS FOR IMMEDIATE READINESS

The following should be ready to share within 24 hours of any serious investor request:

1. Cap table (current and pro-forma post-Series-A)
2. Series-A term sheet confirmation / redacted copy
3. PureBrain revenue model with assumptions
4. Unit economics detail (LTV, CAC, ARPU basis)
5. Use of funds breakdown
6. Founder bio (Jared)
7. Product demo (PureBrain live or recorded)
