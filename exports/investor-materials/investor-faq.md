## SECTION 1: THE COMPANY

### What is Pure Technology?

Pure Technology Inc. is a New York-based AI technology company incorporated as a C-Corp in December 2017. The company builds AI-native infrastructure for enterprise intelligence, mobile engagement, and marketing automation.

Pure Technology operates three business units:
- **PureBrain.ai** - AI enterprise intelligence platform (primary product)
- **PureInfluence.ai** - AI-powered influencer and creator marketing infrastructure
- **PureMarketing.ai (PMG)** - Full-service AI marketing group, revenue-generating today

The company's thesis: AI is not a feature layer on top of existing software. It is the new operating system for business. Pure Technology is building the infrastructure layer for that transition.

---

### What is PureBrain?

PureBrain is a persistent AI intelligence platform that learns, retains, and compounds organizational knowledge over time. Unlike generic AI tools (ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot) that reset context with every session, PureBrain maintains a permanent, evolving memory of your team, your customers, your processes, and your priorities.

**What makes PureBrain different:**

| Generic AI Tools | PureBrain |
|-----------------|-----------|
| Resets every session | Permanent memory |
| General-purpose | Org-specific context |
| Single user | Team intelligence layer |
| Tool you use | Partner that learns you |
| $20-100/month | $345/month blended ARPU |

PureBrain enables sub-100 headcount organizations to operate at enterprise-grade output - the core value proposition driving 78% EBITDA margins and a 225:1 LTV:CAC ratio.

### What is Pure Technology's competitive moat?

**1. Persistent Memory Architecture**
The core technology - AI that retains organizational context over time - is architecturally distinct from all major AI platforms. Replicating this requires not just technical development but a base of trained organizational memory data.

**2. Network effects in the mobile platform**
The PT mobile platform creates data compounding: more devices generate more behavioral signal, which improves targeting, which increases platform value, which attracts more advertisers and devices.

**3. PMG as a distribution channel**
PureMarketing.ai (PMG) is an active, revenue-generating marketing operation. It functions as both a standalone business and a customer acquisition engine for PureBrain and PureInfluence.

**4. First-mover in persistent enterprise AI**
The window for establishing the organizational memory standard in enterprise AI is open now. Incumbents (Salesforce, SAP, Microsoft) are adding AI to existing architectures rather than rebuilding for AI-nativity. PT is purpose-built for this architecture.

## SECTION 5: RISKS AND DILIGENCE

### What are the key risks?

**Market execution risk**: Revenue projections assume successful go-to-market execution. PureBrain must convert existing traction into the subscriber volumes required to reach Year 1 ARR targets.

**Series-A timing risk**: While the MAKR term sheet is signed, closing processes involve legal and regulatory steps. Delays are possible.

**Competitive risk**: Well-capitalized incumbents (Microsoft, Google, Salesforce) are aggressively adding AI capabilities. Pure Technology's moat depends on maintaining architectural differentiation.

**Team scaling risk**: Sub-100 headcount operations will require disciplined hiring as the company scales to enterprise ARR targets.

**Technical risk**: The persistent memory architecture is novel. Implementation at scale may surface unforeseen engineering challenges.

*This is not an exhaustive risk disclosure. Investors should conduct their own due diligence.*
