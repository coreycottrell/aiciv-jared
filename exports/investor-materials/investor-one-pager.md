## The Opportunity

The global marketing and advertising market exceeds **$4 trillion annually**. The shift from attention-based advertising to AI-personalized, behavior-driven engagement is the defining infrastructure transition of this decade.

Pure Technology is building the operating layer for that transition - an AI-native platform that turns every mobile device into a personalized intelligence endpoint, and every business interaction into a compounding data asset.

The timing is precise: AI model costs have collapsed, consumer trust in AI is consolidating around a small number of trusted brands, and the enterprise market is actively displacing legacy martech stacks. **This window will not remain open.**

## The Product: PureBrain

PureBrain is not a chatbot. It is a **persistent AI intelligence layer** that learns an organization's context, team, processes, and customers - and compounds that knowledge over time.

Key differentiators:
- **Permanent memory**: Unlike generic AI tools that reset every session, PureBrain retains organizational context indefinitely
- **Sub-100 headcount scalability**: Enterprise-grade output without enterprise headcount
- **LTV:CAC of 225:1**: Among the highest unit economics in enterprise SaaS
- **Blended ARPU: $345/month** per seat
