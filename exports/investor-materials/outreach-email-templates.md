## TEMPLATE 3: Follow-Up After Investor Portal Visit

**Use for**: Anyone who has been sent the investor portal link and has had time to review. This is the conversion email - moves from interest to action. Send 3-5 days after sharing the portal link.

**Tone**: Low-pressure, helpful, creates clarity on next step. Assumes they've looked and have questions.

---

**Subject**: Re: Pure Technology - any questions after reviewing?

**Body**:

Hey [FIRST NAME],

Wanted to follow up after sharing the investor page last week.

If you had a chance to look through the materials - particularly the Series-A timing and the ROI projections - I'd love to answer any questions you have. - **Valuation basis** - how we landed at $55M and what the $105M Series-A anchor means

If you're interested in participating, minimum is $50K and we have limited capacity remaining before we close.

If it's not the right fit right now - completely fine. Just let me know and I'll keep you posted on future rounds.

Either way, happy to get 20 minutes if that would be useful.

## USAGE NOTES

**Template 1 (Warm):**
- Keep it short - the personal relationship carries more weight than the details
- The P.S. is important - gives them an easy out and signals confidence
- If they reply with interest, send the portal link immediately

**Template 2 (Cold):**
- The subject line does heavy lifting - it frontloads the near-term event
- The [PERSONALIZATION] line is not optional - cold emails without personalization convert poorly
- Research their fund thesis/recent investments for 1 specific sentence of relevance
- If no reply after 5 days, one follow-up is appropriate: "Wanted to make sure this landed - happy to send the full materials if helpful"

**Template 3 (Follow-up):**
- Send 3-5 days after portal link, not same day
- The bulleted list of common questions signals you know your material and reduces friction
- Never follow up more than twice total - three touches maximum (initial outreach, follow-up, final close reminder)
