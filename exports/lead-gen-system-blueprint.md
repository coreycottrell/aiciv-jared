# The PureBrain "Warm Circle" Lead Generation System

## 1. System Name & Overview

### System Name
**"Warm Circle" - The LinkedIn Pre-Qualification Engine**

### What It Does
A systematic 14-day warm-up approach that turns cold LinkedIn prospects into qualified PureBrain demo calls by:
1. Building familiarity through consistent content engagement BEFORE any outreach
2. Using a comment-to-DM keyword trigger for inbound lead capture
3. Routing leads through a 60-second qualification form
4. Automating tier-based follow-up

### Why This System (Data-Backed)
| Approach | Acceptance Rate | Source |
|----------|-----------------|--------|
| Cold DM with pitch | ~5-10% | Industry average |
| Personalized connection request | 32% | LinkedIn research |
| Blank connection request | 68% | LinkedIn research |
| Warm-up engagement + DM | 50%+ | Engineered conversations research |

**The math**: 50%+ acceptance with warm-up vs 32% without = **56% improvement in pipeline entry**

### Phase 2: Warm-Up Engagement (Days 4-14)

#### Daily Routine (45 minutes)

**Morning Block (20 min) - 8-9 AM:**

**Step 1: Comment on 5-7 target prospects' posts** (15 min)

Go to your tracking sheet. Find prospects who posted recently.

Comment guidelines:
- 15+ words minimum
- Add genuine insight or question
- Reference something specific in their post
- NO pitch, NO mention of PureBrain

**Comment Templates** (adapt authentically):

**Template A - Agreement + Extension**
```
This is spot on, [Name]. [Specific point they made] really resonates.

In my experience, [related insight from your perspective].

Curious - have you found [follow-up question]?
```

**Template B - Respectful Challenge**
```
Interesting perspective on [topic].

I wonder if [alternative viewpoint or nuance].

What's been your experience with [specific aspect]?
```

**Template C - Story + Question**
```
[Name], this reminds me of [brief personal story related to their post].

The part about [specific element] is particularly true.

How did you approach [related challenge]?
```

**Template D - Data + Curiosity**
```
The insight about [their point] aligns with something I've seen too.

I've noticed [related observation or data point].

Do you think [thought-provoking question]?
```

**Template E - Appreciation + Expansion**
```
Bookmarking this one. [Specific part] is underrated.

One thing I'd add: [brief additional insight].

Has [related question about their experience]?
```

**Step 2: Send 3-5 blank connection requests** (5 min)

To people you've now commented on 2-3 times.

**Before sending**:
- Visit their profile (they'll see the notification)
- Like their most recent post
- Send between 9-11 AM their timezone

**NO MESSAGE with the request** - 68% acceptance rate vs 32% with message.

---

**Afternoon Block (15 min) - 2-3 PM:**

**Step 3: Check your own post engagement** (5 min)

- Reply to EVERY comment on your posts (increases distribution)
- Like comments
- Ask follow-up questions to engaged commenters

**Step 4: Check for "BRAIN" DM keyword** (5 min)

When someone DMs "BRAIN":

**Response Template**:
```
Appreciate you reaching out!

Here's the AI Partnership Readiness Assessment - takes 60 seconds and shows whether persistent AI would change your workflow:

[Link to AI Readiness Assessment PDF]

If you want me to score your specific situation, fill this quick Fit Check:
[Link to Google Form]

I'll reply with whether PureBrain would be a fit and what that would look like for you.
```

**Step 5: Check new connections** (5 min)

For anyone who just connected:

**First message (not a pitch)**:
```
Thanks for connecting, [Name].

I noticed you post about [topic from their content]. Curious - [genuine question about their work/perspective]?
```

---

**Evening Block (10 min) - 5-6 PM:**

**Step 6: Update tracking sheet**

- Log comments left today
- Note connection request status
- Update any DM conversations
- Plan tomorrow's engagement targets
