## FIT CHECK FORM QUESTIONS

1. Company name (short answer)
2. Your role (Founder/CEO, CMO/VP, Operations, Other)
3. What's your biggest AI challenge? (Don't know where to start / Tried tools that didn't stick / Need to scale personal capacity / Want AI partnership like Aether)
4. Timeline to implementation (This month / This quarter / Exploring)
5. Have you named an AI before? (yes/no)
6. Best email to reach you
7. Anything else we should know? (optional)

### Scoring Logic

| Answer | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Founder/CEO | 25 |
| CMO/VP | 20 |
| "Want AI partnership like Aether" | 25 |
| "Need to scale personal capacity" | 20 |
| "This month" timeline | 20 |
| "This quarter" timeline | 15 |
| Has named an AI | 10 |

**Tiers:**
- 60+ = Tier A (Book call immediately)
- 35-59 = Tier B (Nurture sequence)
- <35 = Tier C (Send assessment only)
