## Instruction 7: Non-Compete / Non-Circumvent / Non-Disclosure (Section 8)

**Status: Complete — New Section 8 Added**

This is an entirely new section not present in the original agreement. Three components:

**8.1 Non-Compete (24 months post-termination)**
- Neither party will develop or distribute a directly competing AI partnership product
- Geographically limited to active markets under this agreement
- Standard severability carve-out if found overbroad

**8.2 Non-Circumvent (24 months post-termination)**
- Neither party will bypass the other to deal directly with clients or partners introduced through this agreement

**8.3–8.4 Non-Disclosure (5 years post-termination; trade secrets indefinite)**
- Covers: financial terms, customer data, technical architecture, trade secrets, business strategies
- Standard exceptions: public info, prior knowledge, independent development, written consent, legal compulsion
- Consistent with the original confidentiality section (Section 8 of original, now renumbered)

**8.5 Remedies**
- Injunctive relief available without bond, recognizing irreparable harm nature of violations
