## SECTION 1: PURPOSE AND NATURE OF RELATIONSHIP

The parties expressly intend that this Agreement be construed as a services and distribution agreement, and not as a partnership or joint venture under any applicable law.

## SECTION 2: COMMERCIAL MODEL OVERVIEW

### 2.1 Tier Offerings

**Post-Launch Price Increases:** All subscription tiers except Insiders will increase in price upon hard commercial launch, as follows. Insiders pricing is permanently fixed at $74.50/month and will not increase.

For the avoidance of doubt, customers who subscribe at pre-launch pricing retain their tier pricing unless they voluntarily upgrade. New subscriptions entered after the hard launch date are subject to post-launch pricing.

**Pricing Authority:** AiCIV retains sole authority to set base pricing and minimum price floors for all tiers. Pure Technology may set its own retail pricing at or above the applicable minimum floor but may not offer discounts exceeding ten percent (10%) without AiCIV's prior written consent.

### 2.2 Referral Reserve (5%) and Marketing Fund

Five percent (5%) of Collected MRR shall be deducted monthly before commission calculation. This Referral Reserve is allocated as follows:

- Qualified referral payouts under the referral program (terms to be mutually agreed in a Referral Program Addendum);
- Any balance not paid out as referrals in a given month is allocated to the joint Marketing Fund, subject to a mutually agreed approval process.

The governance of the Referral Program and Marketing Fund, including any joint bank account arrangement, shall be established by written addendum before the referral program launches.

### 2.3 Commission Structure

**Pre-1,000 Cumulative Sign-Ups:** AiCIV, Inc. receives **60%** of the Commission Base and Pure Technology Inc. receives **40%** of the Commission Base.

**After 1,000 Cumulative Sign-Ups:** The split transitions to **50% to AiCIV, Inc. and 50% to Pure Technology Inc.** for all subscribers going forward.

**Enterprise Deals:** Regardless of total sign-up count, enterprise deals shall **always be split 50/50** between AiCIV, Inc. and Pure Technology Inc.

"Cumulative Sign-Ups" means the total number of unique customer accounts that have subscribed to any paid tier of the Service since the Effective Date of this Agreement. The transition from 60/40 to 50/50 occurs automatically when the 1,000th cumulative sign-up is recorded, and applies to revenue earned from that point forward (not retroactively).

### 2.4 Definitions

**"Collected MRR":** Gross subscription revenue actually received from customers in a calendar month, net of chargebacks, refunds, and failed payments.

**"Referral Reserve":** Five percent (5%) of Collected MRR, allocated monthly per Section 2.2.

**"Commission Base":** Collected MRR less the Referral Reserve.

**"Distribution Commission":** Pure Technology's share of the Commission Base (40% pre-1,000 sign-ups; 50% post-1,000 sign-ups).

**"AiCIV Retained Revenue":** AiCIV's share of the Commission Base (60% pre-1,000 sign-ups; 50% post-1,000 sign-ups), retained by or remitted to AiCIV.

**"Infrastructure Service Fees":** Per-account amounts payable by Pure Technology to AiCIV for foundational technology infrastructure and operational support, as set forth in Section 5.

**"Hard Launch":** The date on which AiCIV officially launches the Service for general commercial availability, to be confirmed by AiCIV in writing to Pure Technology.

## SECTION 5: ROLES, RESPONSIBILITIES, AND REVENUE FLOW

### 5.1 AiCIV (Provider)

AiCIV is responsible for: Service development and delivery; infrastructure maintenance and uptime; customer onboarding; technical product roadmap; pricing authority and minimum price floor guidance; data security and privacy compliance; and all technical support for the Service.

### 5.2 Pure Technology (Distributor)

Pure Technology is responsible for: distribution; sales; campaign packaging; brand introductions; partner channel management; and customer acquisition through its established channels.

### 5.3 Revenue Collection and Remittance

Pure Technology shall collect subscription revenue from customers through its distribution channels. Within **ten (10) business days** after the end of each calendar month, Pure Technology shall remit to AiCIV:

**(a)** AiCIV's Retained Revenue (60% of the Commission Base, pre-1,000 sign-ups; or 50% of the Commission Base, post-1,000 sign-ups); **plus**

**(b)** Infrastructure Service Fees of **$20.00 per active customer account** for foundational technology infrastructure, **plus $15.00 per active customer account** for operational infrastructure support, totaling **$35.00 per active customer account per month**.

Pure Technology retains the Distribution Commission and the Referral Reserve per Section 2.2. For clarity, the Infrastructure Service Fees are payments from Pure Technology to AiCIV, in addition to AiCIV's Retained Revenue, reflecting AiCIV's cost of providing the underlying technology that enables each customer account.

The Infrastructure Service Fees amounts may be adjusted only by mutual written agreement of both parties, with no less than sixty (60) days' advance written notice to the other party. Neither party may unilaterally modify these amounts.

### 5.4 Automated Payment Infrastructure

The parties mutually intend to eventually automate payment distribution so that AiCIV, Inc. can receive daily or near-daily payouts rather than monthly remittance. The parties agree to work cooperatively to migrate from PayPal to a more suitable automated payment distribution infrastructure, such as Vantack.com or a comparable platform that enables programmatic revenue splitting at the point of payment.

Until such automated infrastructure is implemented:

- Pure Technology shall hold AiCIV's Retained Revenue and Infrastructure Service Fees in a **segregated account** and shall not commingle such funds with Pure Technology's general operating funds;
- Monthly remittance per Section 5.3 applies.

The timeline for implementing automated payment distribution shall be determined by mutual agreement of both parties based on operational feasibility, platform readiness, and technical requirements. Neither party shall be obligated to implement a specific platform without mutual written agreement.

### 5.5 Joint Responsibilities

Both parties collaborate on: referral program governance; Marketing Fund governance; and quarterly business reviews.

### 5.6 IP License

AiCIV grants Pure Technology a **non-exclusive, non-transferable, non-sublicensable** license to use AiCIV's trademarks, marketing materials, and product descriptions solely for the purpose of marketing and distributing the Service under this Agreement. This license is co-terminus with this Agreement and terminates automatically upon expiration or termination. Pure Technology's use of AiCIV's marks shall comply with AiCIV's brand guidelines as updated from time to time. AiCIV retains all right, title, and interest in its intellectual property.
