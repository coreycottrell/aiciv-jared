## Part 5: Lead Generation Systems to Replicate

### System 1: Comment-to-DM Pipeline

**How it works**:
1. Create valuable educational post
2. End with: "Comment '[KEYWORD]' and I'll send you [SPECIFIC RESOURCE]"
3. When comments come in, send DM with resource
4. Resource includes soft CTA for next step

**For Jared**: "Comment 'EXPERIENCE' for our Experiential Marketing Playbook"
**For PureBrain**: "Comment 'BRAIN' for our AI Productivity Checklist"

**Why it works**:
- Low friction (just a word)
- Builds connection list
- Starts conversation naturally
- Resource provides value + positions next step

### System 2: Engineered Conversations (50%+ Acceptance)

**6-Step Process**:
1. **Build target list** - Specific companies/roles (not random)
2. **Warm-up engagement** - Comment on their content 3-5 times over 2 weeks
3. **Content exposure** - Post content they'll see in their feed
4. **Qualify** - Score based on engagement, fit
5. **Personalize message** - Reference prior interaction
6. **Send native** - LinkedIn DM, human pacing

**Key insight**: "Relevance isn't created in the first message. It's created before the message is ever sent."

### System 3: Profile-as-Storefront

**Elements**:
1. Banner = billboard (Who/What/Proof/CTA)
2. Headline = value prop
3. About = sales page
4. Featured = portfolio/proof
5. Content = trust builder

**Result**: Every profile visitor gets clear conversion path

### System 4: Newsletter Conversion

**Process**:
1. Identify top-performing posts (2x average)
2. Expand into newsletter article (15 min conversion)
3. Newsletter drives to deeper offer
4. 80% of revenue potential from newsletter, not social posts

**Why it works**: Social = awareness, Newsletter = relationship, Offer = revenue

### System 5: Audit/Assessment Offer

**Template**:
"I'll audit your [THING] for free. Here's what you'll get:
- [Specific deliverable 1]
- [Specific deliverable 2]
- [Specific deliverable 3]

DM me 'AUDIT' if you want in."

**Claimed result**: 67% conversion from audit to paid engagement

**For Jared**: "Free Marketing Experience Audit - I'll show you 3 ways to engineer fascination instead of buying attention. DM 'AUDIT'"
