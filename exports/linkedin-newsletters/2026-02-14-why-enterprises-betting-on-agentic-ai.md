**What Agentic AI Actually Means**

Let's cut through the jargon.

**Traditional AI**: A tool you use. You ask questions, it answers.

**Agentic AI**: A system that works for you. You set goals, it figures out the steps.

Think of it this way:
→ Traditional AI = A really smart search engine
→ Agentic AI = A tireless team member who never sleeps

The 31% automation figure isn't about chatbots answering FAQs. It's about AI agents handling onboarding, processing invoices, qualifying leads, managing inventory—end to end.
