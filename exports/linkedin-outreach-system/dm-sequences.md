# PureBrain LinkedIn Outreach - DM Sequences

**Initiative**: 2 (LinkedIn Direct Outreach)
**Prepared by**: marketing-strategist
**Date**: 2026-02-24

---

## Sequence Overview

Each sequence below covers the full DM journey from first message through final follow-up. Three primary sequences are mapped to the three target variants (A, B, C from the roadmap). Two additional sequences handle edge cases: the warm referral introduction and the declined-affiliate-but-interested-in-product path.

All sequences use the same underlying logic:
1. Open with their world, not your product.
2. Name the specific problem before naming the solution.
3. Make the affiliate ask explicitly and early — do not hide it.
4. Make the next step tiny (15 minutes, not "a demo").
5. Follow up twice maximum. Respect their time.

---

## Sequence A: The AI Frustration Path

**Best for**: AI Strategy Consultants, Fractional Executives, Business Coaches who have posted about generic AI tools failing

**Persona signal to use this sequence**: Target has posted in last 30 days about AI tool limitations, generic AI failing clients, or frustration with context loss between sessions.

---

### Touch 1 (Day 8-10 after connection accepted)

```
[Name], I noticed your post about [specific AI tool/frustration they mentioned] —
that tension between generic AI tools and the actual nuance of [their specific work]
is something we've been building toward for a while.

We built PureBrain specifically for [coaches/consultants/advisors] who work with
clients where context matters — the tool learns the client's history, language,
and goals across every conversation. No re-explaining from scratch.

We're building a partner program for advisors who want to offer this to clients
and earn recurring income. 20% monthly recurring, no selling required — just
genuine recommendation if it fits your work.

Worth a 15-minute look? I can have Jared (founder) walk you through it directly.

— [Your name]
```

**Word count**: ~100 words. Short enough to read fully. Long enough to be credible.

**Personalization required**: [their specific AI frustration or tool mentioned] + [their role/work type]

---

### Touch 2 (5 days after Touch 1, if no response)

```
[Name], just following up to make sure this didn't get buried.

No pressure — if timing isn't right, I'm happy to reconnect down the road.

If you're curious, here's a short demo of what the experience looks like
for clients: [demo link or purebrain.ai/#awakening]

Happy to answer any questions here too if easier than a call.
```

**Word count**: ~55 words. Brevity here signals respect, not desperation.

**Tone note**: Do not repeat the affiliate pitch. Keep it light. The demo link does the work.

---

### Touch 3 (4 days after Touch 2, if still no response)

```
Last follow-up from me, [Name]. No response needed.

If PureBrain ever seems relevant for your clients, the partner page is at
purebrain.ai/partners.

One thing I'll leave you with: if you know anyone whose clients would benefit
from AI that actually learns them over time, happy to take an intro instead.
```

**Word count**: ~50 words. Clean close. The referral-of-referral ask preserves value even from non-conversions.

**After this**: Move to 90-day cold status in CRM. Do not send further messages.

## Sequence B: The Client Parallel Path

**Best for**: Executive Coaches, Fractional Executives, Leadership Coaches whose clients are the exact PureBrain ICP

**Persona signal**: Their bio or posts show they work with C-suite executives, business owners, or senior knowledge workers.

---

### Touch 1 (Day 8-10 after connection accepted)

```
[Name], you work with [executives/business owners/founders] — the exact people
who should have a persistent-memory AI partner, but most are using generic tools
that forget everything between sessions.

Quick context: PureBrain is a personalized AI that actually learns and remembers —
the difference between an AI that asks "what are your goals?" every session and
one that already knows your Q1 targets, your communication style, and the three
blockers you've been working through for six months.

We're quietly building a partner program for advisors who serve this audience.
Recurring commission, no sales pressure, and the product does the convincing on
its own once clients try it.

If your clients would benefit, happy to connect you with Jared directly for a
15-minute conversation.
```

**Word count**: ~110 words.

**Personalization required**: [their specific client type — executives, founders, etc.]

**Tone note**: "Quietly building" signals selectivity without false scarcity. It also implies they are being invited in early, which is true.

---

### Touch 2 (5 days after Touch 1, if no response)

```
[Name], following up in case this got lost.

The short version: we have a recurring affiliate program for advisors whose
clients are senior professionals using AI tools. 20% monthly, no sales
requirement.

If you want to see what the client experience looks like before deciding:
[demo link]

No obligation at all — happy to answer questions here if easier.
```

**Word count**: ~55 words.

---

### Touch 3 (4 days after Touch 2, if still no response)

```
Closing the loop, [Name] — no reply needed.

If you ever have clients struggling with AI tools that "forget" them between
sessions, purebrain.ai/partners is the place.

And if you know a colleague whose clients are in that situation, happy to
take an intro instead. Thanks for the connection.
```

**Word count**: ~50 words.

## Sequence C: The Content Creator Path

**Best for**: Business Coaches and Consultants with larger LinkedIn audiences (5,000+ followers), newsletter publishers, or those who regularly recommend tools to their audience

**Persona signal**: Active content creator, posts 3x+ per week, has a newsletter or course, follows or mentions affiliate/ambassador work.

---

### Touch 1 (Day 8-10 after connection accepted)

```
[Name], I follow your content on [specific topic they post about] — it's one of
the clearer voices in [their space].

I have a somewhat direct pitch: PureBrain is building a partner program for
creators whose audiences are knowledge workers and business professionals.
20% recurring commission, and more importantly — it's something genuinely
worth recommending.

The product is a personalized AI that remembers everything across sessions,
learns how you think, and becomes more useful the longer you use it. The
opposite of generic AI tools.

No obligation — happy to send you access to try it yourself first before
deciding if it's a fit for your audience.
```

**Word count**: ~105 words.

**Personalization required**: [their specific content topic] + [their audience type]

**Key differentiator from A and B**: This sequence leads with the trial access offer. Creators need to experience the product before they recommend it. The offer to "try it yourself first" respects this and signals product confidence.

---

### Touch 2 (5 days after Touch 1, if no response)

```
[Name], just making sure this didn't fall through the cracks.

Happy to send a trial link with no strings — just want you to experience
PureBrain before deciding if it's relevant for your audience.

No need for a call. If you want access: [trial link or reply here]
```

**Word count**: ~45 words.

**Note**: This sequence's Touch 2 is the most frictionless of all three — direct trial access with no call required. Creators respond to low-friction paths.

---

### Touch 3 (4 days after Touch 2, if still no response)

```
Last note from me, [Name].

If PureBrain ever feels relevant for your audience, partner page is
at purebrain.ai/partners and trial access at [trial link].

Thanks for the connection — your content on [topic] is consistently good.
```

**Word count**: ~40 words.

**Tone note**: End with a genuine compliment referencing their specific content. Not flattery — specific appreciation. This closes the relationship positively regardless of outcome.

## Sequence D: The Warm Referral Path

**Use when**: Someone referred you to this contact. You can mention the referrer's name.

**Touch 1 is different** from cold sequences — the referral name does the opening work.

---

### Touch 1 (Day 1-2 after connection accepted — skip pre-warm for warm referrals)

```
[Name], [Referrer name] connected us — they thought our work might be
complementary given you both work with [similar audience/domain].

Quick context: I'm [Jared/working with Jared] at PureBrain, a personalized
AI platform built for [their specific audience]. We have a partner program
[Referrer name] was generous enough to mention to you.

If you want to explore: 15 minutes with Jared, completely informal. He
can show you the product and answer questions about the program.

Either way, glad to be connected — [Referrer name] has good judgment.
```

**Word count**: ~90 words.

**Note**: Warm referrals should receive a response within the first DM exchange 40-60% of the time. If no response after Touch 1, use standard Touch 2 and Touch 3 from whichever primary sequence fits.

## Sequence E: The Declined-Affiliate, Interested-in-Product Path

**Use when**: Target responds and declines the affiliate program but asks questions about PureBrain itself.

This is not a failed outreach — this is a direct buyer in disguise. Switch tracks.

---

### Response to Decline + Product Interest

```
[Name], completely understood on the affiliate program — no pressure there.

The questions about PureBrain itself — happy to answer those here, or if
easier, Jared does 15-minute product walkthroughs for people who are curious
about using it for their own work.

Short version: [one sentence of the value prop most relevant to what they asked].
[Trial link] if you want to try it yourself.
```

**Personalization required**: The one-sentence value prop should directly address whatever they said they were curious about. Do not use a generic line.

---

### If They Take the Trial

Move them to the subscriber pipeline immediately. Send a welcome message 48 hours after they start the trial:

```
[Name], glad you're trying it out.

One thing that makes a difference in the first week: [specific power prompt or
first session recommendation from the PureBrain onboarding sequence].

Let me know what you notice after the first few sessions — I'm genuinely
curious what someone with your background sees in it.
```

**This message serves two purposes**: Increases product activation (reduces churn) and keeps the relationship warm enough for an organic affiliate conversation later if they become a satisfied customer.

## DM Timing Rules

| Situation | Timing |
|-----------|--------|
| After connection accepted | Wait 24-48 hours, then Touch 1 |
| After Touch 1 with no response | 5 business days |
| After Touch 2 with no response | 4 business days |
| After Touch 3 (close the loop) | Do not send more messages |
| Cold status duration | 90 days minimum |
| After they engage with your content organically | Can reach out again regardless of cold status |

---

## Response Handling Guide

**They respond positively and book a call**: Log in CRM as "Call Scheduled." Jared handles the call. You send a brief confirmation message with Calendly link or calendar invite.

**They respond with questions but no commitment**: Answer directly in DMs. Do not push for a call. Let them get comfortable. Offer trial access as low-friction next step.

**They say they are not interested**: "Thanks for letting me know, [Name] — appreciate the response. If you know anyone whose clients would benefit from AI that learns them over time, happy to take an intro." Then close the loop in CRM.

**They are rude or dismissive**: Do not respond. Log as "Declined - DNR" (Do Not Re-contact). Move on.

**They ask for more time**: "Of course — I'll check back in [X weeks]." Log the date. Follow up once at that date with a brief note. Only once.
