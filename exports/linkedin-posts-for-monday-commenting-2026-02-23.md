## Commenting Best Practices

1. **Comment within 1 hour of opening LinkedIn** - early comments get more visibility
   on high-engagement posts

2. **Length sweet spot**: 3-5 sentences. Long enough to show genuine thought,
   short enough to be read

3. **Formula that works**: Validate their insight + add your real-world experience +
   ask a specific question. Questions drive replies and keep the thread alive.

4. **Avoid**: Generic praise ("Great post!" "So true!"). These get buried and don't
   build your profile.

5. **Your PureBrain angle**: You're the practitioner. They're the theorists.
   Your comments should add field-level observations they can't have. This is your edge.

6. **If a post is older than 3 days**: Still comment, but prioritize any posts from
   the past 24-48 hours for maximum algorithmic boost.
