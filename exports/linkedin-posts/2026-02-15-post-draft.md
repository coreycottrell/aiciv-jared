"Yesterday I tracked every task" is specific, personal, and implies data. Research shows posts starting with specific timeframes outperform generic statements.

- Uses arrow (->) formatting for scannability
- Time comparisons create dopamine hits (readers love before/after)
- Table alternative format works better on mobile than actual tables

"Outsourced your thinking" - acknowledges the objection many readers are already thinking, then reframes it. This drives comments from both agreement and disagreement.
