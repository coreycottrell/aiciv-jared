Your company is spending $85,521/month on AI tools.

A third of that value evaporates before anyone does real work.

Here's the math nobody wants to do:

46.5% of workers switch between 2+ AI tools just to complete ONE task. Every switch means re-explaining who you are, what your company does, and what you've already tried.

Speakwise measured it: employees lose nearly 4 hours per week just reorienting after tool switches. That's 5 full working weeks per year — gone.

Not to bugs. Not to downtime. To amnesia.

We call it the Context Tax: the cumulative cost of AI tools that start every interaction from zero.

And it's worse than wasted time.

Think about your best senior employee. On day one, they were competent but generic. By year two, they had institutional judgment no competitor could replicate.

Now imagine wiping their memory every morning.

That's what stateless AI does to your organization. Every. Single. Session.

76% of enterprises have already experienced negative outcomes from disconnected AI tools. Not because the tools don't work — because they don't remember.

The model layer is approaching commodity. GPT, Claude, Gemini — the performance gaps close faster than procurement cycles can track.

When capability is commodity, differentiation shifts to context.

The companies that own their categories in 2027 are the ones that stopped paying the Context Tax in 2026.
