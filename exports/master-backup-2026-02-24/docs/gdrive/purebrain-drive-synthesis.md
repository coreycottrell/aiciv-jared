## 1. Who Pure Technology Is

**Pure Technology Inc.** was established in December 2017 by Jared Sanborn.

**Core Business**: Pure Technology is a data aggregation company that provides complete consumer profiles to brands and companies, enabling superior ROI on market research, marketing, and advertising.

**The Fundamental Problem They Solve**: The market research, marketing, and advertising industries have enormous budget waste problems - not enough data, falsified data, stale data, incorrect data, too much wrong data. Pure Technology exists to end this via innovation and aggregation.

**Key Product (Historical Thesis)**: Phree Phones (smartphones given free to consumers in exchange for data access) + DiMAP (Data Intelligence Marketing & Analytics Platform) - a complete consumer data profile that no other platform has. Google knows what you search; Facebook knows who you are; Amazon knows what you buy. DiMAP pieces the puzzle together as ONE complete consumer picture.

**Current Focus**: Pure Technology is actively building PureBrain (AI partnership platform) through Aether and the AI team, while PMG (Pure Marketing Group) serves as the revenue-generating, proof-generating services arm.

**Core Philosophy**: Quality Over Quantity. Pure Technology's entire model is the polar opposite of Google/Facebook/Amazon. They genuinely care whether clients get ROI. "If our price was twice our competitors, our clients still get the best deal."

**Blue Ocean Strategy**: Every 3-5 years, what you know will be obsolete. Goal: ~25% of revenue always comes from projects developed in the last 24 months. "Put yourself out of business before someone else does."

## 12. LinkedIn Strategy

### LinkedIn Hook Frameworks (2026)

**1. The Specificity Effect Hook**:
Formula: `[Specific Pain Point] + [Quantified Transformation] + [Timeframe]`
Example: "I stopped chasing MQLs like it's 2015. One shift in our intent-data filter added $400k to the pipeline in 22 days."
Why: Detail too specific to be AI-generated fluff = instant credibility.

**2. Contrarian Pattern Interrupt**:
Formula: `[Bold Disagreement with Best Practice] + [Counter-Intuitive Result]`
Example: "Hard truth: Your customers don't care about your new AI features. They care about getting to dinner by 6:00 PM."
Why: Creates "cognitive itch" - brain cannot scroll past contradicting beliefs.

**3. Timeframe Tension Hook**:
Formula: `[Long-term Struggle] vs [Short-term Insight]`
Example: "3 years of back-end mess solved in a 30-minute audit."
Why: Promises "dopamine hit" of fast information.

**4. Vulnerable Authority Hook**:
Formula: `[Raw Admission of Failure] + [The Pivot Moment] + [The Lesson]`
Example: "I almost quit Puremarketing last November."
Why: Lowers reader defenses; feels authentic in 2026's "polished = fake" environment.

### LinkedIn Profile Audit Framework

**AUTOMATIC FAIL CONDITIONS** (score = 0):
- No profile photo
- No headline
- Under 50 connections

**Scoring Sections** (0, 5, or 10 points each):
- Photo: Human face 60%+ of circle, solid color background, looking at camera, professional lighting
- Headline: First 40 chars state clear outcome (10 pts) vs just role (5) vs generic (0)
- Featured: 1-3 tiles with 1200x628px visuals + clear CTA (10) vs wrong dimensions (5) vs none (0)
- About: Opens with metric/proof in first line + has CTA (10) vs story no proof (5) vs missing (0)
- Experience: Current role has bullets with numbers/results (10) vs no metrics (5) vs title only (0)
- Connections: Shows 200-499 exactly = 10pts (sweet spot of "growing not massive")

**Decision Score**: 60 total points
- 3-second test: Photo/Headline/Connections (first 30 pts)
- 30-second test: Featured/About/Experience (last 30 pts)
