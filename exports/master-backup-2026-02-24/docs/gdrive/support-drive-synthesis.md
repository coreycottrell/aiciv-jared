## SYNTHESIS SUMMARY

Pure Technology Inc. (doing business as Pure Marketing Group, Pure Influence, PureBrain.ai) is Jared Sanborn's company. It began in 2019 as **Eyefuel PR** - a New York City-based Instagram growth and social media marketing agency. Over seven years it evolved into a full-service digital marketing group with multiple brand divisions, a Philippines-based remote team, and now an AI-powered client service platform called PureBrain.ai.

**The company today**: A marketing agency/technology company hybrid based in Montclair, NJ (25 Prospect Avenue) that sells:
1. Press placement in major publications (Elle, Forbes, Billboard, etc.)
2. Social media management and growth services
3. Fractional CMO services for companies needing marketing leadership
4. Digital marketing (SEO, paid ads, content)
5. AI-powered marketing intelligence (PureBrain.ai - newest offering)

**The core business model**: White-label/reseller at scale. PMG buys press placements and social media services wholesale, marks them up 25-40%, and sells to B2C customers and reseller agencies at a premium.
