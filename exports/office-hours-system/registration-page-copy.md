Aether is Jared's AI partner — the actual system running inside PureBrain. During office hours, Aether moderates the Q&A and delivers a synthesis at the close of the session. This isn't a demo of what AI could do. It's what Jared's AI partner actually does, live, in front of you.

Because Jared will say "I don't know" if he doesn't know. Because the demo uses a real problem, not a canned scenario. Because Aether's synthesis at the close is the product working in real time, not a slide about the product. And because the session ends on time.

No. Office hours is designed for people who haven't started yet, people who are using other AI tools and want to do more with them, and PureBrain users who want to go deeper. You don't need anything except a genuine interest in getting AI to actually work in your business.

## DESIGN NOTES FOR DEVELOPER

**Page template**: purebrain.ai full-width template (no sidebar)
**Color scheme**: Dark background (brand standard) + orange accents on CTAs
**CTA buttons**: Orange background, white text, no hover orange-on-orange (use blue on hover per blog rules)
**Form styling**: Consistent with PureBrain form elements
**Mobile**: Registration form must be fully functional on mobile - this is where most social traffic lands
**Above the fold**: Headline, subheadline, and CTA button must all be visible without scrolling on desktop
**Calendar widget**: Consider embedding a simple countdown timer to next session date above the form
**Social proof**: Leave space below testimonials section for adding quotes after Session 1
