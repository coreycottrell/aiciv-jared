**Topic**: "AI That Knows Your Business: Building a Context Layer That Makes Every Tool Smarter"

**Strategic rationale**: Sessions 1 and 2 addressed the individual skill gap (Director vs User) and the memory problem. Session 3 addresses the organizational/systemic layer — building AI that is specific to your business. This is the graduation from "AI user" to "AI system builder." Maximum relevance to coaches, consultants, and team leads.

**Presentation outline**:
- Why generic AI gives generic results (2 min — brief, attendees know this by now)
- What a Context Layer is: the document/system that tells your AI everything it needs to know about your business before you ask it anything (5 min)
- What goes in a Context Layer: 7 categories (5 min)

  2. Your language, voice, vocabulary
  3. Your current priorities and goals
  4. Your current client roster and status

## TOPIC SELECTION PRINCIPLES

When adding topics beyond this calendar, use these criteria:

1. **Does it address a real frustration, not a nice-to-have?** (Frustrations convert; nice-to-haves don't)
2. **Can Aether demonstrate the answer live?** (Every session needs a demo moment)
3. **Is there a natural next step tied to this topic?** (The offer must feel like the logical continuation)
4. **Does this build on what came before?** (Sessions should feel like a curriculum, not a podcast)
5. **Would someone share this with a colleague?** (Shareability is the growth mechanism)
