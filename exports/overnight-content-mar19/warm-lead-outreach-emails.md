## Voice Notes (Before You Send)

Jared's voice has five fingerprints. Keep them all:

1. **First names only** — no "Hi there," never "Dear [Name]"
2. **One-sentence paragraphs** — reads like a text, not an essay
3. **Exclamation points used sparingly but genuinely** — not on every line
4. **Social proof is experiential** — "people are naming their AIs," not "clients report 40% ROI"
5. **Warmth at the close** — always ends with something human

## PS Line Options (Add to Any Version)

Pick the one that fits where the lead is:

**Urgency — spots:**
> P.S. We're keeping this group tight at the start. Spots are limited and I want to make sure you're in before we close it.

**Urgency — price:**
> P.S. The price is going up soon. This is the lowest it'll ever be — wanted you to have access at this rate.

**Social proof kicker:**
> P.S. One of our members named their AI "Marcus" and now runs their entire client pipeline through it. I want that for you too.

**Curiosity:**
> P.S. If you're on the fence, just go look at the page. You'll understand immediately why people aren't looking back.
