## Executive Summary

LinkedIn has fundamentally changed its distribution model in the past 30 days. The algorithm now runs on 360Brew — a transformer-based AI model that measures dwell time, saves, and substantive comments over vanity signals. This creates an exceptional window for Jared: the algorithm rewards exactly what he uniquely has — verifiable, operational AI builder content that no one else can replicate. Jared's PureBrain story (a live, multi-agent AI system running a real company in real-time) is the most differentiated narrative available in the AI space on LinkedIn. The company page (PureBrain) should function as a support vehicle for Jared's personal brand, not compete with it. This brief recommends a "saves-first" content architecture: every post designed to be bookmarked and returned to, not just liked.

## Strategy: PureBrain Company LinkedIn Page

### Role Clarity

The PureBrain company page is a **credibility anchor**, not a primary content channel. Personal brand reaches 500x more people organically. Invest 20% of LinkedIn time here, 80% on Jared's personal profile.

### What the Company Page Should Do

1. **Amplify Jared's personal posts** — company page shares Jared's top posts within 2 hours of publishing (employee share = 8x amplification)
2. **Thought Leader Ads** — use PureBrain ad account to amplify Jared's best-performing posts as TLA ads (2.68% CTR vs 0.42% for standard ads, $2.29 CPC)
3. **Social proof hub** — testimonials, case studies, product updates (not opinion content)
4. **Employee advocacy seed** — any team members, even contractors, share company content
5. **Company updates** — pricing changes, feature launches, PureBrain milestones

### What the Company Page Should NOT Do

- Post opinion/thought leadership content (personal brand wins)
- Compete with Jared's content calendar
- Include external links in posts (same penalty applies)
- Post more than 2x per week (diminishing returns)

### Company Page Content Mix (2x/week)

- Monday: Social proof (customer result, case study snippet, testimonial)
- Thursday: Product/company update (feature launch, milestone, team news)

### Aether on the Company Page

Aether cannot have a standalone LinkedIn profile (confirmed TOS violation). Compliant options:
- Posts authored by Jared on the company page labeled "Written by Aether, PureBrain's AI Partner"
- "Aether's Take" as a recurring series under the company page
- Jared's personal posts regularly reference Aether by name (builds familiarity)
