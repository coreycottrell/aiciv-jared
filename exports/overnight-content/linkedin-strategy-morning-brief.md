## Lead Generation Plays (Ranked by Expected Impact)

### Play 1: Comment-to-DM Automation (HIGHEST ROI - Deploy This Week)
**What it is**: Post content that instructs people to comment a keyword (e.g., "AUDIT"), then a tool automatically DMs them the promised resource.
**Expected result**: 20-40% comment-to-DM conversion on posts with 50+ comments
**Why it works**: Eliminates all friction. No form fill. No email required. Prospect self-identifies AND gives you DM permission.

**Setup**:
1. Choose a tool: Expandi, PhantomBuster, or MeetAlfred all have 2026 comment-to-DM capability
2. Set trigger keyword: "AUDIT" or "ASSESS" (professional keywords that filter intent)
3. Create the DM template:
   > "Hey [First Name], here's the AI Partnership Self-Assessment you asked for: [link]. Takes 5 minutes. Happy to walk through the results with you if useful - just reply here."
4. Create the post that drives comments:
   > "If you want an honest look at where your AI partnership actually stands vs. where you think it stands, comment AUDIT below. I'll send you our assessment tool."

**Cost**: $50-100/month for automation tool. Zero ad spend required.

**Important**: 2026 LinkedIn AI intent detection means people don't even need to use your exact keyword. Tools like Expandi detect "I'd love that" or "send it" and still trigger the DM.

---

### Play 2: Thought Leader Ads - Organic Post Amplification (HIGH ROI)
**What it is**: You take your best organic post and put $50-200 behind it as a Thought Leader Ad
**Expected result**: CPC of $1-3 (median $2.29), vs $22+ for standard company ads. 1.7x higher CTR than company ads.
**Real case study found**: One AI services client closed a $120K deal from $2,035 in Thought Leader Ad spend.
**Best post to boost**: Your Tuesday carousel (highest organic engagement of all formats)

**Targeting for Jared's ICP**:
- Job titles: CEO, COO, VP of Operations, Chief Digital Officer, VP of Technology
- Company size: 50-500 employees (small enough to not have enterprise AI teams, large enough to need them)
- Industries: Professional Services, Consulting, Healthcare Administration, Financial Services
- Geography: Start with US only

**Budget recommendation**: $50/week to test. Scale posts that convert. Cut posts that don't within 72 hours.

### Play 3: LinkedIn Newsletter as Consultation Pipeline (MEDIUM ROI, COMPOUNDING)
**Current performance benchmark**: LinkedIn newsletters deliver 25-35% open rates vs. 21% for email average
**Scale benchmark**: 500 engaged newsletter subscribers + 3% consultation inquiry rate = 15 warm consultation conversations per month

**The pipeline**:
1. Post content on feed -> drives newsletter subscribers
2. Newsletter provides deep value -> builds trust + familiarity
3. Within newsletter, include one soft CTA per issue:
   > "Working on your own AI partnership and want a second set of eyes? Reply to this email."
4. Replies are warm leads - they know your thinking, they opted in, they reached out

**Growth lever**: LinkedIn's paid newsletter promotion = 300-500 new subscribers per $500 ad spend. This is the single most cost-effective list-building mechanism on any platform right now.

**Guest newsletter collaboration**: Identify 10-20 LinkedIn newsletter creators in adjacent spaces (digital transformation, future of work, business leadership) with 3,000-10,000 subscribers. Propose a guest swap. Each collaboration yields 50-200 new subscribers.

---

### Play 4: LinkedIn Events + Live Q&A (MEDIUM ROI, BRAND BUILDING)
**What it is**: Host a 30-minute LinkedIn Live titled something like "AI Partnership Office Hours - Ask Me Anything"
**Why**: Event registrations = declared intent leads. LinkedIn notifies all your followers about the event.
**Conversion mechanism**: During the Live, mention "I'll follow up with everyone who registered with our AI Partnership Assessment - no obligation."
**Result**: You get permission to DM every registrant.

**Logistics**:
- Create the event 5-7 days in advance
- Post about it twice (announcement + day-before reminder)
- Keep it casual, no slides, just conversation
- 30 minutes maximum - professional audiences won't commit to more

**Expected attendance**: 50-200 live viewers for accounts in the 2,000-10,000 follower range.
**Lead capture**: Every registrant is a warm outbound target.

---

### Play 5: Pre-Warm Prospect Sequence (STEADY-STATE PROCESS)
**The 2026 "Pre-Warm" methodology that outperforms cold DM by 2x**:

Step 1: Identify your top 50 prospects (use LinkedIn Sales Navigator to filter by ICP)
Step 2: Follow them without connecting (less defensive trigger)
Step 3: Ring their notification bell (see their posts first)
Step 4: Leave 2-3 thoughtful, expertise-demonstrating comments on their content over 2-3 weeks
Step 5: THEN send connection request with no note (68% acceptance rate for blank requests vs. 32% with a message)
Step 6: After they accept, wait 3-7 days before first DM

**Timing**: This takes 3-4 weeks per prospect. But by the time you DM, they recognize your name, they've seen your thinking, and you're not cold.

**Volume**: Work 10-15 prospects per week through this process. At 30-40% DM response rate = 3-6 conversations per week consistently.
