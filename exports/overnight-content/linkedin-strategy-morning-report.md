## Part 1: Top 10 Actionable Improvements - Jared's Personal LinkedIn

### Improvement 1: Fix the Link Penalty (Do This Today)
**The Problem**: Posts with external URLs in the body receive 60% less algorithmic reach. This is one of LinkedIn's most consistent penalties and has been confirmed across dozens of studies.

**The Fix**: Never put a link in your post body. Instead:
- Write your full post with no URL
- Publish
- Immediately go to your own post and leave the first comment containing the link
- Pin the comment if possible

**Impact**: Immediately improves reach on every future post. Zero creative effort required.

**Example**: Post ends with "I broke down the 5 key patterns below." Then first comment: "Full breakdown here: [URL] - drop a comment if you want the PDF version too."
