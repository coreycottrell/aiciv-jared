### 2.2 Paid Channels

**Philosophy**: Do not run paid ads until the organic conversion path is proven. Run paid only to amplify what already converts organically. Based on current state, paid should begin Month 2 after the About Aether page is live and blog-to-trial conversion is measured.

#### LinkedIn Ads (Month 2 Structured Test)

**Test structure:**
- Budget: $300 total ($100/week x 3 weeks)
- Objective: Website visits (not brand awareness) to the AI Partnership Audit page
- Targeting: Job title = VP Marketing, CMO, Director of Marketing, Brand Manager; Company size = 50-500 employees; US only
- Ad format: Single image ad with one data point and one action
- Headline options to test: "Your AI should remember you by name." / "95% of AI pilots fail. Here's what the 5% did differently." / "What if your AI partner knew your entire history?"
- Landing page: AI Partnership Audit (lead magnet page), not homepage
- Success metric: Cost per audit download under $15

**When to scale:**
- If cost per audit download is under $15 and audit-to-trial rate is above 10%, scale to $500/week
- If CPL is above $25, pause and diagnose landing page or targeting before scaling

#### Google Ads (Month 2, Limited Test)

**Target keywords only:**
- "AI implementation consultant" (high intent, low competition)
- "AI partner for my business" (category-defining)
- "why do AI tools fail" (problem-aware stage)
- "PureBrain" brand terms (protect against competitor bidding)

**Budget**: $200/month max in test phase
**Landing page**: Assessment quiz (intent-matching) or comparison page

#### Retargeting (Month 2 Setup)

**Audience segments:**
- Blog readers who have not subscribed (retarget with lead magnet ad)
- Lead magnet downloaders who have not started trial (retarget with testimonial or case study ad)
- Trial starters who have not converted to paid (retarget with testimonial or urgency ad)

**Platform**: LinkedIn retargeting pixel on all purebrain.ai pages
**Budget**: $150/month (separate from acquisition)
**Creative**: Social proof-first (quote from a customer, or from Aether with a metric)

### 2.4 Automated Lead Generation Systems

These are systems that run without daily human intervention once built.

#### System 1: Automated Prospect Identification

**What it does**: AI monitors Bluesky, Reddit, Quora, and LinkedIn for people expressing frustration with AI tool memory, repetition, lack of personalization, or ChatGPT/Claude context loss. Identifies high-intent prospects. Drafts personalized outreach. Jared reviews batches once per week (30 minutes).

**Trigger signals to monitor:**
- "my AI doesn't remember" / "keeps forgetting"
- "I have to re-explain everything to ChatGPT"
- "AI tools feel generic"
- "I need an AI that knows my business"
- "AI pilot failed" / "AI implementation issues"
- "switching from [competitor]"

**Weekly output target**: 20-50 warm contacts per week
**Build timeline**: Month 2 (Aether builds the monitoring agent using bsky-manager + web-researcher)

#### System 2: Chatbot Funnel (Site-Wide Exit Intent)

**What it does**: When a visitor has spent 60+ seconds on any purebrain.ai page and shows exit intent (or scrolls to bottom without converting), a chat interface appears with a single question: "What brought you to PureBrain today?" Answers route the visitor to the appropriate CTA.

**Routing logic:**
- "Tired of my AI forgetting things" → Lead to memory comparison page
- "Looking for a smarter AI" → Lead to Assessment quiz
- "Heard about it from someone" → Lead to About Aether page
- "Just exploring" → Lead to lead magnet
- "Ready to try it" → Direct to trial

**Build**: Aether builds this using PureBrain's existing chatbox infrastructure (already deployed on-site)
**Expected outcome**: 5-15% of exit intent visitors convert to email capture

#### System 3: Lead Magnet Nurture Sequences

**Currently built but not all live in Brevo:**
- AI Partnership Audit download → 5-email audit nurture sequence (status: built, Brevo status unknown)
- Assessment quiz completion → personalized results email + 3-email sequence (status: built, Brevo status unknown)
- Neural Feed subscribe → 7-email welcome sequence (status: LIVE and active)
- Migration sequences (ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini) → 4-email each (status: built, NOT live in Brevo)

**This week**: marketing-automation-specialist verifies which sequences are live in Brevo and activates any that are built but not running

**New sequence to build (Month 1):**
- Re-engagement sequence for Neural Feed subscribers 60+ days inactive
- Referral program announcement sequence for all paying customers
- Post-trial expiry sequence for users who started trial but did not convert

#### System 4: Content Syndication Engine

**What it does**: Every blog post automatically gets:
1. Reformatted for Medium (posted with canonical link back to purebrain.ai)
2. Reformatted for LinkedIn Articles (condensed, posted with canonical link)
3. Bluesky thread created by bsky-manager
4. LinkedIn post drafted by linkedin-writer
5. Key stat extracted and formatted as stat card for Instagram/visual social

**Current state**: Steps 3 and 4 are happening. Steps 1, 2, and 5 are not.
**This month**: Set up Medium account, establish LinkedIn Articles posting cadence, create stat card template

#### System 5: Social Proof Loop

**What it does**: Automatically collects, formats, and surfaces customer proof that converts skeptics.

**Input sources:**
- Post-onboarding email at Day 7: "What's one thing Aether has helped you figure out this week?" (Brevo automation)
- Day 30 email: "Would you be willing to share what's changed in how you work with AI?" (testimonial request)
- In-dashboard feedback prompt at milestone moments

**Output:**
- Raw quotes formatted as testimonial cards (Aether generates the card, Jared approves)
- Case study one-pagers (monthly, one per customer willing to be named)
- LinkedIn post series: "What PureBrain customers are saying" (once per month)
- Rotating testimonials on homepage, pricing page, and lead magnet pages

**Build timeline**: Day 7 automation this week, Day 30 Month 2, case study template Month 2
