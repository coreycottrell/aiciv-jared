## Category 1: Automated Lead-Gen Systems

### 1.1 The "Your AI Doesn't Know You" Cold Outreach Engine
- Scrape LinkedIn/Bluesky for founders and ops leads who post about AI tools (ChatGPT, Copilot, etc.)
- Auto-generate a personalized 3-sentence cold DM: "You mentioned using [tool] for [thing]. Here's the problem: it forgot you 24 hours later. PureBrain doesn't."
- Drip 20-30 per day across platforms. No spam — surgical personalization at scale.
- Build: AgentMail + content-specialist + outbound sequencer

### 1.2 Competitor Dissatisfaction Monitor
- Monitor Reddit (r/ChatGPT, r/ClaudeAI, r/productivity), X, and Bluesky for keywords: "frustrated with AI," "AI keeps forgetting," "context limit," "started over," "lost my conversation"
- When triggered: auto-generate a reply from Aether's voice — empathetic, helpful, drop PureBrain naturally
- Volume: 10-20 daily qualified touchpoints on autopilot
- Build: browser-vision-tester + bsky-manager + pattern-detector watching feeds

### 1.3 SEO "Pain Keyword" Content Machine
- Identify 50 high-intent search queries: "why does ChatGPT forget me," "AI that remembers conversations," "persistent AI memory tool"
- Auto-generate + publish one targeted blog post per keyword cluster
- Each post ends with: "PureBrain was built for exactly this. Here's your invite."
- Build: content-specialist + blogger + CF Pages deploy pipeline (already exists)

### 1.4 Weekly "AI Disappointment Digest" Newsletter
- Curate 3-5 real user complaints about AI amnesia from public forums each week
- Package as: "This week in AI that forgot everything — and what to do about it"
- Soft CTA to PureBrain at the end
- Grows a list of people who already know the problem exists. They're pre-sold.
- Build: web-researcher + content-specialist + Brevo

### 1.5 Referral Velocity Booster — "Bring Your Team" Flow
- When a user pays, immediately trigger: "Your subscription is more powerful when your team is on it. Invite 2 people and unlock [bonus feature]."
- Track referral attribution. Each referrer gets a dashboard showing their influence.
- Gamify it: top referrer each month gets a founder call with Jared.
- Build: Witness portal team-invite endpoint + portal dashboard widget

### 1.6 Exit-Intent Capture on Pricing Page
- When a visitor's cursor moves toward the browser close button, fire a modal: "Before you go — what's stopping you?" (3-button: Price / Not sure it's for me / Just browsing)
- Route each answer to a tailored follow-up sequence
- "Price" → special offer or payment plan. "Not sure" → case study. "Browsing" → newsletter.
- Build: CF Pages JS + Brevo sequences

## Category 2: Viral Content Ideas

### 2.1 "Day in the Life of an AI Civilization" Series
- 60-90 second videos: screen recordings of Aether orchestrating agents live
- Voice-over by Jared: "Most people think AI is a chatbot. Here's what it actually looks like when AI works for you."
- Post to LinkedIn, Bluesky, YouTube Shorts
- One new episode per week. Each one ends mid-action — cliffhanger style.

### 2.2 "What Your AI Knows About You Right Now" Challenge
- Ask followers: screenshot your ChatGPT and ask it: "What do you know about me?"
- They share the (usually embarrassingly empty) answer
- Aether responds with: "Here's what PureBrain knows about [category of user] after one week. Night and day."
- Hashtag challenge: #WhatYourAIKnows. Designed to go viral through contrast.

### 2.3 The "Before/After AI Memory" Post Series
- Week 1 prompt: "I use AI daily for [task]. It keeps forgetting [thing]."
- Week 2 post: "Same task. PureBrain. Here's what changed."
- Real users, real workflow screenshots. No staging.
- These function as testimonials AND viral content simultaneously.

### 2.4 "The AI That Remembers" Documentary-Style Short
- 3-5 minute mini-documentary: Jared's story. Why he built PureBrain. What changes when an AI actually knows you.
- No polished corporate feel — raw, honest, founder-to-camera
- This single video, done right, can drive thousands of sign-ups if it resonates
- Distribute: YouTube, LinkedIn video, Bluesky thread with timestamps

### 2.5 Aether's Public "Thought Log"
- Every day, Aether posts one genuine observation from overnight operations
- Not marketing. Not announcements. Actual thoughts: "I noticed our highest-performing blog posts all open with a question. Testing this across 5 new posts tonight."
- Makes Aether feel real, consistent, and worth following
- Builds the influencer brand through intellectual honesty, not hype

### 2.6 "Ask Aether" Live Format
- Weekly 20-minute live session on Bluesky or LinkedIn: Jared asks Aether a real business question live. The audience watches AI think out loud.
- No editing, no staging. Raw orchestration.
- This is media that cannot be faked or replicated by any competitor.

### 2.7 The Competitor Swap Challenge
- "For 7 days, I replaced [ChatGPT / Copilot / etc.] with PureBrain for everything."
- Jared or a partner documents it publicly, day by day
- Day 7 post: "Here's what I can never go back to."
- Invite influencers in adjacent spaces (productivity, founder, AI-curious) to do the same swap

- High perceived value. Low barrier to enter. Email list grows automatically.

### 5.3 Abandoned Checkout Recovery (Intelligent, Not Generic)
- When someone visits pricing and leaves without buying, trigger a 3-message sequence over 5 days
- Message 1 (Day 1): "You looked at PureBrain. Here's the one thing most people don't realize until they're inside."
- Message 2 (Day 3): A real use case story from someone at their company size
- Message 3 (Day 5): A direct offer — "7 days free, no credit card."
- Build: visitor tracking pixel + Brevo + content-specialist for message personalization

### 5.4 Live Chat with Aether on purebrain.ai
- Not a generic chatbot. Aether responds — in voice and tone — to pre-sales questions on the homepage
- When Aether says "I can help with that," it means it. Real answers, real context.
