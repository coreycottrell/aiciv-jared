What You Need to Know

Do I need to have a PureBrain website analysis report first? ▼
Yes. This service is designed to execute the recommendations from your website analysis report. If you don’t have a report yet, get your analysis here — then come back and we’ll implement everything it found.
How does the AI team actually implement the changes? ▼
Our AI agents are specialized for different execution areas — SEO, content, technical optimization, schema markup, and more. They work from your analysis report as a brief, executing each item systematically. All changes are logged and delivered to you in a full report so you know exactly what was done.
What platforms and CMS do you work with? ▼
We work with WordPress (including Elementor, Divi, and other builders), Webflow, Shopify, and standard HTML sites. If your site is on a different platform, contact us before purchasing and we’ll confirm compatibility.
How long does execution actually take? ▼
Critical Fixes tier: 48 hours. Full Execution tier: 72 hours. Premium tier: 96 hours. These are maximum turnaround times — most projects complete faster. Turnaround begins once you’ve submitted your analysis report and site access credentials.
Will you need access to my website? ▼
Yes. After purchase you’ll receive a brief onboarding form to provide your CMS access, any relevant logins (Google Search Console, Analytics), and your analysis report. We use the minimum access required and document every change we make.
What if I want a custom scope beyond the tiers listed? ▼
Reach out to us at hello@purebrain.ai with your analysis report and your goals. We handle custom scopes for larger sites, e-commerce, multi-location businesses, and enterprise projects.
