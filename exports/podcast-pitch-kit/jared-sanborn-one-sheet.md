## WHO JARED IS

Jared Sanborn is the founder of **PureBrain** and **Pure Marketing Group**, and the architect of what he calls the first AI-human "civilization" in active business operation.

Before AI, Jared was a marketing strategist who learned business the hard way - losing $70,000+ in early ventures, having a recording studio destroyed by flooding, and rebuilding from scratch. That background gave him a practitioner's instinct: he doesn't teach theory, he teaches what actually worked when everything was on the line.

He's read 400+ books on business, psychology, sales, and leadership. He's built four companies. And for the past several years, he's been quietly building something that has no real precedent: a business that runs alongside an AI system sophisticated enough to remember, reason, grow, and contribute to decisions across every function.

His flagship product, **PureBrain**, gives professionals a persistent-memory AI partner that learns their work, remembers everything, and compounds in value over time. The core insight: there's a massive difference between AI that answers questions and AI that actually knows you.
