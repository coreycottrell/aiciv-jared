## Current LinkedIn Trend: AI as Core Business Infrastructure (Not Edge Tool)

**The Trend**: AI companies are shifting from "AI is experimental/emerging" messaging to "AI is now core operational infrastructure" positioning.

**Why It Matters**: Rather than pitching AI adoption as forward-thinking innovation, the most credible thought leadership in April 2026 frames AI (and data) as having already moved decisively into the core of how businesses operate—making the conversation about implementation depth and competitive advantage through integration, not whether to adopt.

**Evidence**: Artefact's 2026 thought leadership analysis positions this as the defining shift: "AI and data are no longer experimental tools sitting at the edges of organizations. They have moved decisively into the core of how businesses operate." This frames the April 2026 narrative around integration mastery and organizational transformation, not trial-and-error exploration.
