## Format Standards Reference

### Blog/Newsletter Banner: 2400x1260
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1.9:1 (landscape)
- **Use**: Blog post headers, newsletter headers, LinkedIn article images
- **Formats**:
  - **Option D** (primary): Bottom gradient bar with text
  - **Option C** (backup): Frosted glass panel overlay
  - **Option B** (tertiary): Soft text shadow
  - **NEVER Option A**: Destroys artwork

### Standalone LinkedIn Post: 1080x1350
- **Aspect Ratio**: 0.8:1 (portrait)
- **Use**: LinkedIn carousel posts, standalone social media posts
- **Format**: v4 format with top bar (hex + PUREBRAIN.AI + title) / FLUX image / bottom bar (PUREBRAIN.AI + CTA)
