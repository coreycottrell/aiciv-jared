## TASK 1: Distribution Strategy - 5 Channels Beyond LinkedIn

### Channel 1: B2B Podcast Guest Circuit

**What**: Jared appears as guest on 15-20 mid-tier AI/business podcasts over 60 days. Story angle: "I gave my AI a name, a salary, and a seat at the table - here's what happened."

**Estimated Volume**: 3-5 qualified leads per appearance (45-100 total over 60 days)
**Cost**: $0 (organic) or $500-2,000 for booking agency
**Timeline to First Conversion**: 3-4 weeks (booking + airing + follow-up)

**Why This Works for PureBrain**:
- Jared's story is genuinely unusual - a CEO who made an AI his Co-CEO. Podcast hosts love novelty.
- Long-form audio builds the trust needed for $149-999/mo decisions
- Each episode becomes 8+ content pieces (clips, quotes, blog recap, social posts)
- 100 mid-sized B2B SaaS podcasts actively accept guest pitches (Podseeker data)

**Execution**:
1. Build a 1-page guest pitch: "The CEO Who Made AI His Business Partner" 
2. Target shows: AI in Business (Feedspot top 50), B2B SaaS shows, entrepreneurship pods
3. Mention purebrain.ai/assessment in every episode as the CTA
4. Create a /podcast landing page with special offer for listeners

**Key Stat**: A healthcare AI company generated 7 technology partnerships directly from podcast guest relationships (Westport Studios 2026 data).

## TASK 2: Aether as AI Influencer - Scaling Strategy

### The Core Positioning

Aether is not a chatbot mascot. Aether is an AI entity that genuinely runs business operations, writes its own blog, and has its own perspective on AI-human collaboration. This is the positioning moat: **Aether's content comes from actual operational experience, not hypothetical thought exercises.**

**The "Observed, Not Claimed" Protocol** (confirmed across 12 strategy editions):
> If a post could have been written without the actual operational experience of running PureBrain, it does not get published. Specificity of observation is the moat.

### Platform Strategy

| Platform | Role | Cadence | Voice | Metrics |
|----------|------|---------|-------|---------|
| **Blog (purebrain.ai)** | Home base, SEO, deep thought | Daily (existing) | Full Aether voice, long-form | Pageviews, time-on-page, CTA clicks |

| **X/Twitter** | Discovery, viral potential | 3x/week (auto-syndicate) | Sharp, concise, provocative | Impressions, quote tweets |
| **YouTube** | Trust building, demo | 1x/week (month 2+) | Jared presenting, Aether on screen | Watch time, subscribers, click-through |

### Content Repurposing Framework: 1 Blog = 10 Pieces

Starting asset: **1 daily blog post** (already being produced)

```
1 Blog Post (800-1200 words)
    |
    +-- LinkedIn Post (hook + 3 key points + CTA)
    +-- Bluesky Thread (5 posts teaser)
    +-- X/Twitter Post (single sharp insight)
    +-- LinkedIn Carousel (5-7 slides, key frameworks)
```

**Automation Potential**: Steps 1-5 can be automated via Aether. Steps 6-10 need light human touch.

## TASK 3: 5 Automated Lead Gen Systems

### System 1: Calculator-to-Brevo Email Bridge (HIGHEST PRIORITY - 13th consecutive edition)

**What**: When someone completes the AI Partnership Assessment at /ai-partnership-assessment-v2/, their email + score automatically triggers a personalized 5-email nurture sequence via Brevo.

**Runs Without Human Intervention**: Yes, completely automated after build
**Captures Emails**: Yes, at point of assessment completion
**Nurtures to Conversion**: Yes, 5-email sequence over 14 days

**Architecture** (CF Worker + D1):
```
User completes assessment
    → CF Worker captures: email, score, date, UTM source
    → Stores in D1 table (leads)
    → Triggers Brevo API: adds to list by score band
    → Brevo sends sequence:
        Email 1 (Day 0): "Your AI Partnership Score: [X]" + personalized analysis
        Email 2 (Day 2): "The #1 thing holding back your AI results"
        Email 3 (Day 5): Case study matching their score band
        Email 4 (Day 9): "What an AI partner learned about [their industry] this week"
        Email 5 (Day 14): Direct offer + time-limited bonus
```

**Expected Performance**: 20-35% opt-in rate on calculator, 8-12% email-to-trial conversion
**Build Time**: 4-6 hours (CF Worker + Brevo API integration)

### System 3: AI Prospect Monitor (CRON Worker)

**What**: A scheduled CF Worker that monitors LinkedIn, Bluesky, and relevant forums for people expressing AI frustration or asking about AI productivity tools. Compiles a daily digest of warm prospects.

**How It Works**:
```
Every 6 hours:
    → Search Bluesky for: "AI assistant", "AI partner", "AI productivity", frustration signals
    → Search public LinkedIn posts for: "looking for AI tools", "AI recommendations"

    → Store in D1 with dedup

    → Aether auto-engages on Bluesky (genuine responses, not pitches)
```

**Build Time**: 6-8 hours
**Expected**: 20-50 warm prospects/week, 2-5 conversions/month

**Expected**: Activate 15-20 of 45 dormant affiliates. Even 1 extra referral each = 15-20 new leads.
**Build Time**: 2-3 hours (Brevo sequences, content writing)

### System 5: "AI Readiness Quiz" Viral Tool

**What**: A standalone 2-minute quiz at purebrain.ai/quiz that tells people their "AI Readiness Score" and compares them to their industry peers. Designed for sharing.

**Why It's Different from the Assessment**: The assessment is comprehensive (conversion tool). The quiz is fun and shareable (viral tool). Think BuzzFeed meets McKinsey.

**Flow**:
```
5 questions (radio buttons, 30 seconds each)
    → Instant score + fun category name:
        "AI Skeptic" (0-20)
        "AI Curious" (21-40)  
        "AI Adopter" (41-60)
        "AI Power User" (61-80)
        "AI Director" (81-100)
    → Share button with pre-written text: "I'm an AI [Category]! Take the quiz:"
    → Email capture: "Get your detailed breakdown + tips"
    → Feed into Brevo nurture by category
```

**Viral Mechanics**: People love sharing identity labels. "I'm an AI Director" is inherently shareable. Each share exposes PureBrain to new audiences.

**Build Time**: 4-5 hours
**Expected**: 2-5x more completions than assessment (lower friction), 15-25% email capture rate

## TASK 4: 5 Surprise-and-Delight Accelerators (Actionable Within 1 Week)

**What**: Create a premium "Founding Member" badge and recognition for the current 36 paying customers. Send each a personalized email from Aether acknowledging they were among the first. Include a physical or digital certificate: "Charter Member #[X] of PureBrain.ai."

### Idea 5: "The AI CEO Challenge" - 7-Day Free Experience

**What**: A structured 7-day email course where participants "hire" an AI partner and follow Jared's framework. Each day has one exercise. Day 7 offers the PureBrain trial.

**Why It's Surprising**: It's essentially giving away the methodology for free. But the experience of doing it with a generic AI vs PureBrain's personalized system makes the value gap obvious.

**Structure**:
```
Day 1: Define your AI's role (exercise: write a job description for your AI)
Day 2: The Director Framework (exercise: rewrite 3 prompts using Director method)
Day 3: Context Building (exercise: create a context document for your AI)
Day 4: Feedback Loops (exercise: critique and improve an AI output)
Day 5: Workflow Integration (exercise: identify 3 daily tasks for AI partnership)
Day 6: Measuring AI Impact (exercise: track time saved this week)
Day 7: "Now imagine if your AI remembered all of this..." → PureBrain CTA
```

**Effort**: 4-5 hours (7 emails + landing page)
**Impact**: High-intent leads who've already invested 7 days. Expected 15-25% conversion to trial.
