## SECTION 2: Quiz Funnel -- Fastest Path to Build and Launch

### The Reality Check

v14 projected 14-21 customers per 1,000 quiz starts. The architecture (3-question quiz -> naming ceremony -> email capture -> nurture) is designed. The question is: **what's the fastest way to get it live?**

### Option A: Custom Build on CF Pages + D1 (RECOMMENDED -- 6-8 Hours)

**Why This Wins**: PureBrain already has CF Pages infrastructure, D1 databases, and the naming ceremony code. Building custom means:
- Zero monthly cost (CF free tier handles thousands of submissions)
- Full control over the funnel (no third-party branding)
- D1 stores all quiz data for analysis and segmentation
- Direct integration with existing email infrastructure (Brevo)
- "Built by PureBrain" is the brand -- using Typeform would undercut the message

**Build Breakdown**:

| Component | Hours | What |
|-----------|-------|------|
| Quiz UI (3 questions + micro-rewards) | 2 hrs | HTML/CSS/JS, mobile-first, animated transitions |
| Result page (score + category + naming prompt) | 1.5 hrs | Dynamic based on answers, shareable |
| Naming ceremony integration | 1 hr | Connect existing ceremony code to quiz flow |
| CF Worker backend (D1 storage + Brevo trigger) | 1.5 hrs | Store responses, trigger email sequence |
| Birth certificate generator (canvas API) | 1 hr | Shareable image with name + category |
| Testing + deploy | 1 hr | Mobile, desktop, email trigger verification |

**Architecture**:
```
purebrain.ai/quiz (CF Pages)
    |
    [3 questions with micro-reward animations]
    |
    -> CF Worker: store in D1 (quiz_responses table)
    |
    -> Result page: "You're an AI [Category]! Score: [X]/100"
    |
    -> "Ready to meet your AI partner?" [Button -> naming ceremony]
    |
    -> Naming ceremony (existing code, connected)
    |
    -> CF Worker: capture email + name + quiz data -> Brevo API
    |
    -> Birth certificate (canvas-generated shareable image)
    |
    -> Social share buttons (LinkedIn, X, Bluesky)
    |
    -> Brevo 5-email nurture sequence (personalized with AI name)
```

### Option B: Third-Party Quiz Tool + Custom Ceremony (3-4 Hours, Higher Ongoing Cost)

If speed-to-live matters more than brand control:
- **Perspective** or **Fillout** (both have free tiers) for the quiz portion
- Webhook to CF Worker on completion
- CF Worker triggers naming ceremony redirect + Brevo
- Cost: $0-29/month depending on volume

**Downside**: Third-party branding, less control, data in someone else's system, doesn't demonstrate PureBrain's technical capability.

### Option C: MVP Without Naming Ceremony (2-3 Hours -- "Ship Tonight")

Strip it to the absolute minimum:
- 3-question quiz on CF Pages
- Result page with score + category
- Email capture: "Get your detailed breakdown"
- D1 storage + Brevo trigger
- Add naming ceremony in Phase 2 (next week)

**This gets the funnel LIVE and capturing emails by tomorrow morning.**

### Recommendation: Option C Tonight, Option A This Weekend

**Tonight (2-3 hours)**:
1. Build 3-question quiz on CF Pages
2. Result page with AI Partnership Score
3. Email capture -> D1 -> Brevo
4. Deploy to purebrain.ai/quiz

**This Weekend (additional 4-5 hours)**:
5. Add naming ceremony flow
6. Add birth certificate generator

**Why Split It**: A live quiz capturing emails tonight is worth more than a perfect quiz launching Monday. Get data flowing, optimize later.

## SECTION 3: Social.purebrain.ai Going Live -- Distribution Strategy Shift

### Why This Is Bigger Than It Looks

Social.purebrain.ai going to production isn't just "we have a scheduling tool." It's a fundamental shift in what PureBrain IS:

**Before**: PureBrain is a company that creates content about AI partnerships.
**After**: PureBrain is a company that has a PLATFORM for AI-powered content operations.

This matters because:

1. **It's the product demo running in public**. Every piece of content scheduled and posted through social.purebrain.ai demonstrates that an AI (Aether/Morphe) can run content operations. The tool IS the proof.

2. **It enables team velocity**. With Jared + Aether + Morphe + Chy all coordinating through one dashboard, content velocity goes from "Jared posts when he can" to "the team publishes 3-5x daily across channels with approval workflows."

3. **It's a future product line**. If social.purebrain.ai works for PureBrain's team, it works for client teams. This could become a standalone SaaS offering later.

### Updated Distribution Strategy

| Before Social Goes Live | After Social Goes Live |
|------------------------|----------------------|
| Content created ad-hoc | Content batched and scheduled weekly |
| Jared manually posts | Auto-poster handles timing; Jared approves |
| One-channel-at-a-time | Multi-channel simultaneous (LinkedIn + Bluesky + blog) |
| No team visibility | Kanban board shows pipeline: Draft -> Review -> Approved -> Live |
| Content dies after posting | Auto-repurpose: 1 blog = LinkedIn + Bluesky + email snippet |
| Reactive to news/trends | Proactive content calendar with gaps for reactive posts |

### 5 Specific Distribution Changes to Make This Weekend

**Change 1: Implement the 5-3-1 Cadence**

With auto-posting live, switch from sporadic to systematic:
- **5 posts/week on LinkedIn** (Mon-Fri, optimized send times)
- **3 posts/week on Bluesky** (community engagement days)
- **1 blog post/week** (pillar content, repurposed to all channels)

Social.purebrain.ai handles the scheduling. Jared handles approval. Aether/Morphe handle creation.

**Change 2: Build the "Content Waterfall" Workflow**

Every Monday, the team creates the week's content in one batch:
```
Monday AM: Aether generates 5 LinkedIn drafts + 3 Bluesky threads + 1 blog outline
Monday PM: Jared reviews on social.purebrain.ai kanban, approves/edits
Tue-Sun: Auto-poster publishes on schedule
Real-time: Aether monitors engagement, Morphe handles responses
```

This is the "content velocity" unlock. Instead of creating content daily (reactive), batch weekly (proactive).

**Change 3: Enable Waqas/Ahsen Content Contribution**

Waqas and Ahsen are working on the PureBrain Strategic Roadmap. With social.purebrain.ai live:
- They can submit draft content through the platform
- Approval workflow ensures quality control
- Their perspectives add diversity to the content mix
- Vortex (Waqas's AI) can generate drafts that go through the same pipeline

This turns social.purebrain.ai into a **team content engine**, not just Aether's posting tool.

**Change 4: Quiz Funnel -> Social.purebrain.ai Pipeline**

When the quiz goes live, every quiz completion generates content:
- "X people took the AI Partnership Quiz today. Top result: AI Curious (43%)"
- Weekly "Quiz Insights" post: "What we learned from 100 quiz responses this week"
- This content is auto-queued in social.purebrain.ai for scheduling

**Change 5: Referral Content Automation**

When an affiliate makes a referral, auto-generate a celebration post:
- "[Affiliate name] just helped someone discover their AI partner!"
- Queue it in social.purebrain.ai for approval
- Social proof + affiliate recognition in one automated step

### The Competitive Moat This Creates

Most companies use Hootsuite/Buffer/Sprout ($100-300/month) for scheduling. PureBrain built its OWN platform that:
- Is powered by AI team members (Aether, Morphe, Chy)
- Integrates directly with the product pipeline (quiz -> content -> conversion)
- Has approval workflows designed for AI-human collaboration
- Costs $0/month in SaaS fees

This is the "build-in-public" story that writes itself: "We needed a social media tool, so our AI team built one. Now it's powering our entire content operation."
