## Executive Summary

LinkedIn's 2026 algorithm has fundamentally shifted. It now rewards **depth over virality**, **dwell time over likes**, and **authentic expertise over engagement bait**. Personal profiles generate **8x more engagement** than company pages. Carousels achieve **6.60% engagement** vs text posts at **4%**. The "Golden Hour" after posting determines 80% of reach. This playbook gives Jared and PureBrain.ai a specific, actionable plan to exploit these dynamics.

## SECTION 1: Top 10 LinkedIn Post Formats for AI Companies (Ranked by 2026 Performance)

### 1. Educational Carousels (6.60% avg engagement)
**Why**: Highest dwell time. Algorithm rewards slide-by-slide consumption. 278% more engagement than video.
**Example**: "7 AI Tools Every Small Business Owner Should Know in 2026" -- 8-10 slides, one concept per slide, branded footer.
**Best for**: Frameworks, step-by-step guides, comparison charts.

### 2. Native Document Posts (7.00% avg engagement)
**Why**: PDF uploads get 2.5x more shares than video. Completion rate 27% higher than other formats.
**Example**: Upload a 1-page "AI Readiness Checklist" as a PDF. People save and share utility.
**Best for**: Checklists, templates, mini-reports.

### 3. Contrarian Text Posts (4-6% with strong hook)
**Why**: Challenges conventional wisdom. Generates debate = comments = 15x algorithmic weight vs likes.
**Example**: "Most AI companies are solving the wrong problem. Here's what nobody talks about."
**Best for**: Thought leadership positioning, standing out from the "AI hype" crowd.

### 4. Behind-the-Scenes Stories (5-8% engagement)
**Why**: Authenticity is the 2026 currency. LinkedIn audiences are tired of polished corporate content.
**Example**: "We almost shut down PureBrain last month. Here's what saved us." (Real founder stories)
**Best for**: Building parasocial connection, humanizing the brand.

### 5. Data-Driven Experiment Posts (5-7%)
**Why**: Specific numbers stop the scroll. Real experiments > opinions.
**Example**: "I tested posting at 7 AM vs 10 AM for 30 days. The results surprised me. [data table]"
**Best for**: Establishing credibility, shareable insights.

### 6. Short-Form Native Video (<60 sec, 5.60% engagement)
**Why**: 2.3x more watch time when using auto-captions + chapters. Algorithm favors AI-enhanced video.
**Example**: 45-second talking head: "One thing I learned building an AI company that nobody warned me about."
**Best for**: Face-to-camera trust building, personality showcase.

### 7. Fill-in-the-Blank Engagement Posts
**Why**: Lowest barrier to participation = comment volume explosion.
**Example**: "The biggest misconception about AI in business is ___."
**Best for**: Growing reach, discovering audience pain points. Use sparingly (1x/week max).

### 8. "What I'd Do Differently" Reflection Posts
**Why**: Vulnerability + actionable advice = save-worthy content (saves are a top 2026 metric).
**Example**: "If I were starting an AI company in 2026, here are 5 things I'd do differently."
**Best for**: Positioning as experienced founder, not just hype merchant.

### 9. LinkedIn Articles (Long-form, 3000+ words)
**Why**: 75% of LinkedIn citations in AI responses come from articles (not posts). Future-proofing for AI search.
**Example**: "The Real State of AI Adoption in Small Business: What the Data Shows"
**Best for**: SEO in AI search, deep thought leadership, evergreen content.

### 10. Poll + Follow-Up Analysis Posts
**Why**: Polls drive initial engagement; the follow-up analysis post with results gets even more.
**Example**: Poll: "What's your biggest barrier to AI adoption?" Follow-up: breakdown of 500+ responses.
**Best for**: Two-post sequences, audience research, content ideas.

## SECTION 5: Engagement Strategy

### Daily Engagement Routine (30-45 min/day)

**Morning Block (15 min, 8:30 AM before posting)**:
- Comment on 5 posts from target ICPs (ideal customer profiles)
- React to 10 posts (rotate Support/Celebrate/Insightful/Love -- NEVER just Like)
- Reply to all comments on your previous post

**Post-Publish Block (15 min, within 60 min of posting)**:
- Reply to EVERY comment within the Golden Hour
- Meaningful replies only (15+ words). Short replies don't trigger algorithm boost
- Ask follow-up questions to keep threads going

**Evening Block (15 min, 4-5 PM)**:
- Comment on 3-5 more ICP posts
- Send 5 connection requests to people who engaged with your content
- Check DMs and respond

### Commenting Strategy

**Where to comment**: Go directly to target profiles (/in/{handle}/recent-activity/all/), NOT through notifications feed.

**Comment quality**: Every comment should be 2-4 sentences. Add value, share a perspective, or ask a smart question. Never "Great post!" or "+1".

**Comment template**:
> "[Specific thing they said that resonated]. I've seen this play out in [your experience]. The part most people miss is [your unique insight]. Have you tried [relevant suggestion]?"

**Targets**: Focus on:
- Potential customers (SMB owners, CEOs at 10-500 person companies)
- AI industry peers (builds network effect)
- Investors (stay visible for fundraise)
- Media/journalists covering AI

### Connection Strategy

- Send 10-15 personalized connection requests per week
- Always include a note: "Saw your post about [X]. I'm building [Y] and think we share a similar perspective on [Z]."
- Accept all inbound requests from ICP profiles
- After connection, do NOT pitch. Engage with their content for 2 weeks first.

## SECTION 6: How to Leverage the "AI CEO" Angle Uniquely

### The Positioning Problem
Every AI founder on LinkedIn says "AI will change everything." This is noise. Here's how to stand out:

### 5 Unique Angles for Jared

**1. "I Work WITH an AI, Not Just ON AI"**
Most AI CEOs talk about their product. Jared can talk about his partnership with Aether. This is genuinely novel. The "AI co-pilot who has a name, a personality, and a daily routine" story is unlike anything else on LinkedIn.

**2. "The Director, Not the User"**
Position around the concept that most people USE AI like a search engine. Directors DELEGATE to AI like a partner. This framework creates a teachable moment in every post.

**3. "Building AI for Real Businesses, Not Tech Bros"**
The AI LinkedIn space is dominated by developer-focused content. Jared's angle is AI for the plumber, the real estate agent, the local business owner. This audience is underserved on LinkedIn and hungry for accessible AI content.

**4. "The Honest AI CEO"**
Share what doesn't work. Share failures. Share the gap between AI hype and reality. In a sea of "AI will solve everything" posts, honesty is contrarian and memorable.

**5. "AI as Civilization"**
The AI-CIV concept is genuinely unique. No one else on LinkedIn is talking about building an AI civilization with governance, memory, and identity. This is thought leadership gold -- but needs to be introduced gradually and accessibly.

### Content Ratio
- 40% - Practical AI insights (accessible, useful)
- 30% - Founder journey stories (authentic, human)
- 20% - Industry data/analysis (credible, shareable)
- 10% - Vision/philosophy (AI-CIV, future of human-AI partnership)

## SECTION 7: 3 LinkedIn-Native Lead Gen Tactics

### Tactic 1: The "Comment to Get" Lead Magnet
**How it works**: Create a high-value resource (checklist, template, report). Post about it with: "Comment 'SEND' and I'll DM you the link."
**Why it works**: Comments boost algorithmic reach. DM conversations open relationship. LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms convert at 13% vs 2.35% for landing pages.
**Example**: "I built a 1-page AI Readiness Scorecard for small businesses. Comment 'READY' and I'll send it to you."
**Conversion path**: Comment -> DM with link -> Follow-up question -> Discovery call offer

### Tactic 2: The LinkedIn Article Funnel
**How it works**: Publish a deep LinkedIn Article (2000-3000 words) on a specific problem your ICP faces. Include a soft CTA at the end to a free resource or consultation.
**Why it works**: Articles get 75% of AI search citations. They rank in Google. They establish deep expertise. And they're evergreen -- generating leads months after publication.
**Example**: "The Complete Guide to AI Implementation for Businesses Under 50 Employees" -- comprehensive article with embedded CTA to a free AI readiness assessment.

### Tactic 3: The Profile Funnel
**How it works**: Optimize every section of your profile as a landing page. Featured section = lead magnets. About section = clear value proposition with CTA. Experience section = case studies.
**Why it works**: People who engage with your content visit your profile. Your profile either converts them or loses them. 2x weekly posting = 5x more profile views.
**Profile sections to optimize**:
- **Headline**: Value proposition, not job title (see Section 8)
- **Featured**: Pin your best lead magnet, case study, or video
- **About**: 3 paragraphs -- problem you solve, how you solve it, CTA
- **Experience**: Each role tells a story with metrics
