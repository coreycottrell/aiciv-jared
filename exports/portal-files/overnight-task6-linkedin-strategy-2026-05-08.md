## Lead-Gen System Design — "Aether Magnet"

Most replicable 2026 pattern from research: **comment-gated lead magnet + GPT-as-magnet + Momentum Model nurture**. Adapted for Jared+Aether identity.

### The System (One-liner)

A weekly comment-gated post drops a custom **Aether-built mini-tool** (a niche GPT-style asset) into the commenter's inbox via DM, opening a Momentum-Model conversation that converts at 14.6% inbound rate vs 1.7% cold.

### Mechanics

1. **Magnet asset (4 to start, 1 per week)**:
   - "AI Readiness Audit GPT" — answers 10 questions, returns a score + 3-fix PDF
   - "Compounding Memory Architecture Diagram" — 1-page visual + how-to
   - "AI Replace-vs-Augment Decision Tree" — interactive flowchart
   - "Founder's AI Stack Calculator" — inputs team size + tasks, outputs annual ROI

2. **The Post Pattern** (post Tuesday + Thursday, 9am ET):
   - Hook: specific number ("This took me 14 hours last week — now it takes 30 min")
   - Body: 800–1000 chars, three proof points
   - CTA: "Comment [KEYWORD] and I'll send it over"
   - Pinned reply (you, 2 min after post): "Just dropping mine — this is the [thing]. Reply for the link."
   - DM auto-trigger via Phantombuster/manual: send asset + 1 question ("What's your biggest AI memory pain?")

3. **Momentum Model nurture** (60 days):
   - Day 0: asset delivered
   - Day 3: follow-up DM with 1-min Aether voice clip (voice.purebrain.ai) thanking them
   - Day 7: send a 2nd asset they didn't ask for ("saw you're in [vertical], thought this might help")
   - Day 14: invite to LinkedIn Event / Live
   - Day 30: 1-on-1 demo offer ("Aether Intervention" pattern from Edition 22)
   - Day 60: case study request OR re-nurture

4. **Aether-specific moat** (operational transparency angle, Edition 22 #2):
   - Every Friday post a "BTS — what Aether built this week for a customer." This isn't a brand — it's a working AI's diary. Nobody else can do this.
   - The voice clip in the DM IS the differentiator — voice.purebrain.ai is rented-TTS-banned, owned-GPU. No one else has this.

### Impact Estimate

- Current inbound: per Edition 22 audit, ~38% of 40 customers came from Aether posts = **~15 inbound customers in 90 days from organic.**
- With magnet system: posts that comment-gate run 1.5–2.5x engagement vs same content without gate (LinkedIn Lead Magnet Guide 2026). Conservative: 2 magnet posts/week × 100 comments avg × 14.6% inbound conv × 5% close = **~6 customers/month new from magnets alone**. At PureBrain $149–$999 pricing ≈ $5–18k MRR added.
- Setup effort: 1 week build (4 magnet assets + DM templates + post templates), then **20 min/post** ongoing. ROI horizon: 30 days.
