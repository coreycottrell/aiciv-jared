## OUTREACH STRATEGY RECOMMENDATIONS

1. **Warm intros beat cold outreach**: For Tier 1 targets, find mutual connections on LinkedIn before reaching out
2. **Lead with the metric**: "25 onboarded customers, 150 in pipeline, $197-$12K/mo revenue model" -- this is real traction, not vaporware
3. **Differentiate from chatbots immediately**: "Persistent AI partners, not chatbots" should be in the first sentence
4. **NYC investors first**: Belsky, Banta, Chang, Wilson, Vaynerchuk are all NYC-based -- offer in-person demos
5. **Florida investors second**: Lando (Miami), New World Angels, Florida Angels -- geographic proximity advantage
6. **Angel check stacking**: At $50K minimum, getting 5-10 angels writing $50K-$250K checks each can fill $2.5M efficiently
7. **Signal investors**: Andrew Ng or Marc Benioff investing even $50K would create massive signal value for follow-on investors
