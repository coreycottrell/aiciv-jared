## Pre-Game: The Philosophy

Product Hunt is a marathon disguised as a sprint. The 24-hour window is real, but the key metric is hours of exposure at the top of the feed. Products submitted at 12:01 AM PST have 23+ hours of visibility. Products submitted at 9 AM PST have 15 hours. Products submitted at noon have 12 hours.

Every hour you are not responding to comments, you are losing ranking. Comment response velocity is one of the strongest signals to the PH algorithm. This is the #1 tactical priority on launch day.

The goal for May 6: respond to EVERY comment within 2 hours. No exceptions. This requires Jared to be available (or Aether monitoring) throughout the business day.

## Emergency Protocols

**If the listing gets marked as spam or taken down**:
- Contact Product Hunt support immediately via their help chat
- While waiting: do not panic publicly, do not post about it
- If restored: resume normal engagement. The hours lost cannot be recovered but the launch can still succeed.

**If the hunter doesn't submit at 12:01 AM**:
- Wait until 12:10 AM
- If still nothing: self-submit immediately
- Text the hunter: "Did you submit? If not, I've submitted as maker. No problem either way."

**If the website goes down**:
- full-stack-developer notified immediately
- Jared posts on PH: "We're experiencing some traffic-related issues — working to resolve immediately. The team is on it."
- Priority: restore service within 30 minutes
- If extended outage: pause email sending and social amplification until restored

**If there is a PR crisis or negative press**:
- Do not delete comments
- Respond factually and calmly
- Consult Jared before any public statement beyond "we hear you, here's what we're doing"
