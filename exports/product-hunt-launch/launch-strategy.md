PureBrain has strong product-market fit for the Product Hunt audience: makers, growth hackers, founders, and early adopters who actively seek AI productivity tools.

## Strategic Context

### Why Product Hunt for PureBrain

Product Hunt's core audience is ideal-fit early adopters for PureBrain:

- **Indie hackers and founders**: Exactly who uses AI tools obsessively and talks about them publicly
- **AI-first community**: Product Hunt launched many of the leading AI tools (Notion AI, Perplexity, Midjourney)
- **Early adopter multipliers**: PH hunters write newsletters, post on Twitter/X, appear on podcasts — one PH upvoter can bring 10 more signups through secondary reach
- **SEO juice**: High-authority product pages with backlinks that persist long after launch day

### Positioning for the PH Audience

Product Hunt users are makers and builders. They respond to:
- Clear problem/solution framing (not corporate marketing speak)
- Founder authenticity and maker story
- Unique insights or "aha moments" in the product description
- Demos they can try immediately
- Numbers and proof points

PureBrain's strongest angle for this audience: **"AI that gets better the longer you use it."** PH users have tried generic ChatGPT wrappers. The differentiation — personalization, memory, AI that learns YOUR business — is immediately resonant.

## The 6-Week Preparation Timeline

### T-30: Week 5 of Roadmap (March 23-29, 2026)

**Goal**: Account setup, upvote list foundation, pre-launch awareness begins.

**Actions**:

1. **Create/verify Jared's Product Hunt account** at producthunt.com
   - Username should be simple and professional (not just "jared" — try "jaredpurebrain" or "jaredsanborn")
   - Upload a professional headshot
   - Fill in bio: "Founder at PureBrain.ai — building AI that remembers you"
   - Link to purebrain.ai

2. **Build maker credibility** (required BEFORE launch)
   - Comment thoughtfully on 5-10 products in the AI/productivity category
   - Genuine engagement only — "Great product!" does nothing. Ask a real question or share a specific insight
   - This is not optional: new accounts with zero engagement history perform significantly worse

3. **Begin upvote list — pass 1** (target: 50 names by end of Week 5)
   - Export every contact who has interacted with PureBrain: assessment completers, Neural Feed subscribers, paying customers, social followers who engaged
   - Create a simple spreadsheet: Name | Email | How they know PureBrain | Product Hunt account? (Y/N/Unknown)
   - Do NOT ask them to sign up for Product Hunt just to upvote. Existing PH users only have meaningful impact.

4. **Install "coming soon" collector**
   - Create a simple page or Typeform: "PureBrain is launching on Product Hunt in May — want a launch-day reminder?"
   - Promote this in Neural Feed issue (Week 5) with context: "We're launching publicly — be one of the first to support us"
   - Add CTA to assessment thank-you page and post-payment thank-you page

5. **Research hunters** (list to be built this week, outreach in Week 6)
   - Visit producthunt.com/leaderboard
   - Find top hunters who have hunted AI tools OR productivity tools in the last 6 months
   - Note their follower count, recent hunts, and whether they appear active
   - Build a shortlist of 10-15 potential hunters to approach

### T-15 to T-9: Weeks 7-8 (April 6-19, 2026)

**Actions**:

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|------------|------------|
| No hunter found | Medium | Launch as maker-only — many top products do. Compensate with strong email list activation. |
| Upvote list under 100 | Medium | Start the "coming soon" collector on the assessment thank-you page immediately (today). |
| Low organic PH traffic | Low | PH algorithm favors early morning submissions. 12:01 AM PST ensures maximum exposure hours. |
| Negative comments | Low | Respond to every comment, positive or negative, within 2 hours. Negativity handled well publicly = trust builder. |
| Product bugs on launch day | Medium | Do a full product audit the week of April 20. Freeze all code changes after May 1. |
