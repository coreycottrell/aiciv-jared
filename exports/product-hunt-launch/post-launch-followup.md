## The Reality of Post-Launch Traffic

Product Hunt traffic spikes on Day 1 and drops sharply by Day 3. The window for converting PH visitors into genuine users is narrow — 48 hours at most. After that, you are living on the product page's long-tail SEO value and any press or secondary distribution that picked up the launch.

This plan covers how to:
1. Convert the Day 1 traffic spike into actual users
2. Leverage the Product Hunt badge and credibility long-term
3. Turn PH launch momentum into content, media, and community traction
4. Do a thorough post-mortem to capture learnings for the next launch

## Converting PH Traffic: May 7-14

### The Conversion Window

Product Hunt visitors who land on purebrain.ai in the 48-72 hours after launch are warm but fast-moving. They are specifically looking for new tools. This is the highest-intent traffic the launch generates.

**Critical**: The purebrain.ai homepage and assessment entry points must be functioning perfectly. Any friction here loses the conversion.

**Priority checklist for May 7 morning**:
- [ ] Assessment loads cleanly and fast
- [ ] "Get Started Free" CTA is prominent and works
- [ ] Post-assessment results page is rendering correctly
- [ ] No broken links from the PH listing to the site

### Segment the New Users

In the week after launch, create a segment in Brevo for "Signed up May 6-7" — these are PH-sourced users. Their behavior will tell you a lot:

- Completion rate of the assessment among this group
- Conversion to paid among this group (compared to baseline)
- Churn within 30 days

This data shapes the next launch strategy.

### Targeted Follow-Up Email for PH Signups (May 8-10)

Send to anyone who completed the assessment on May 6-7 but did NOT start a paid subscription:

```

Any questions about your results? Reply to this email — I read every one.
```

## Leveraging the Product Hunt Badge: Permanent

### The Badge

Product Hunt provides official "Featured on Product Hunt" badges. These are credibility signals that persist long after launch day.

**Actions**:

1. **Add badge to purebrain.ai homepage** — in the social proof section, "as featured on Product Hunt" with the official badge graphic and link
2. **Add badge to Neural Feed email footer** — small logo, links to PH listing
3. **Add badge to assessment results page** — reinforces credibility at the point of highest engagement
4. **Add to jareddsanborn.com** — in the "about" or credentials section

**Badge source**: Product Hunt provides an official badge generator. Search "Product Hunt badge" in their help docs.

**Timeline**: Live on all pages by May 10.

### Using "Product Hunt Launch" as a Credibility Marker

In all future marketing:

- LinkedIn bio: "Launched on Product Hunt [Month 2026]" (for 6 months after launch)
- Press pitches: "Featured on Product Hunt with [X] upvotes"
- Partner pitches: "Over [X] upvotes on Product Hunt — validated by makers and founders"
- Social media: "As seen on Product Hunt"

This framing works best in the 3 months immediately following the launch. After that, it fades in relevance.
