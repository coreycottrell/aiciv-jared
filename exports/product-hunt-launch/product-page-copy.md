## 3. FULL PRODUCT DESCRIPTION (Long form — for the "About" section)

```
Most businesses are using AI — but very few are using it well.

They've subscribed to five tools. Their team uses them inconsistently. Nobody knows if it's actually working. And every new AI announcement makes the strategy conversation more chaotic, not less.

PureBrain was built to fix that.

**Four tools. One platform. A real AI strategy.**

**AI Adoption Assessment** (free, 10 minutes)
Find out exactly where your business stands on AI adoption. Not a generic quiz — a scored assessment that benchmarks you against your industry and identifies the specific gaps holding you back. 90% of businesses discover at least one critical gap they didn't know existed.

**AI Tool Stack Calculator**
You're probably paying for tools you don't use and missing tools you need. The calculator audits your current stack, identifies redundancies and gaps, and shows you what an optimized AI tool set costs versus what you're paying now.

**AI Migration Portal**
Migrating from one AI tool to another (or from no AI to structured AI) without a plan is how businesses lose months and thousands of dollars. The portal gives you a structured migration path — what to move, in what order, with what dependencies.

**AI Partnership Audit**
AI isn't replacing your team — it's changing how your team works. The audit identifies which roles have the highest leverage for AI augmentation and builds a priority roadmap for human-AI integration that your team will actually adopt.

**Why PureBrain works when other AI tools don't:**

Other AI tools ask "what do you want to do today?" PureBrain asks "what's actually broken in your AI strategy?" Then it gives you a specific, sequenced plan to fix it.

The platform is used by founders, operations leaders, and marketing directors who are tired of AI experimentation and ready for AI strategy.

Try the free AI Adoption Assessment at purebrain.ai — takes 10 minutes, gives you a roadmap you can act on today.
```

## COPY DO'S AND DON'TS FOR THE PH AUDIENCE

**Do**:
- Use specific numbers ("10 minutes", "90% of businesses", "6 months to build")
- Acknowledge what the competition gets wrong without naming competitors
- Invite genuine dialogue in the maker comment
- Use plain language — no corporate buzzwords
- Lead with the problem, not the feature

**Don't**:
- Use the word "revolutionary" or "game-changing" or "cutting-edge"
- Claim to be "#1" without proof
- Use passive voice ("Businesses are empowered by...")
- Lead with funding, team size, or accolades
- Write about the company — write about the user's problem
