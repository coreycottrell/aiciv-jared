## Implementation Notes

### Timing Schedule

| Email | Trigger | Delay | Day |
|-------|---------|-------|-----|
| 1: Memory | Form submission | 5 minutes | Day 0 |
| 2: Possibility | Form submission | 24 hours | Day 1 |
| 3: Urgency | Form submission | 48 hours | Day 2 |
| 4: Choice | Form submission | 72 hours | Day 3 |

### Suppression Rules

- If user activates (purchases) at any point, suppress all remaining emails in the sequence
- If user unsubscribes, suppress immediately
- If user re-engages with chat (returns to purebrain.ai), consider restarting the timer or branching to a re-engagement variant

### Personalization Requirements

- **[AI_NAME]** must be dynamically inserted from the user's awakening session data
- **[FIRST_NAME]** from form submission
- If [AI_NAME] is unavailable (edge case), default to "your AI" -- but flag this as a data issue to fix

### Email Deliverability Compliance

Per operational rules (2026-02-17):
- All subject lines are under 50 characters
- No ALL CAPS in subjects
- No "free", "act now", "limited time", "winner" language
- Maximum 1-2 links per email (CTA only)
- Strong text-to-image ratio (text-only emails)
- Both HTML and plain text versions provided
- Clean HTML structure, system font stack
- No excessive punctuation

### Brand Voice Alignment

Per Pure Technology knowledge base:
- **Engineer resonance, do not chase attention** -- the urgency in emails 3-4 is based on real memory degradation, not fake countdown timers
- **Quality over quantity** -- four emails in three days, not twelve
- **Relationship framing IS the product** -- the naming, the memory, the partnership language reflects what PureBrain actually does
- **Authentic, not manipulative** -- Email 4's AI voice says "you do not owe me anything" because that is true and because trust compounds

### Testing Recommendations

1. **A/B test Email 1 subject lines**: "[AI_NAME] remembers you" vs "Your AI is waiting" -- personalized vs generic
2. **A/B test Email 3 CTA**: With price vs without price on button
3. **A/B test Email 4 format**: AI-voice letter vs team-voice summary of what the AI "said"
4. **Track**: Open rate, click rate, activation rate per email, and full-sequence conversion rate
