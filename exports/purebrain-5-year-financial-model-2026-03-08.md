## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

PureBrain.ai is positioned at the intersection of two powerful market forces: the explosion of AI adoption in business (projected 38% CAGR through 2030) and the acute shortage of AI implementation expertise. The company's three-tier subscription model—Partnered ($499/mo), Unified ($999/mo), and Awakened ($999/mo, infrastructure tier)—delivers $25,000–$90,000/month in equivalent human services at a fraction of the cost.

Pure Technology's single most underutilized asset is Jared's existing network: direct relationships with decision-makers at P&G, Coca-Cola, Pepsi, Mars, Kroger, Target, MGM, and Carnival Cruise Lines through Pure Marketing Group. These are not cold leads. They are warm relationships with enterprise buyers who already trust the founder.

**Key Findings Across Scenarios:**

| Metric | Conservative | Base Case | Aggressive |
|--------|-------------|-----------|------------|
| Year 5 ARR | $4.2M | $12.8M | $31.4M |
| Year 5 MRR | $350K | $1.07M | $2.62M |
| Year 5 Customers | 292 | 825 | 2,002 |
| Year 5 Net Margin | 22% | 38% | 44% |
| Break-Even Month | Month 19 | Month 14 | Month 11 |
| Year 5 Headcount | 12 | 22 | 45 |

**Bottom Line Recommendation**: Execute Base Case immediately. The PMG network represents an extraordinary warm-pipeline advantage that most SaaS companies would pay $5M to acquire. Even capturing 1% of existing PMG relationships converts to meaningful ARR in Year 1. The infrastructure is already built. The cost to acquire the first 50 customers is essentially relationship capital, not cash.

## SECTION 10: RECOMMENDATIONS

### 10.1 Immediate Actions (Next 30 Days)

1. **Activate top 25 PMG contacts immediately.** Target 5 Unified conversions in first 60 days. This alone covers 8 months of burn at break-even. These are warm relationships — the pitch is "let me show you what PureBrain does, on me for 30 days."

2. **Defer Jared's salary for 90 days or reduce to $4,000/mo.** This extends cash runway by $12,000–$24,000 — effectively buying 1–2 additional months before needing customer revenue. Reinstate full salary at Month 4.

3. **Price the Awakened waitlist appropriately.** $999 for enterprise-grade AI infrastructure is dramatically underpriced. Before launching publicly, set Awakened at $2,499/mo minimum. Current waitlist contacts expect a premium product — deliver it at a premium price.

4. **Set up basic financial tracking now.** Monthly P&L, cash position, MRR by tier. Track 5 metrics weekly: New MRR, Churned MRR, Net MRR, Cash Balance, # Active Customers. Use a simple spreadsheet. Upgrade to a proper accounting system (Wave or QuickBooks) at $5K MRR.
