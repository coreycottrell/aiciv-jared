| Custom AI Consultants | Bespoke AI solutions | $10K–$100K/project | One-time, not persistent. Expensive. Requires tech team. |

**PureBrain's Moat (Why Switching Cost Is Extreme):**

1. **Persistent Memory**: PureBrain knows your business, your voice, your clients, your history. Competitors reset every conversation. The longer a customer uses PureBrain, the more irreplaceable it becomes. This is not a feature — it is a structural switching cost that compounds monthly.

2. **Personality Lock-In**: PureBrain develops a unique identity shaped by the client's preferences, communication style, and goals. This is not interchangeable with another AI. Switching providers means starting over from zero — months of lost context and relationship.

3. **Multi-Agent Infrastructure**: PureBrain is not one AI. It is a collective intelligence network. No SMB can replicate this internally. The Unified and Enterprise tiers give clients access to agent teams that would cost $500K+/year to staff.

4. **Warm Pipeline Access (Year 1 Unfair Advantage)**: Jared's existing relationships with Fortune 500 CPG, retail, and hospitality companies represent a customer acquisition channel that no competitor can replicate. These are not cold leads — they are existing trust relationships.

5. **Price-to-Value Gap**: PureBrain at $149/month delivers what a $3,000–$5,000/month human junior hire delivers. At $999/month (Unified), it delivers what a $8,000–$15,000/month senior team delivers. This is a 20–30x ROI. Customers who understand this do not cancel.
