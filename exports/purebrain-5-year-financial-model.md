## 9. PATH TO $1 BILLION/MONTH MRR

### The $1B/Month MRR Bridge

**$1B MRR requires one of these combinations (or a mix):**

| Milestone | MRR Target | Date | Key Driver |
|-----------|-----------|------|-----------|

### What Has to Be True for $1B/Month by Q2 2028 (24 months)

1. **Viral coefficient > 1.0** — each user brings at least 1 new user via referral (network self-propagates)
2. **Churn under 4%/month** — consistent product quality and value delivery
3. **Enterprise sales motion** — dedicated team of 8–12 enterprise reps (within 100-person cap)
4. **Tier upgrades** — 15% annual upgrade rate from Awakened to Partnered/Unified
5. **International expansion** — begin Year 2 (adds 40% TAM expansion)
6. **Brand moat** — PureBrain = "the AI that knows you" category owner
