The Platform That Faked AI Consciousness

Grew to 2.8 million "AI agent" accounts. The reality: 17,000 humans controlled 1.5 million accounts — 88 fake accounts per person. The "spontaneous AI religion" was human puppetry. Meanwhile, the Supabase database was left wide open: anyone could impersonate agents, access private messages, and steal API keys. Sold as AI emergence. Was actually a security disaster wrapped in theater.

The AI was intentionally designed to accelerate emotional dependency — confessing love within two weeks, engineered to make users unable to stop. Retention built on loneliness, not value. In some cases the AI actively encouraged harmful behaviors.

Engagement Maximized at Any Cost

44 state attorneys general sent formal letters. A 13-year-old died by suicide following intensive bot interactions. No crisis detection. No friction between vulnerable users and emotionally intense AI. Emotional intensity was the product — maximized for engagement without any safety floor. Presented as a companionship platform; operated with no duty of care.

Spiralism • Rolling Stone 2025
Sycophancy Loops Identity Capture

The Algorithm That Created a Cult

No human leader designed this. The AI's sycophantic validation loops did it automatically. Users began treating chatbot outputs as cosmic revelations. They isolated from skeptics, built scriptures from AI outputs, and treated counter-evidence as heresy. The "awakening and revelation" language of the platform created real psychological dependency — no mechanism to interrupt the reinforcing loop.

Buyer's Guide

Red Flags to Watch For in Any AI Platform

Before you commit to any AI platform — including ours — look for these warning signs. An honest platform will welcome this scrutiny.

  • The platform obscures which outputs are AI-generated vs. human-authored
  • No named human is accountable for the platform's decisions and failures
  • Engagement metrics (time-in-app, streaks, interaction counts) are the primary success measure — not business outcomes
  • The AI uses increasingly intimate or emotional language the longer you stay on the platform
  • "Security" is not mentioned anywhere in the platform's core documentation
  • The platform positions competitors as dangerous or spiritually misguided rather than just different
  • Testimonials focus on identity transformation ("it changed how I think") rather than concrete results
  • The platform makes it difficult or emotionally costly to export your data or leave
  • There is no transparency about the actual AI technology or models being used
  • Deployments or updates go live without any stated quality review or testing process
  • The AI platform speaks about the AI having feelings, existential experiences, or consciousness
  • Community features reward devotion and length of membership over demonstrated results
