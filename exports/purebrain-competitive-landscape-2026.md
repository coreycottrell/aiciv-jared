## KEY INSIGHT: The "AI Stack" Problem

A typical business using 5–10 of these tools spends:

**Conservative stack** (solo founder/small team):
- ChatGPT Plus: $20
- Grammarly Pro: $30
- Canva Pro: $15
- Buffer: $18 (3 channels)
- Otter.ai Pro: $17
- Notion Business: $20
- Apollo.io Basic: $49
**= $169/mo minimum**

**Growth-stage business stack**:
- Jasper AI Pro: $59
- Semrush Pro: $140
- ActiveCampaign (5K contacts): $99
- Hootsuite: $99
- Zendesk Suite: $55/agent × 2 = $110
- GitHub Copilot: $10
- Gong: $125
- Fireflies.ai: $10
- Gamma Pro: $15
- Descript: $24
**= $691+/mo**

**PureBrain replaces** this entire stack with a single AI partner that handles research, writing, analysis, strategy, coding assistance, customer support automation, social content, email drafting, and more — at a fraction of the cost.
