## WHAT GETS US TO $1B/MONTH

**The math:**
- 2,500,000 consumer subscribers x $345 blended ARPU = $863M
- 2,000 enterprise clients x $20,000 avg = $40M enterprise
- Tier upgrade uplift = ~$100M additional
- **TOTAL: ~$1.003B MRR**

**What has to be true:**
1. Viral coefficient stays above 1.0 (each user brings 1+ new user)
2. Monthly churn stays under 4% (great product quality)
3. Enterprise sales motion builds (8-12 dedicated reps, within 100-person cap)
4. 15% annual upgrade rate (Awakened to Partnered/Unified)
5. International expansion begins Year 2
