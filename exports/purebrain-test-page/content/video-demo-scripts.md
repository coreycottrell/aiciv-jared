## Context

Three 30-60 second demo videos that play AFTER the user names their AI. These videos demonstrate the three core differentiators that justify the PureBrain value proposition. Each video should feel personal - the user has just named their AI, so address "your [AI Name]" where possible.

## Video 1: Memory Demo

**Title**: "Your AI Remembers Everything"
**Duration**: 45 seconds
**Trigger**: Plays after naming ceremony completes

### Script

```
OPENING (0-8 sec)
[Screen: Split view - ChatGPT on left, PureBrain on right]

VOICEOVER:
"You've probably had this conversation before."

[Text on ChatGPT side: "Hi, I'm your marketing consultant from Austin"]
[ChatGPT response: "Hello! How can I help you today?"]

---

PROBLEM (8-18 sec)
[ChatGPT side continues]

VOICEOVER:
"And this one. And this one."

[Multiple chat bubbles appear, fading: "I'm a marketing consultant..." repeated]
[Text overlay: "Every. Single. Session."]

---

SOLUTION (18-35 sec)
[Focus shifts to PureBrain side]

VOICEOVER:
"Your PureBrain AI remembers.

Two weeks from now, when you mention 'that campaign we discussed,' [AI Name] will know exactly what you mean.

No repeating yourself. No context lost.
Your partner picks up where you left off."

[Visual: Conversation showing "[AI Name] remembers" with context from previous sessions flowing in]

---

CLOSE (35-45 sec)
[Clean frame with AI Name displayed]

VOICEOVER:
"[AI Name] doesn't just respond to you.
[AI Name] remembers you."

[Text: "No more starting over."]
```

### Key Message
"No more re-explaining context"

### Visual Requirements
- Split-screen comparison format
- ChatGPT side shows repetitive "who are you" intros
- PureBrain side shows continuous, contextual conversation
- Use actual UI screenshots where possible

## Video 2: Autonomous Work Demo

**Title**: "Your AI Works While You Sleep"
**Duration**: 50 seconds
**Trigger**: Plays in onboarding sequence OR on demo page

### Script

```
OPENING (0-10 sec)
[Screen: Clock showing 11:47 PM]
[Person closing laptop, stretching]

VOICEOVER:
"It's late. You've got three blog posts to write, a market analysis to run, and tomorrow's meeting deck to prep."

[Visual: Task list with items unchecked]

---

THE HANDOFF (10-20 sec)
[Screen: Quick typing into PureBrain interface]

VOICEOVER:
"So you tell [AI Name] what you need. And go to bed."

[Visual: Simple task handoff - "Write up the Q1 analysis we discussed. Focus on the retention metrics."]
[Response: "[AI Name]: Got it. I'll have this ready by morning."]

---

THE MAGIC (20-35 sec)
[Time-lapse: Clock spinning from 11:47 PM to 7:30 AM]
[Background: Subtle visualization of work happening - text generating, documents forming]

VOICEOVER:
"While you sleep, [AI Name] works.

Not randomly. Not generically.
On YOUR tasks, with YOUR context, in YOUR voice."

[Visual: Document building itself, clearly personalized]

---

CLOSE (35-50 sec)
[Morning scene: Person opens laptop, coffee in hand]
[Notification: "3 items ready for review"]

VOICEOVER:
"Morning comes. The work is done.

Not 'here's a template.'
Not 'here's a starting point.'

Done. Ready. Waiting for you."

[Text: "Wake up to completed work."]
```

### Key Message
"Wake up to completed work"

### Visual Requirements
- Night-to-morning time passage
- Task list transformation (unchecked to checked)
- Emphasis on PERSONALIZED output (not generic templates)
- Avoid "robot doing work" imagery - focus on results

## Video 3: Learning Demo

**Title**: "Your AI Gets Smarter About YOU"
**Duration**: 55 seconds
**Trigger**: Plays in onboarding sequence OR as feature explainer

### Script

```
OPENING (0-12 sec)
[Screen: Calendar view - Week 1]

VOICEOVER:
"Week one: You tell [AI Name] you're a marketing consultant in Austin.
You prefer bullet points.
You hate corporate jargon."

[Visual: Info cards appearing next to AI avatar]

---

WEEK 2-4 (12-25 sec)
[Calendar advances: Week 2, Week 3, Week 4]

VOICEOVER:
"Week two: [AI Name] notices you always ask for three options.

Week three: [AI Name] learns your clients are mostly in B2B SaaS.

Week four: [AI Name] starts anticipating what you need before you ask."

[Visual: Each week, the AI "profile" grows more detailed]
[Info cards accumulating: "Prefers 3 options" / "B2B SaaS focus" / "Morning meetings"]

---

THE SHIFT (25-40 sec)
[Screen: PureBrain interface with proactive suggestion]

VOICEOVER:
"Then something changes.

Instead of waiting for your question, [AI Name] reaches out:
'I noticed your client mentioned a rebrand. Want me to draft talking points for that conversation?'"

[Visual: Proactive notification from AI]

---

CLOSE (40-55 sec)
[Frame: Simple comparison graphic]

VOICEOVER:
"Generic AI gives you what you ask for.

[AI Name] gives you what you need.

The longer you work together, the better it gets."

[Text: "The AI that learns YOU."]
[Subtext: "Not just your prompts. Your patterns."]
```

### Key Message
"The longer you work together, the better it gets"

### Visual Requirements
- Calendar/timeline progression visual
- AI "knowledge profile" growing over time
- Key moment: AI being PROACTIVE, not reactive
- Warm, partnership-focused imagery (not cold tech)

## Production Notes

### Tone for All Videos
- Conversational, not corporate
- Focus on relief (solving pain points) not features
- Use "[AI Name]" placeholder - to be personalized if tech allows, or use "your AI"

### Music Recommendations
- Warm, minimal electronic
- Builds subtly, not dramatic
- Think: productivity app vibes, not sci-fi

### Length Priority
If videos must be shorter:
- Video 1 can trim to 30 sec (cut Problem section)
- Video 2 can trim to 40 sec (compress magic section)
- Video 3 should stay 50+ sec (progression is the point)

### Call-to-Action After Videos
Each video should end with subtle CTA driving to:
- Continue onboarding (if in sequence)
- "See pricing" (if on demo page)
- "Start your journey" (if on landing page)
