## Executive Summary

Mentimeter is a Swedish interactive presentation and audience engagement platform founded in 2014, generating approximately $38–75M ARR with 500M+ registered users and 411 employees. It dominates the "passive-to-active audience" niche used primarily in corporate meetings and higher education, trusted by 95% of Fortune 500 companies. Its core moat is radical simplicity: no app download required, real-time anonymous polling, instant visualization. However, it is a **presentation engagement tool, not an AI coaching or intelligence platform** — which is where PureBrain occupies fundamentally different ground. Direct competitive overlap is low; the key insight is Mentimeter solves "how do I make my presentation interactive?" while PureBrain solves "how does AI know me and grow with me?"

## 1. What Is Mentimeter?

**Category**: Interactive Presentation / Audience Engagement Platform
**Tagline**: "Listen, learn, and think together"
**Core Value Prop**: Transform passive presentations into two-way real-time conversations using polls, quizzes, word clouds, Q&A, and surveys — accessible from any device via a code or QR, no app required.

**Founded**: 2014
**Headquarters**: Stockholm, Sweden
**Founders**:
- Johnny Warström (CEO & Co-Founder) — Electrical Engineering background, former Telenor Connexion IoT expansion lead
- Niklas Ingvar (Co-Founder)
- Henrik Fräsén (Co-Founder)
- Kristoffer Renholm (Co-Founder)

**Origin Story**: Warström grew frustrated with unproductive corporate meetings and wanted tools to measure real-time engagement. Launched with $500K seed from Swedish investors. Profitable within 2 years of launch. Named Sweden's fastest-growing startup by The Next Web in 2018.

## 3. Pricing

### Plan Structure (2025)

| Plan | Price | Key Limits |
|------|-------|------------|
| **Free** | $0/month | 50 participants/month total; 30-day lockout after cap; unlimited presentations |
| **Basic** | ~$13/month (billed annually) | Unlimited participants/presentation; all question types; Excel exports |
| **Pro** | ~$27/month/presenter (billed annually) | Custom branding; team templates; advanced collaboration; moderation tools |
| **Education Basic** | ~$10/month (annual) | Same as Basic for academic users |
| **Education Pro** | ~$16/month (annual) | Same as Pro for academic users |
| **Enterprise** | Custom (10+ licenses minimum) | SSO, SCIM, user activity insights, dedicated CSM; estimated $6.50–$13.08/user/month |

### Pricing Gotchas
- **Per-presenter pricing**: Each presenter costs separately — teams scale expensive quickly
- **Annual commitment only**: No monthly billing option
- **Hidden VAT**: Advertised prices exclude VAT (adds 15–25% depending on location)
- **Restrictive refunds**: 14-day window only; no refunds after live event usage
- **Participant cap is monthly**: The 50-participant free limit resets monthly — not per-session

## 12. PureBrain vs. Mentimeter — Competitive Reality

### Are They Direct Competitors?

**No. Different categories entirely.**

| Dimension | Mentimeter | PureBrain |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Core purpose** | Make presentations interactive | AI that knows and grows with you |
| **Relationship model** | Session-by-session, anonymous | Persistent, personalized, remembered |
| **AI role** | Slide generation + response clustering | Core intelligence, coaching, growth engine |
| **Memory** | None (resets each session) | Persistent memory is the product |
| **User relationship** | Audience member (passive→active) | Partner/student (ongoing relationship) |
| **Primary value** | Engagement in the moment | Intelligence over time |
| **Data model** | Aggregate responses | Individual longitudinal profiles |
| **Who uses it** | Presenters + their audiences | Individual learners, professionals |

### Where Overlap Could Occur

**Potential overlap zone**: Corporate L&D and training contexts where Mentimeter is used for knowledge checks and PureBrain could be positioned as the ongoing coaching/intelligence layer.

**The pitch**: Mentimeter captures the moment. PureBrain captures the journey. They could theoretically be complementary — Mentimeter for live engagement, PureBrain for sustained development.

### PureBrain Advantages vs. Mentimeter's Model

1. **Memory is the moat**: PureBrain's persistent AI memory has no equivalent in Mentimeter
2. **Personalization**: Mentimeter is inherently anonymous and aggregate; PureBrain is the opposite
3. **Ongoing relationship**: Mentimeter value ends when the presentation ends; PureBrain compounds over time
4. **AI depth**: Mentimeter's AI is surface-level (slide templates + response sorting); PureBrain's AI is the core product
5. **Switching cost**: Once someone's AI knows them deeply, switching is emotionally and practically costly — Mentimeter has near-zero switching cost

### PureBrain Disadvantages vs. Mentimeter

1. **Brand recognition**: Mentimeter has 500M users and Fortune 500 penetration; PureBrain is building
2. **Simplicity for casual use**: Mentimeter requires no onboarding; PureBrain requires investment
3. **IT/procurement trust**: Mentimeter is a known quantity; PureBrain must earn enterprise trust
4. **Network effects in events**: Mentimeter works because audiences already know it; PureBrain's value is individual

### Strategic Insight

Mentimeter is **not the primary threat** to PureBrain. They are solving different problems. The risk is being perceived as "another engagement tool" — PureBrain must clearly communicate that it is a **persistent intelligence partner**, not a presentation add-on. The positioning language matters enormously here.

If PureBrain ever enters the live session/training space, the comparison will intensify — but the memory moat and personalization depth should be decisive differentiators.

---

## 13. Key Takeaways for PureBrain Strategy

1. **Mentimeter owns "engagement in the moment"** — don't compete there directly
2. **Position PureBrain as the "after the presentation" intelligence layer** — what happens between sessions
3. **The memory moat is real and defensible** — Mentimeter has zero persistent memory by design (anonymous is their feature)
4. **Enterprise L&D is an adjacent market** — organizations using Mentimeter for training still need the ongoing coaching layer PureBrain provides
5. **Pricing comparison is favorable**: PureBrain's per-tier pricing vs. Mentimeter's per-presenter annual billing — flexibility is a differentiator
6. **Mentimeter's user complaints about feature degradation** = opportunity to capture churning power users who want more than polling
