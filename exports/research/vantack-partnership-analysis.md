## Section 1: What Is Vantack?

### Company Overview

Vantack is a stablecoin payment infrastructure platform. Their core pitch: "A Smarter Way to Accept Payments — Now with Vantack Wallets + Built-in On/Offramps."

They solve the problem most crypto payment tools have: they are not user-friendly. Vantack is built for high-volume merchants who want crypto's advantages (speed, low fees, global reach, no chargebacks) without the complexity.

**Website**: https://www.vantack.com/
**Booking**: https://link.xltdigital.com/widget/bookings/vantack-discovery
**Support**: support@vantack.com

### Traction Metrics
- 50,000+ successful payments processed
- 100+ businesses using the platform
- 4.99/5.0 average rating
- 40+ years combined team experience
- 178+ countries covered, 40+ real-time payment markets

### Core Features
- Instant wallet-to-wallet transfers (minutes to settlement)
- Dedicated merchant wallets — USDC and USDT on EVM-compatible networks; Solana planned
- Automatic fiat conversion via MoonPay and Transak
- Recurring subscription billing in stablecoins
- Non-custodial architecture — merchants can only charge pre-approved amounts
- Compatible with Stripe, PayPal, Square (additive, not replacement)
- Whitelabel-ready for PSPs and large operators
- 99.9% uptime SLA, 24/7 support

### Security Certifications
SOC 2 Type 1 & 2 (2024), WebAuthn, DASP License, ISO 27001/27017/27018. In progress: ISO 22301/22380, SOC 1.

### Target Markets
- High-volume payment businesses
- Philanthropy organizations
- iGaming platforms
- Global cross-border payment users
- African markets (priority expansion)
- Property managers, casino operators, e-commerce businesses

### Leadership
- **Max Rosen** (Founder/CEO): NFL athlete payments background, private equity distribution. Traction built via PR, podcasts, NASCAR sponsorship. Distribution-focused operator.
- **Abaz Ibekwe** (VP Global BD, Africa): Fintech strategist, founder of Techfield Africa. Bridge to the fastest-growing payment market in the world.

## Section 2: Vantack vs PayPal — The Real Comparison

### Fee Analysis at PureBrain Price Points

**PayPal standard checkout rate: 3.49% + $0.49 per transaction**

| Tier | Price | PayPal Fee | Vantack Est. (1%) | Savings |
|------|-------|-----------|-------------------|---------|
| Insiders | $74.50 | ~$3.09 | ~$0.75 | ~$2.34 |
| Awakened | $197 | ~$7.38 | ~$1.97 | ~$5.41 |
| Partnered | $579 | ~$20.70 | ~$5.79 | ~$14.91 |
| Unified | $1,089 | ~$38.55 | ~$10.89 | ~$27.66 |

At partner negotiated rate (0.5%), savings approximately double. At 500 customers per year across tiers, estimated annual savings: $8,000–$18,000+ depending on volume mix.

### Feature Comparison

| Dimension | PayPal | Vantack | Edge |
|-----------|--------|---------|------|
| Transaction Fees | 3.49% + $0.49 | ~0.5–1.5% (TBD) | Vantack |
| International Fees | +1.5% + FX | None — borderless stablecoin | Vantack |
| Chargeback Risk | Full exposure | Zero — crypto is final | Vantack |
| Settlement Time | 1–3 business days | Minutes | Vantack |
| Account Freeze Risk | High | Non-custodial — minimal | Vantack |
| Brand Recognition | Universal | Low (crypto space) | PayPal |
| Buyer Friction | Low — most have PayPal | High — requires crypto wallet | PayPal |
| Subscription Billing | Complex | Native, built-in | Vantack |
| Partner Relationship | None — pure vendor | Active partner, co-growth | Vantack |
| WhiteLabel | None | Available | Vantack |

### Recommendation

Do not fully replace PayPal immediately. Run parallel payment options — offer both at checkout. Target: crypto-native buyers first (Partnered + Unified tiers). Migrate fully when Vantack handles 30%+ of volume organically and customer wallet ownership data supports it.

## Section 3: How PureBrain Uses Vantack

### Use Case 1: Parallel Checkout Option
Add "Pay with USDC/USDT via Vantack" alongside PayPal on /insiders/, /awakened/, /partnered/, /unified/ pages. Crypto buyers get lower fees; we get lower processing costs and no chargeback risk on those transactions.

### Use Case 2: AiCIV Revenue Settlement (Priority)
Per Section 5.4 of the AiCIV agreement: automated daily 60/40 (later 50/50) revenue splits settled in USDC via Vantack wallet-to-wallet transfers. This is lower friction than public checkout — activate this first.

### Use Case 3: International Expansion
Africa, Southeast Asia, and other markets where PayPal is expensive or unavailable. Vantack's 178-country reach and dedicated Africa team open new customer geographies for PureBrain immediately.

### Priority Activation Sequence

1. **Immediate** — Book call with Max Rosen, confirm fee schedule, negotiate partner rate
2. **Week 1–2** — Activate AiCIV settlement via Vantack (lower-friction first use case)
3. **Month 1** — Add Vantack checkout option to /partnered/ and /unified/ pages
4. **Month 2–3** — Extend to /awakened/ and /insiders/
5. **Month 4–6** — Evaluate full migration based on conversion and volume data
