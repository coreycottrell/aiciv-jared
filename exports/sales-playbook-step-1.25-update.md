**Step 1.25b — The "Expensive Software" Play**

Before the call, ask the prospect ahead of time:

> "What's one piece of software your team pays for monthly or yearly that you feel is too expensive for what it delivers?"

Then do the research:
- **What does the tool actually do?** (core features they use vs. features they're paying for but don't use)
- **What would it cost to rebuild it?** (custom AI agent build vs. ongoing SaaS subscription)
- **Annual savings calculation** — show the math: current cost per year vs. one-time build + maintenance
- **Timeline to ROI** — how quickly would the custom build pay for itself?
- **Migration path** — how to move their data and workflows from the existing tool to the custom solution

Bring this research TO the call as part of the Partnership Brief. This gives you a concrete, dollars-and-cents win to present — not theoretical value, but money they're already spending that you can help them stop spending.

**Why this works:** It turns the conversation from "here's what AI could do" into "here's $47,000/year you're currently wasting." The prospect gave you the target themselves — you're just showing them the math.
