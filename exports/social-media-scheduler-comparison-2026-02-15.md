# Social Media Scheduler Comparison for Multi-Brand Management

**Agent**: web-researcher
**Domain**: Social Media Tools Research
**Date**: 2026-02-15

---

## Executive Summary

This research compares social media scheduling tools for managing 3 brands (PureBrain.ai, PureMarketing.ai, Jared Sanborn personal brand) across 5+ channels with 3-4 team members. **Bluesky support is the critical differentiator** - only 6 of 8 tools researched support it. Based on comprehensive analysis, **Metricool** emerges as the top recommendation for this specific use case, with **SocialPilot** as a strong alternative.

## Final Recommendations

### Primary Recommendation: Metricool ($38-54/month)

**Why Metricool wins:**
1. **Best price-to-value ratio** - $38-54/month vs $100+ competitors
2. **Unlimited team members** - No per-user costs
3. **Full Bluesky support** - Threads, analytics, scheduling
4. **All required channels** - Plus TikTok and YouTube
5. **Strong analytics** - Track performance across all platforms
6. **Room to grow** - Easy to add more brands later

**Recommended Plan:** Advanced ($54/month)
- 15 brands (plenty of room for expansion)
- 5,000 posts/month
- Zapier integration
- Custom reports
- Approval workflows

### Secondary Recommendation: SocialPilot ($100/month)

**Why SocialPilot as backup:**
1. More generous account allowances (25 accounts)
2. Better approval workflows
3. White-label options
4. Client management features (if needed for agency work)
5. US-based company

**Recommended Plan:** Premium ($100/month)
- 25 accounts
- 6 users
- Content library
- Full analytics

### Budget Option: Publer ($53-90/month)

If budget is tight and you can accept some feature limitations.

### Avoid for This Use Case:

- **Later** - No Bluesky support (critical requirement)
- **Hootsuite** - 4-10x more expensive than needed
- **Sprout Social** - Enterprise pricing inappropriate for 3 brands
