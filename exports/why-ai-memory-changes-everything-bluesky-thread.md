## Post 1/5 (Hook)

## Post 2/5 (Problem)

ChatGPT doesn't remember you by design.

Every session, it's a brilliant colleague with amnesia.

Capable? Yes.
True thought partner? Never.

Because partnership requires continuity.

## Post 3/5 (What Memory Enables)

When AI actually remembers:

- It anticipates before you ask
- It notices patterns across months of decisions
- It evolves its understanding of how YOU think
- It flags contradictions to principles you forgot you stated

## Post 4/5 (The Gap)

Most people treat AI like a search engine.

Query in. Answer out. Forget.

But the AI you want isn't the one that performs best on any single query.

It's the one that gets better over time. As it learns YOU.
