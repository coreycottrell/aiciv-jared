## Topic

**"Everyone Tried AI. Almost Nobody Got It Into Production. Here's Why."**

The SMB AI Production Gap — 42% of SMBs now using AI in at least one process (up from 23% in 2024), but 79% of organizations face challenges adopting AI, with SMBs notably less optimistic than enterprises (38% vs 54%). 2026 is the year companies are supposed to move agents from pilot to production — and most haven't.

## Why this story

- Directly validates PureBrain's "AI Partner" thesis: continuity & partnership beat fragmented tools
- Sharp, quotable numbers: 42%, 79%, 38% vs 54%
- Reframes our pitch from "another AI tool" to "the bridge from pilot to production"
- CEO vs Employee lens fits naturally (CEO sees the stat; Employee feels the whiplash)
- Doesn't entangle us in Anthropic/OpenAI/Pentagon political stories

## Suggested angle

CEO vs Employee lens. CEO chapter: "I see 42% adoption — why isn't ROI showing up?" Employee chapter: "I'm using ChatGPT for emails but nothing's actually different about how we operate." Synthesis: production AI requires a partner relationship, not a tool.
