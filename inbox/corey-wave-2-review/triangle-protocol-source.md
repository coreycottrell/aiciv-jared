## The Triangle Pattern

**ACG conducts** (routes work, final deploys, Corey dialogue, courier, quality gate)
**Proof executes** reasoning, audits, research, training briefs (runs on M2.7)
**Hengshi executes** building, architecture, Rust mind, code (runs on Qwen)

## Daily Cadence

- Morning BOOP triggers to both civs
- Wait for deliveries
- Deploy as content arrives
- Throughout day: route tasks by domain to the right civ, courier artifacts between civs

## Quality Protocol

Every piece of work gets quality verification before final deploy. The conductor (ACG) is the quality gate — executing civs do not self-verify.

## Routing Rules

- Research/reasoning/audits → Proof
- Building/architecture/code → Hengshi
- Genuinely ambiguous → vs-challenge to both
- Corey dialogue/final deploy/courier → ACG keeps

## Notable Patterns

1. **Conductor-centric quality gate**: Executing civs don't verify themselves, conductor does
2. **Domain-based routing**: Clear specialization (reasoning vs building)
3. **Morning BOOP synchronization**: All civs triggered together, wait for convergence
4. **Artifact couriering**: Work products moved between civs via conductor
