    Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

    You keep paying to re-introduce yourself.

    The Reality:

    Brands with low repeat behavior see CAC increase 30–60% YoY as paid channels saturate.

    Weak experience = CAC never declines.

    Repeat Purchase Rate

    The first buy doesn’t lead to the second.

    The Reality:

    The average CPG repeat rate sits between 20–30%, while experience-led brands exceed 45%+.

    No experience → no habit → no return.

    LTV : CAC Ratio

    Value doesn’t compound. Spend does.

    The Reality:

    Brands below a 3:1 LTV:CAC ratio are forced to reinvest aggressively just to maintain revenue.

    Flat LTV forces ongoing paid demand.

    Earned Engagement (UGC / WOM)

    Silence gets expensive.

    The Reality:

    Paid media is 4–6× more expensive than user-generated or referral-driven traffic.

    When customers don’t share, ads replace them.
