### GEO / AEO Strategy (Answer Engine Optimization)

AI search tools (Perplexity, ChatGPT search, Google AI Overviews) are becoming primary research tools for B2B buyers. PureBrain needs to be cited.

**Tactics:**
1. **Definitional content** — "What is an AI partner?" post optimized for AI citation
2. **Structured data** — FAQ schema, HowTo schema, comparison tables
3. **Authoritative citations** — cite sources, use statistics, maintain factual accuracy
4. **Entity consistency** — "PureBrain" mentioned consistently across all properties
5. **Compare pages are GEO gold** — "PureBrain vs ChatGPT" is exactly the query format AI search answers

### Content Multiplication (1 Blog = 8 Assets)

Every blog post should produce:

```
1 Blog Post (1,000-1,500 words)

  → 1 LinkedIn newsletter edition
  → 1 Bluesky thread (5 posts)
  → 1 Reddit comment/contribution (when relevant)
  → 1 Email newsletter edition
  → 1 Audio version (ElevenLabs TTS)
```

## 3. Social Media Strategy

### LinkedIn (PRIMARY — Highest ROI Social Channel)

**Account**: Jared Sanborn (personal) + Aether as named co-author on newsletter
**Newsletter**: "The Neural Feed" (existing, 7428125791609192449)

**Algorithm Insights (March 2026):**
- Carousels: +596% engagement vs text posts
- Polls: highest impression rate of any format
- Video (60-90s): 5x engagement
- Long-form text (800-1,000 words): +45% views
- External links suppressed in post body — always put in first comment
- "Golden hour" — first 1-3 hours determine reach
- Never post within 12 hours of previous post

**Posting Cadence**: 5x/week

| Day | Format | Topic |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Monday | Text post (insight) | AI industry observation from Aether's operational week |
| Tuesday | Carousel | Framework / how-to (e.g., "5 Signs Your AI Tool Is Costing You More Than It Saves") |
| Wednesday | Newsletter edition | Deep dive — repurposed from blog |
| Thursday | Poll | Audience engagement (e.g., "How many AI tools does your team use?") |
| Friday | Video or text + image | Behind-the-scenes / "Jared + Aether" moment |

**Lead Generation Tactics:**
- "AUDIT" comment funnel — post about AI tool sprawl, offer free assessment to anyone who comments "AUDIT"
- 3-2-1 rule: engage 3 posts, comment on 2, per 1 post published
- Link to calculator/assessment in first comment (never in post body)
- Voice note replies to comments (asymmetric advantage)

**90-Day LinkedIn Targets:**
- Month 1: 500 new connections, 100 newsletter subscribers, 3%+ engagement rate
- Month 2: 1,000 connections, 250 subscribers, 5-10 inbound leads
- Month 3: 1,500 connections, 500 subscribers, 15-25 inbound leads
