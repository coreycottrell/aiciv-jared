## Part 2: What Made These Posts Successful

### Format Patterns

| Format | Engagement Rate | Notes |
|--------|----------------|-------|
| Carousels | 278% higher than video | Best for frameworks, lists |
| Multi-image posts | 6.6% engagement rate | Highest performer |
| Document posts | 6.1% | Strong for guides |
| Short video | 5.6% | Growing but not dominant |
| Text-only | Lower but can go viral | Needs strong hook |

### Hook Patterns

1. **The Transformation Story**: "I used to believe X. Then Y happened. Now I know Z." - Most common viral format
2. **The Behind-the-Scenes Reveal**: "Here's what nobody tells you about X" - Insider knowledge appeal
3. **The Contrarian Take**: Challenge something everyone believes - Comments are 15x more valuable than likes
4. **The Data Bomb**: Start with a shocking statistic - Forces the scroll to stop
5. **The POV Format**: "POV: [relatable professional situation]" - Creates instant identification

### 2026 Algorithm Insights

- **Golden Hour Rule**: Replying to comments within 60 minutes = 2.4x higher reach
- **Engagement Velocity**: Quality interactions in first 60 minutes determine viral potential
- **3-5 Hashtags Maximum**: Posts with 3+ hashtags now get 29% LESS reach
- **Authenticity Update**: LinkedIn penalizes engagement-baiting language
- **AI Detection**: Algorithm downgrades "perfect grammar with zero personality"

### What NOT To Do

- Explicit engagement asks ("Like if you agree!") - now penalized
- More than 5 hashtags
- AI-generated slop with no personal voice
- Company page posts (personal profiles get 10x the reach)
- Vague claims without proof
