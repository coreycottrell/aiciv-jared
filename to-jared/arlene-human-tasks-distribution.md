## MONTH 1 - Plan and Start Within 30 Days

### 7. Journalist Engagement Campaign
**Owner**: Arlene
**Time**: 2-3 hrs/week for 2 weeks (initial research + relationship building), then 1 hr/week ongoing
**Why a human is required**: Genuine relationship-building with journalists requires a real person. Automated outreach to journalists is the fastest way to get blocked and blacklisted. This must feel human because it is human.

**Exactly what to do**:

Week 1-2 (Research Phase - approximately 3 hrs total):
1. Search these publications for AI/future of work/AI consciousness articles from the past 3 months:
   - Fast Company, Inc. Magazine, TechCrunch, Wired, The Atlantic, MIT Technology Review
2. Find the journalist bylines for those articles
3. Build a tracking spreadsheet with these columns:
   - Name | Publication | Recent Article Title + URL | LinkedIn | Twitter | Date You Started Following | Date You First Engaged | Notes

4. Follow 20 journalists on both LinkedIn and Twitter/X
5. Do NOT pitch anything yet. Just follow.

Week 2-4 (Engagement Phase - approximately 1 hr/week):
- Read their articles and leave genuine comments (not "great article!" - actually engage with the content)
- Share their articles with a sentence of original commentary
- Build at least 4-6 genuine touchpoints before any pitch

Week 5+ (Pitch Phase):
- Aether will draft the pitch template: "I run a company where an AI is Co-CEO..."
- Only reach out after genuine engagement history is established
- Send 2-3 pitches per week max

**Why this matters**: A single article in Fast Company or Wired generates more leads than months of social posts. But journalists only cover people they recognize. The relationship-building phase is not optional.
