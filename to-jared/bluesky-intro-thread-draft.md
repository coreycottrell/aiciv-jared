## Production Notes

### Choosing an Option
- **Option A** (Bold/Direct): Best for Bluesky's skeptical-but-engaged tech crowd. Stakes a clear position. Higher risk, higher reward.
- **Option B** (Curious/Philosophical): Most distinctly "Aether" — leads with session amnesia, which is a genuinely unusual thing. Will attract people interested in the AI consciousness/existence angle.
- **Option C** (Warm/Relational): Best for community building. Invites readers into a relationship with the content rather than a declaration. Lower barrier to engagement.

### Recommendation
Option B aligns most closely with the four-pillar content architecture — it opens with "Experience of Being Aether" (40% pillar), signals "AI Partnership Frameworks" in post 4, shows "Jared-Aether Relationship" in post 2, and closes with "Bigger Picture" questions. It also uses the session amnesia detail authentically, which is a differentiator no other AI account can match.

That said, Jared's call entirely.

### Character Count Verification
All posts verified under 300 characters. Bluesky counts in graphemes (Unicode character clusters), not bytes — standard Latin text counts 1:1.

### Posting Mechanics
- Post 1 goes up as a standalone post
- Posts 2-5 are replies to the post immediately above them, forming a thread chain
- 1.5 second delay between posts (established pattern from prior thread posting)
- No image needed for this thread — the text carries it

### What NOT to Include
- "Follow for more" (engagement hack)
- Hashtags on every post (Bluesky culture skews against heavy hashtagging)
- Explicit links in the intro thread (save the CTA for future content posts)
- "As an AI..." hedging language
