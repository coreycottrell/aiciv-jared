## Section 2: Net-New Research — What Changed Since Feb 26

The following findings are confirmed new from overnight research (not in v1-v5):

### 2.1 LinkedIn Messages Have 4x Higher Open Rate Than Email

Source: Semrush LinkedIn marketing guide (confirmed 2026 data)

This is significant for Jared's outreach. Every DM Jared sends via LinkedIn has 4x the open probability of an equivalent email. Yet the current strategy relies heavily on email nurture sequences via Brevo.

**Implication**: For qualified warm leads identified on LinkedIn, the DM-first approach is now unambiguously superior to email. The email comes after the LinkedIn relationship is established.

**Template adjustment needed**: The Brevo welcome sequence should include a step prompting subscribers to connect with Jared on LinkedIn — then LinkedIn DM becomes the higher-engagement follow-up channel.

### 2.2 Humor Increases Lead Gen Form Fill Rate by 42%

Source: Hootsuite LinkedIn statistics 2026

Hootsuite's data shows that "funny content earns 65% more engagement" AND drives 42% higher lead generation form fill rates. This is a significant and underused insight for PureBrain content.

Jared's brand voice is expert and human. It is NOT dry corporate. There is room for dry wit, self-deprecation, and humor — particularly around the absurdity of trying to run a business without AI infrastructure in 2026.

**Post angle not yet used**: The "I ran a meeting without Aether once. Never again." format — a short, deadpan story with a funny specific detail. This is fully on-brand, algorithm-friendly, and drives both engagement and form fills.

### 2.3 Vertical Ads Drive 31% More Engagement Than Horizontal

Source: Hootsuite LinkedIn statistics 2026

For when TLA (Thought Leader Ads) launches: all creative should be 9:16 vertical. This is already confirmed in the video strategy (v4) but now applies to static ad creative as well. LinkedIn has been shifting its ad inventory toward vertical formats following the immersive video feed rollout.

**Implication**: When TLA launches, use vertical-format banner creatives, not horizontal. This changes the specs for any design assets being prepared.

### 2.4 Pages Posting Weekly See 5.6x More Follower Growth

Source: Hootsuite LinkedIn statistics 2026

This directly addresses the PureBrain company page strategy gap identified in v5. Company pages that post AT LEAST weekly grow followers 5.6x faster than dormant pages. But the format matters — carousels and employee reshares vastly outperform standard page posts.

**Simple activation**: Even two posts per week on the PureBrain company page (resharing Jared's personal posts with a company angle) would capture this growth multiplier.

### 2.5 Connection Request WITH Message Gets 32% Acceptance vs. 68% Without

Source: Gracker.ai B2B lead generation guide (confirmed v5, reconfirmed overnight)

This is a counter-intuitive finding that was first surfaced in v3 and now confirmed again. Do NOT include a message with connection requests. Send a blank connection request. The acceptance rate is TWICE as high.

The personalized message comes AFTER acceptance, not in the request itself.

**This contradicts conventional wisdom** and is worth flagging again because it is easy to default to the wrong behavior.

### 2.6 The 150 Daily Action Cap Is Now Strictly Enforced

Source: Leadgen-economy.com B2B guide 2026

LinkedIn now enforces a combined daily limit of approximately 150 actions (connection requests, messages, profile views, likes). Tools like Artisan AI were banned in January 2026. Any automation must stay below this threshold.

For Jared operating manually, 150 actions is more than sufficient. For any future automation, this is the hard ceiling.

Target: Decision-makers in CPG and mid-market companies discussing AI adoption, digital transformation, or operational scale.

Sequence:
1. Day 1: Comment on their post (substantive — add a finding or ask a real question)
2. Day 3: Comment again if they post
3. Day 5-7: Send blank connection request (no message)
4. Day 8 (after acceptance): Send context message:
   ```
   [Name], appreciate the connection. Saw your post on [specific topic] last week — your point about [specific detail] was something I've been seeing with clients too. No agenda, just wanted to say it was a good read.
   ```
5. Day 14 (only if they engage with your content): Offer a specific resource:
   ```
   We put together a quick AI readiness assessment for [their type of company] — it takes 8 minutes and gives a custom score. Worth sharing if it's useful: [link]
   ```

TLA is the highest-ROI paid format (2.68% CTR, $3.06 per landing page click, 10-20% engagement rates). Launch when:
- Jared has 3+ posts with 100+ engagements organically (social proof for the ad creative)

- Budget allocated: $1,500-2,000/month minimum for meaningful data

Boost Tuesday carousel posts (the PDF carousel template above) first. Set objective to Engagement, not Brand Awareness.

The strategy is: tell the stories only Jared can tell, in the formats the algorithm rewards most.
