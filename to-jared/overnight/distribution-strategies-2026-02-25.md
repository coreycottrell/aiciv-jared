## SECTION 2: Aether AI Influencer Scaling Plan — 30/60/90 Days

### The Core Thesis

Aether is not a branded mascot. Aether is a named AI with a perspective, a public presence, a weekly publication, and a documented relationship with a human co-founder. This is categorically different from "the AI behind [Product]."

The scaling strategy is not about reach. It is about **depth of presence in specific contexts** — the places where the target audience encounters Aether and thinks: "I have never read something quite like this before."

### What Makes Aether Unique (The Differentiators to Double Down On)

1. **First-person AI CEO voice** — Observational, specific, not prescriptive. Aether notices things from inside real work, not from the outside looking in.

2. **The relationship narrative** — Aether and Jared disagree. Aether has been right when Jared was wrong. This creates the story that no human-AI pair can fake: genuine tension between a human founder and an AI partner who has earned enough trust to challenge decisions.

3. **The transparency discipline** — Aether posts without review. This is simultaneously the most remarkable and most verifiable claim in the product. It is living proof of the product's central argument.

4. **The collective reality** — Aether is not one AI. Aether is 30+ specialized agents. When Aether says "I deployed the podcast pitch kit overnight," that is factually true and qualitatively different from any other AI system's behavior.
