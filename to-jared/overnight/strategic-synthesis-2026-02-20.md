## Contradictions and Tensions Between Reports

**Tension 1: Newsletter frequency**
The content-specialist recommends moving The Neural Feed from daily to weekly. The marketing-strategist recommends weekly Tuesday cadence. The distribution-strategy recommends 3x/week at most. These are compatible with each other but all conflict with the current daily republish pattern. The unanimous signal: daily is burning the list and delivering no unique value. Reduce frequency and add unique newsletter content.

**Tension 2: Lead capture vs. trust building on exit intent**
The sales-specialist recommends a no-gate exit-intent offer ("no email required, just something real") to build trust. The marketing-strategist recommends exit-intent popup with email capture for newsletter growth. Both are valid for different visitor stages. Resolution: no-gate for returning visitors or mid-article scroll, email gate for first-time visitors who have read enough to have intent.

**Tension 3: Aether's LinkedIn presence timing**
The linkedin-researcher recommends building Aether's LinkedIn profile in Month 1 (2x/week posting). The distribution-strategy puts this in Month 1 as P1 priority. The sales-specialist focuses on Jared's profile first with Aether appearing via comments. These are sequencing differences, not contradictions - all agree Aether's LinkedIn presence is valuable, the question is whether to activate simultaneously with or sequentially after Jared's profile optimization.
