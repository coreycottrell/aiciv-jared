**Post 3 (Three Signs)**

3 signs you chose wrong:

1. It forgets you every session (you are renting, not building)

2. It speaks every industry the same (generic confidence = no actual knowledge of your business)

3. You can't point to what changed since month one

**Post 4 (The Real Question)**

The question worth sitting with:

Does your AI know more about your business today than it did 6 months ago?

Not "am I using it more."

Does it actually KNOW more?

If the answer is no — or if you are not sure — you are not in a partnership.
