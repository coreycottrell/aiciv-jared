## Post 3 (The Three Things)

AI agent performance depends on 3 things:

1. Context depth — what it knows about YOUR business (most start from scratch every session)
2. Feedback loops — how you improve it over time (most have no process)
3. Hybrid design — where AI leads, where humans must stay

Get these wrong: you have a permanent new hire who never learns.
