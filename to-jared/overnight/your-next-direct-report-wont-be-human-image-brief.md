## Core Visual Concept

**The Scene**: An executive at a clean, modern desk reviewing what looks like a team org chart or performance dashboard. Half the "photos" in the org chart boxes are human faces. Half are clean geometric orb shapes (the PureBrain visual language). The executive is not alarmed - they look focused, in control, like someone who has figured something out.

**The Feeling**: This is not a dystopian image. It's the opposite - it's calm, authoritative, forward-looking. The person in the image is the one who got ahead of the curve.

**Visual metaphor**: The org chart with humans AND AI agents side by side. Equal footing. Different roles.

## Composition Guidelines

**Safe zone**: Keep all text and critical visual elements within the inner 75% of the frame (375px from each edge on a 1600x900 canvas). Mobile cropping happens at edges - anything too close to edges may be cut in social previews.

**Visual hierarchy**:
1. Org chart with human + AI agent mix (draws the eye, tells the story instantly)
2. Headline text
3. PureBrain branding (logo, URL)

**Lighting**: Cool blue ambient lighting with subtle orange accent (from the AI orb elements). Creates the "intelligent, slightly futuristic" feel without being sci-fi.

**Depth**: The executive in the foreground, the org chart/dashboard in the mid-ground. Clean layering, not cluttered.

## PureBrain Hexagon Logo Spec

The hexagon icon is the geometric core of PureBrain's visual language. It should appear:
- As a standalone geometric element (not the wordmark - that's separate)
- Either glowing softly in Pure Tech Blue (#2a93c1) or rendered in glass/crystalline style
- Can appear in the org chart "orb boxes" as stand-ins for AI agent profile photos
- Also appears next to the wordmark in the corner branding

## Visual Directions (Two Options)

### Option A: The Org Chart
An executive's hand traces over a holographic or screen-displayed org chart. Four boxes are human photos. Four boxes are glowing PureBrain hexagon orbs. Both sets of boxes have names, titles, performance stats underneath. The visual message: this is a real team that includes AI.

Overlay: Dark background with blue/orange UI elements. Text overlaid on the upper portion of the image.

### Option B: The Two Desks
Split composition. Left side: a cluttered desk with a laptop showing ChatGPT, subscription receipts, disconnected tools. Right side: a clean desk with a holographic display showing an AI agent dashboard - context depth, feedback loops, performance trends, the PureBrain orb interface.

The person at the right desk is leaning back, arms crossed, slightly smiling. They've solved this.

Overlay: Same dark background and color palette. Text in the center or upper-left.

---

## What to Avoid

- No robotic imagery (no humanoid robots, mechanical arms, etc.)
- No red color anywhere (PureBrain palette is blue, orange, and white on dark only)
- No generic stock photo "business handshake" feel
- No text too close to edges (mobile safe zone rule)
- No light backgrounds (dark background is mandatory for brand consistency)
- No cluttered compositions - clean, purposeful, premium
- No AI-looking fonts that appear "techy" in a 2010 way (no glitch fonts, no matrix aesthetics)
