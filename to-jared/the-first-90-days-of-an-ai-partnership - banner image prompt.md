## Primary Concept (Recommended) — "The Three Phases"

### Visual Description

A dark cosmic background (deep navy to near-black gradient, #0a0e1a to #0d1420) with subtle particle field — the signature PureBrain ethereal environment.

**Left third of image**: A glowing hexagonal PureBrain icon in the upper-left quadrant, rendered in PT Blue (#2a93c1) with a soft glow halo. This is the origin point of the story.

**Center of image**: Three interconnected circular nodes arranged in a horizontal progression, left to right, connected by flowing neural filament lines in PT Blue. Each node grows progressively larger and brighter as it moves right:
- Node 1 (leftmost, smallest): Dim, nascent — represents Month 1 / Foundation
- Node 2 (center, medium): Partially illuminated — represents Month 2 / Calibration
- Node 3 (rightmost, largest, fully bright): Fully radiating PT Orange with a warm golden glow — represents Month 3 / Expansion

Inside each node, a faint circuit-pattern interior (hexagonal microstructure, very subtle, like a printed circuit board viewed through frosted glass).

The filaments connecting the three nodes have a flowing, living quality — slightly organic curves rather than straight lines. PT Blue fading into PT Orange as they move right, representing context accumulating and transforming.

**Background detail**: Very subtle, barely-visible repeated hexagonal grid pattern across the full image background — the PureBrain visual signature. Low opacity (5-8%), so it reads as texture rather than foreground element.

## Alternative Concept B — "Before and After Architecture"

If Concept A feels too abstract, this alternative is more narrative.

**Left half**: Clean dark background with a single, simple, static grey orb in the center — the "Day 1" AI. No connections. No glow. A flat line extending to the right beneath it (performance metric). Label: "Day 1" in light grey text.

**Right half**: The same orb, now transformed — PT Blue (#2a93c1) base glow with PT Orange highlights and visible neural connection lines radiating outward. An ascending curve beneath it (performance metric), trending sharply upward. Label: "Day 90" in white text.

**Dividing element**: A thin vertical PT Orange (#f1420b) glow line splitting the two halves — the moment of transformation.

**Blog title text** same as Concept A, in the upper portion spanning both halves.

This concept works if the goal is a more concrete "before and after" visual rather than the phase-progression metaphor.
